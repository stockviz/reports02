Bluspring Enterprises Limited (NSE: BLUSPRING, BSE: 544414) - Analysis

Company Overview: Bluspring is an integrated infrastructure services provider (post-demerger from Quess Corp in FY25), focusing on facility & food services (60% revenue), telecom & industrials (19%), security (19%), and investments in foundit (job platform, ~2%). Serves 1,000+ clients across sectors like industrials, govt/infra, education, BFSI. FY25 revenue ~₹3,484 Cr (consol., incl. foundit); listed June 2025. Q2/H1 FY26 shows resilient growth amid demerger transition.

Headwinds (Challenges)
  • Margin Pressure: EBITDA flat/declining YoY due to investments in sales/leadership (strategic but dilutive); one-off ECL reversals in prior year. Blended margins ~3-4% (low vs. peers in IFM/security).
  • Working Capital Strain: DSO elevated at 105 days from demerger novations/delays; H1 OCF negative ₹97 Cr; receivables up 25% > revenue growth. Net debt up to ₹174 Cr (consol.).
  • foundit Drag: Revenue ₹21 Cr (Q2), EBITDA loss ₹12 Cr; H1 losses persist despite cost cuts (₹44 Cr to ₹33 Cr/quarter). Turnaround focused on product/sales.
  • Cyclicality: Telecom muted QoQ (capex pause); industrials/food vulnerable to economic slowdowns.
  • Post-Demerger Transition: Novation delays, one-time costs (₹12.7 Cr exceptional in Q1).
Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)
  • Revenue Momentum: Broad-based double-digit growth across all core segments; diversified (top-30 clients 50%, 8 sectors >5%). H1 new sales ₹250 Cr ACV.
  • Margin Trajectory: QoQ EBITDA/PAT expansion (41/43 bps); security margins up to 3% (from 2.4%); industrials at 8.3%.
  • Operational Scale: Headcount +5% YoY; digitalization (90k+ attendance digitized, SAP rollout); 95%+ retention; PSARA in 24 states.
  • Macro Support: Infra capex (Govt ₹5.8L Cr H1, +31% YoY); office absorption +35% YoY; urbanization/GCCs/private capex revival; employment incentives.
  • Strategic Wins: 36 new contracts H1 (₹110 Cr ACV); entry into sports/leisure (Para Athletics); central kitchen expansion.
Growth Prospects
  • Core Business: 3x GDP (~15-18% CAGR) to FY30 via organic (hyperscale food/industrials) + inorganic (EBITDA/ROE accretive). Target: 6% EBITDA, 20% ROE, 15% BV CAGR, 50% EBITDA-OCF conversion.
