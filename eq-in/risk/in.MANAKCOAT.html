Analysis of Manaksia Coated Metals & Industries Ltd. (MANAKCOAT)

Company Overview: Manaksia Coated Metals & Industries Ltd. (MCMIL) is a manufacturer of value-added coated steel products (pre-painted galvanized steel - PPGI ~92% of sales; galvanized steel - GI). Key facility in Kutch, Gujarat. FY26 H1 highlights: Revenue ₹478 Cr (+27% YoY), EBITDA ₹58 Cr (+103%, 12% margin), PAT ₹28 Cr (+423%). Exports: 85% of Q2 sales (+151% YoY). Debt reduced 27% to ₹103 Cr; D/E 1.19x. Recent fundraising: ₹175 Cr via warrants.

Tailwinds (Positive Factors)
  • Robust Financial Momentum: Exceptional Q2/H1 FY26 growth driven by volume (production +8-18% YoY), realizations (+7-12% YoY), and export surge. EBITDA margins expanded 534 bps to 13%; ROCE improving. Order book ₹600 Cr (12-month execution, 85% exports).
  • Export-Led Growth: 85% sales from exports to 43 countries (Europe dominant). Back-to-back model (80%+) mitigates commodity volatility.
  • Industry Tailwinds: India steel demand to 300MT by 2030 (PLI ₹6,322 Cr for specialty steel, PM GatiShakti). Coated steel benefits from infra, auto, appliances.
  • Deleveraging & Ratings: Debt down; interest coverage 3.62x (vs 1.89x FY25); Acuité rating upgraded to A1/A2+.
  • Sustainability Push: 7 MWp solar plant (Q1 FY27) to cut 50-55% grid power, save 35-45% costs, offset 9K tons CO₂/year.
Headwinds (Challenges)
  • Commodity Volatility: Steel prices fluctuate; spot exposure (20-25%) passed fortnightly, but back-to-back model limits impact.
  • Margin Pressure Risks: Raw material costs ~67% of revenue; shift from GI (low margin) to PPGI/Alu-Zinc ongoing but incomplete.
  • Domestic Slowdown: Exports dominant (85%); domestic ~15% amid competition.
  • High Working Capital: Inventories/trade receivables up (current ratio 1.67x improving but inventories ₹336 Cr).
Growth Prospects
  • Capacity Expansion (Phased): | Phase | Project | Capacity Impact | Timeline | |——-|———|—————–|———-| | 1 (FY26) | Alu-Zinc conversion | GI: 132K → 180K MT (+36%; 3x corrosion resistance, premium pricing) | Q3 FY26 | | 2 (FY27) | 2nd Color Coating Line + Solar | PPGI: 86K → 236K MT (+174%) | Early FY27 | | 3 (FY28) | 2nd Alu-Zinc + CRM (backward integration) | Alu-Zinc: 180K → 360K MT; CRM: 300K MT | FY28 |
  • Fundraising: ₹350 Cr planned (QIP/private placement) for expansions/debt reduction.
  • Product Shift: Alu-Zinc (55% Al, 43.5% Zn) for niche (infra, roofing); EBITDA +40% potential. Value-added PPGI at 92%.
  • Outlook: Topline potential ~₹1,500-2,000 Cr post-FY28 at 75-80% utilization; margins 12-15% via scale/efficiency.

Summary: MCMIL rides strong tailwinds from export boom, capacity ramps, and steel policy support, positioning for 2-3x growth by FY28 (coated capacity ~600K MT). Q2/H1 FY26 validates execution amid deleveraging. Headwinds like volatility are managed via model shifts. Buy/Hold for long-term; monitor capex delivery and fundraising. Target upside: 20-30% in 12-18 months at current valuations (~₹162/share, MCAP ₹1,714 Cr). Risks moderate but execution-critical.
