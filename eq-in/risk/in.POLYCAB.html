Polycab India Limited (POLYCAB) Analysis

Based on Q2/H1 FY26 Financial Results (ended Sep 30, 2025), Board Disclosures, Press Release, and Related Announcements

Polycab delivered record H1 FY26 performance with 21% YoY revenue growth to ₹1,24 Bn, 55% EBITDA growth to ₹18.8 Bn (15.2% margin), and 53% PAT growth to ₹12.9 Bn (10.4% margin). Q2 saw 18% revenue growth to ₹64.8 Bn, 62% EBITDA growth (15.8% margin), and 56% PAT growth. Wires & Cables (W&C) drove ~87% of revenue with 21-26% YoY growth; FMEG grew 14-16%; EPC declined 19% YoY but EBIT +11-42%. Balance sheet remains strong (net cash ₹29.4 Bn vs. ₹24.3 Bn YoY), with high capex (₹7.5 Bn in H1) signaling expansion.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust W&C Demand: 21% Q2/26% H1 YoY growth; domestic (distribution/institutional) +21%, exports +25% (6.5% of revenue). Infra spending, private capex, and commodity inflation aided volumes/pricing.
  • FMEG Momentum: 14% Q2/16% H1 growth; solar category “poised to be largest” due to govt. rooftop schemes; real estate demand; now profitable despite ad spends.
  • Margin Expansion: EBITDA margins +130 bps QoQ/+~300 bps YoY in Q2 via operating leverage, favorable mix (W&C EBIT 15.1%, +270 bps YoY). Volume-led profitability.
  • Strong Financials: Net cash ₹29.4 Bn; ROCE/ROE implied high (PAT growth outpaces revenue); low debt (borrowings <1% of assets).
  • Strategic Execution: Capex (CWIP ₹8.6 Bn) for capacity; “Project Spring” for growth; 27 plants, 4,300+ dealers, exports to 84 countries.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • FMEG Category Volatility: Fans hit by prolonged monsoon/elevated inventory; other categories grew but overall slower than W&C.
  • EPC Degrowth: 19% YoY revenue drop (₹4 Bn in Q2) due to project timing; though EBIT up 42% YoY from one-offs/efficiencies.
  • Input Cost Pressure: Commodity inflation (copper/aluminum) in late Q2; inventories up 48% YoY (₹54 Bn), signaling stocking for growth but tying capital.
  • Leadership Transition: CFO Gandharv Tongia resigned (Oct 27, 2025); interim Niyant Maru (9-month term) appointed—continuity ensured but potential disruption.
  • Macro/Seasonal: Channel inventory normalization in FMEG; inter-segment eliminations up YoY.

Growth Prospects (Outlook)

  • High Visibility in Core: W&C to sustain 20%+ growth (infra/realty/private capex); exports scaling (5.9-6.5% contribution).
  • FMEG Scale-Up: 16%+ H1 growth; solar/real estate tailwinds; policy support positions it as key driver (target: industry-leading growth).
  • EPC Recovery: EBIT focus despite revenue dip; pipeline from infra push.
  • Capex-Led Expansion: H1 capex ₹7.5 Bn (PPE +15%); new facilities for wires/FMEG; amalgamation with subsidiary (Uniglobus) for integration.
  • Overall Guidance (Implied): FY26 revenue >₹250 Bn (from FY25 ₹224 Bn); EBITDA margins 15%+ sustainable; net cash to fund dividends/capex. Investor meets (Dec 2025) signal confidence.

Summary: Polycab’s strong tailwinds from W&C demand, margins, and balance sheet outweigh headwinds (EPC dip, FMEG weather). Growth prospects excellent (20%+ revenue, 15% EBITDA margins FY26) via capacity/FMEG/export push, but monitor commodities/execution. Stock attractive for growth investors; risks moderate (beta ~1.0 implied). Valuation context: FY25 P/E ~50x; forward ~35-40x on H1 momentum.
