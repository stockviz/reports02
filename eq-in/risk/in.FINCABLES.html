Finolex Cables Limited (FINCABLES) Analysis

FY26 H1 Overview (Ended Sep 2025): Revenue +9% YoY to ₹2,771 Cr (Q2: +5% YoY to ₹1,376 Cr). EBITDA +19% YoY (Q2: +26% YoY, margins expanded). PAT +21% YoY to ₹326 Cr (Q2: +35% YoY). Strong profitability despite modest top-line growth; net working capital at 33 days (ahead of peers). Utilization ~70% in electrical cables. Capex ₹100 Cr (preform plant ready, fiber draw by Mar’26).

Headwinds (Challenges Impacting Performance)

  • Segment-Specific Weakness: Electrical wires flat (agri down due to prolonged monsoon ~10-12% of revenue; building wires muted from real estate slowdown/new launches). Communication cables volumes depressed (no BharatNet contribution; low fiber prices; protracted contract negotiations). FMEG (fans, lighting, switches) missed targets (monsoon hit fans; LED price erosion).
  • Muted Growth vs. Peers: Top-line +5-9% YoY lags double-digit peer growth; small base in high-growth cables (~₹200-250 Cr vs. ₹25,000 Cr market).
  • External Pressures: Commodity volatility (copper dip then surge; 3-3.5% price hike in Sep). Rising ad spends (₹20 Cr in Q2 vs. ₹11 Cr prior) for brand defense.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Margin Resilience: EBITDA/PAT surged despite revenue modesty (electrical cables EBIT ~11%; pricing actions offset volatility). Other income strong (₹110 Cr in Q2).
  • Operational Strength: Balance sheet robust (zero debt ex-lease; inventory 65 days planned). Efficient working capital (receivables 15 days).
  • Brand & Diversification: Premium retail positioning; growth in industrial/solar/auto cables (+double digits); power cables +60% volume in Q2.
  • FMEG Momentum: Conduits doing well; lighting prices bottomed out.

Growth Prospects

  • Capacity Expansion: Preform trials soon (margin accretive via import substitution, saving 5% duty); fiber draw completion by Mar’26 → comms margins to 8-10% (from low single digits).
  • Demand Tailwinds: Infra/infra cables pickup; state BharatNet tenders (7 states in 2-4 months); private telecom (Bharti yearly contract; 60% capacity booked). Electrical peak capacity >₹6,500 Cr (post-expansions).
  • Product Pipeline: New FMEG models (fans/lighting/switches Q3+); cable variants (FR-LSH, HFFR, E-beam); target FMEG ₹500 Cr by FY28. Utilization ramp-up from 70%.
  • Medium-Term: Housing/infra recovery (1-2Q lag); auto geared for growth.

Key Risks

  • Competition: New entrants (UltraTech Q3 start; other business houses) in wires/cables; project discounting eroding margins.
  • Execution/Volume Risks: Lumpy govt orders (BharatNet Phase 3 retenders Sep); real estate moderation persists (affordable housing slowdown, GST clarity awaited).
  • Macro/Commodity: Copper volatility; monsoon/infra delays.
  • FMEG Underperformance: Ongoing misses; in-house mfg deferred (need volumes first).
  • Regulatory/Legal: Minor GST demand (₹1.87L, appealable); shareholder dispute sub-judice (no comment).
  • No Guidance: Management avoids forward-looking statements.

Overall Outlook: Resilient profitability with defensive brand/moat; growth hinges on capex fruition, infra tenders, and real estate recovery. Modest FY26 top-line expected; margins supportive. Strong buyback/dividend history (recent ₹8/share approved). Monitor competition and comms ramp-up.
