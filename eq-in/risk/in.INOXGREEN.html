Analysis of INOXGREEN (Inox Green Energy Services Limited)

Overview: Inox Green is India’s leading listed pure-play renewable O&M services provider (subsidiary of Inox Wind, part of INOXGFL Group). Q2/H1 FY26 results show record performance with portfolio at 12.5 GW (including 6.5 GW investments consolidating FY27). Key catalysts include demerger approval, new 5 GW MoUs, and group synergies. Below is a structured analysis based on provided documents (earnings call, results, presentation, filings, press releases).

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Robust Financial Momentum: Q2 FY26: Revenue +101% YoY (₹129.5 Cr), EBITDA +52% (₹52.2 Cr), PAT +363% (₹28.1 Cr). H1 EBITDA margins ~44%. Cash PAT +121% YoY. Machine availability at 96.3%.
  • Portfolio Expansion: 12.5 GW under management (10 GW wind, 2.5 GW solar). Investments in 6.5 GW wind assets to boost FY27 profitability “manifold”. Target: 17 GW in 2 years; already India’s largest RE O&M.
  • Demerger Progress: Substation business demerger (₹1,000 Cr assets) approved unanimously by shareholders/warrant holders/creditors (Nov 2025). Eliminates ₹50-55 Cr annual depreciation, asset-lights BS, higher ROE/ROCE. IRSL (EPC arm) to list post-merger.
  • Group Synergies: Natural O&M from Inox Wind (3.2 GW order book, 1.2 GW FY26 execution), Inox Solar, Inox Clean IPP (multi-GW annual additions). New MoUs: 5 GW O&M (2.5 GW wind with KP Energy, 2.5 GW solar with KPI Green).
  • Favorable Macros: Wind sector revival (GST 12%→5%, ALMM, hybridization policies). India wind target ~125 GW by 2032 (₹6 Tn capex opportunity).
  • ESG/Tech Edge: 24x7 monitoring, predictive maintenance, value-add services (refurbishment, carbon credits). ESG compliant (EY assured).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Group Dependency: ~70% organic growth from Inox Wind/Inox Clean; any delays in group executions (e.g., monsoon-impacted H1 at Inox Wind) could spill over.
  • Revenue Recognition Issues: Straight-line O&M policy leads to ₹1,198 Cr unbilled (services rendered); disputes/arbitrations ongoing (management expects no material impact).
  • Seasonal/Execution Delays: Monsoons historically impact (noted in Inox Wind call); maintaining 96%+ availability critical.
  • Pending Approvals: Demerger awaits NCLT sanction; SPV investments (₹557 Cr + BGs) tied to regulatory appeals (Inox Wind to bear losses if unrecovered).

Growth Prospects

  • High Visibility: 12.5 GW base + 5 GW MoUs + Inox Wind’s 3.2 GW book + group IPP (1 GW+ annual recurring). Inorganic via portfolio takeovers.
  • Profitability Leap: FY27 “manifold” growth post-6.5 GW consolidation + demerger. EBITDA margins sustainable at 40%+ (asset-light model).
  • Market Leadership: On track to be #1 RE O&M (wind/solar/hybrid). Solar/hybrid tailwinds from Inox Solar/KP tie-ups. Repowering (25 GW potential), offshore wind VGF.
  • Capex Light: FY26 capex ~₹200 Cr; cash generative (H1 cash PAT ₹94.7 Cr).
  • Upside from Listings: IRSL listing unlocks EPC value for Inox Wind (parent holds 56% in Inox Green).

Projected Trajectory: 3-5x portfolio growth in 2-3 years; EBITDA could double+ in FY27. Strong ROE/ROCE post-demerger.

Key Risks

Overall Outlook: Strong Buy/Bullish. Tailwinds dominate with demerger, 5 GW MoUs, and FY27 inflection. Risks manageable via group support. Target portfolio leadership; monitor NCLT/demerger. Valuation upside from profitability boost (current P/E ~50x FY26 PAT est.).
