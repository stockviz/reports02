SJVN Limited: Analysis of Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

SJVN Limited (NSE: SJVN), a Navratna CPSE focused on hydro (core), thermal, and solar power generation, reported Q2/H1 FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) amid strong project execution but offset by operational and regulatory pressures. Installed capacity: 3,146 MW (operational), with 5,091 MW under construction. Total generation H1 FY26: 9,442 MU (+6.3% YoY). Standalone H1 revenue: ₹1,764 Cr (flat YoY), PAT: ₹631 Cr (-21% YoY). Consolidated H1 revenue: ₹1,950 Cr, PAT: ₹535 Cr (-33% YoY). Debt: ₹29,539 Cr (consolidated D/E: 2.03x).

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Capacity Additions Accelerating: Buxar Thermal Unit-1 (660 MW) COD imminent (Nov 2025; full plant COD by Feb 2026; est. ₹982 Cr annual revenue at ₹5.4/unit). Bikaner Solar (1 GW): 680 MW COD achieved; +320 MW by Dec 2025 (total op. capacity ~3,500 MW). Additional solar: Jamui (75 MW), Assam (120 MW) this FY.
  • RE Pipeline Expansion: REIA status; tenders floated for 19.8 GW (LOA: 13.74 GW; PSA/PPA: 3.74 GW). Upcoming: BESS (1.25 GW Haryana), wind (1.2 GW), pumped storage tenders. PM Surya Ghar (rooftop solar) in 3 states + govt. bldgs. (21 MW awarded).
  • Strong Backing & Milestones: PPAs signed for Arun-III (900 MW Nepal), Luhri/Sunni hydro with UP. PM inaugurated Buxar Unit-1 & Khavda Solar (200 MW; ₹2.88/unit; COD Dec 2026). Capex: H1 ₹3,600 Cr (full FY26: ₹7,500 Cr; FY27: ₹8,000 Cr).
  • Generation & Efficiency: Hydro output up (Nathpa Jhakri: +0.6% QoQ). Incentive income: ₹104 Cr (H1).
  • Policy Support: Aligned with 500 GW RE by 2030; Navratna status aids funding.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Revenue/PAT Pressure: Q2 standalone revenue flat at ₹942 Cr (-5% YoY) despite new solar COD; PAT ₹372 Cr (-21% YoY). Reasons: Floods/silt reduced hydro PAF (₹50-60 Cr loss); 80IA tax benefit expiry (₹52 Cr); +finance cost/depreciation (Q2: ₹269 Cr / ₹115 Cr, up sharply); lower LPS/bank interest.
  • High Costs: Consolidated finance cost H1: ₹531 Cr (+50% YoY); depreciation ₹331 Cr (+26% YoY). Debt up to ₹29.5 Bn; DSCR low at 0.75x (H1).
  • Project Delays: RE PPA/PSA delays (9 GW LOA pending due to grid connectivity/Supreme Court issues). Hydro: Etalin (3.1 GW)/Attunli (0.7 GW) land/env. clearances nearing but FY27 start; Arun-III electromechanical just started.
  • Disputes: Himachal hydro (Luhri 210 MW, Sunni 382 MW, Dhaulasidh 66 MW; ₹3,465 Cr capex): GoHP renegotiation/threat to reclaim (HP HC stay on coercive action).

Growth Prospects

  • Near-Term (FY26-27): +1.5 GW solar commissioning FY27; Buxar full ops to boost revenue ~₹1,000 Cr annually. Total FY26 capex to meet MoU targets.
  • Medium-Term (FY27-30): Nepal hydro (Lower Arun 680 MW, Upper Karnali 900 MW) tenders Q2 FY27. 5 GW Arunachal hydro advancing (Etalin land ~70% compensated). REIA to drive 10+ GW PSA soon.
  • Long-Term: Portfolio diversification (hydro 60%, RE ramp-up); target sustainable growth post-consolidation. Cumulative RE tenders/LOAs position for 500 GW national goal.
  • Valuation Upside: Trading at ~FY26E P/E 8-10x (low vs. peers); ROE improvement post-Buxar/RE ramp (current ~10%).

Overall Outlook: Positive with cautious optimism. Tailwinds from commissioning/RE scale outweigh headwinds; growth to 5-6 GW operational by FY27 could drive 15-20% revenue CAGR. Monitor debt, hydro disputes, and RE PPAs. Stock suitable for long-term infra/RE play.
