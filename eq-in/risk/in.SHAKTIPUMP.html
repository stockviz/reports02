Analysis of Shakti Pumps (India) Ltd (SHAKTIPUMP) – Q2 & H1 FY26 Overview

Shakti Pumps, a leader in solar pumps with integrated manufacturing (pumps, motors, VFDs, structures), reported record Q2 revenue of ₹6,664 Mn (+5% YoY) despite headwinds, driven by 21% YoY solar pump installations (22,304 units). H1 revenue grew 7.2% YoY to ₹12,889 Mn, but margins dipped due to costs. Order book stands at ₹13,000 Mn (as of Nov 7, 2025), with capex of ₹17,000 Mn underway. Recent QIP raised ₹293 Cr. Below is a structured summary of headwinds, tailwinds, growth prospects, and key risks based on the filings.

Headwinds (Challenges Impacting Near-Term Performance)

  • Weather & Execution Delays: Prolonged monsoon in key states (e.g., Maharashtra, MP) slowed installations (could have been higher) and RMS verification (7/90-day pump running proof for billing), inflating receivables to ₹16,390 Mn standalone (target: 120 days by FY26-end; 44% not yet due).
  • Margin Pressure: EBITDA margin fell to 20.4% (Q2) / 21.7% (H1) from 23.4%/23.7% YoY due to 3-4% rise in raw materials (copper, steel, solar panels). Finance costs up (₹231 Mn H1).
  • Policy & Order Delays: GST 2.0 scope changes in some states; anticipated large orders from MP/Rajasthan pending despite bidding.
  • High Working Capital: Inventories up, trade receivables ballooned (H1 cash flow from ops: -₹478 Cr), straining liquidity despite QIP proceeds.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • Strong Execution & Order Visibility: Record Q2 revenue; solar installations +21% YoY (Q2)/+19% (H1). Maharashtra leading inflows/execution; order book ₹13,000 Mn (incl. ₹4,640 Mn Maharashtra scheme).
  • Diversification Gains: Exports robust (₹1,029 Mn Q2, ₹2,000 Mn H1; projects in Haiti, Uganda, etc.). Cash sales +67% YoY (₹428 Mn H1 via 100+ outlets). Solar rooftop expanded to Rajasthan/UP/Maharashtra.
  • Fundraising & Capacity: QIP (Jul 2025) raised ₹293 Cr for capex. H1FY26 utilization ~70%; doubling pumps/motors to 10L by Jun 2026, 2.2 GW DCR solar plant by Mar 2027 (₹12,000 Cr capex invested ₹250 Mn in sub).
  • Recognition: ESG rating 75 (“Good”); awards for strategy/employer; 15 patents.

Growth Prospects (Medium-to-Long Term Opportunities)

  • Solar Pumps Leadership: ~25% share in PM-KUSUM (612 MW+ installed); domestic market ~₹5,500 Bn (replacement + new demand for 20-22 Mn pumps). Maharashtra’s 3.5 Mn pump scheme + PM Surya Ghar (rooftop: 41 GW by 2030, 19% CAGR).
  • Diversification: Exports to 100+ countries (24.8% CAGR FY21-25); EV (motors/controllers, patent granted); rooftop (DCR compliance edge); cash/retail scaling.
  • Capex Ramp-Up: FY26 guidance intact; post-expansion: 10L pumps, 4L VFDs, 2L structures/EV units. Pipeline robust (active bids in key states).
  • Guidance: Confident of FY26 growth via orders, diversification, margin recovery (raw mat stabilization).
