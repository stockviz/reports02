Analysis of Navin Fluorine International Limited (NAVINFLUOR)

Navin Fluorine International Limited (NFIL), a leading Indian fluorochemicals player, reported stellar Q2 & H1 FY26 results with 42% YoY revenue growth to Rs. 1,484 Cr, 118% YoY EBITDA growth to Rs. 453 Cr (30.5% margin), and PAT of Rs. 266 Cr (+141% YoY). Growth was broad-based across HPP (41%), Specialty (37%), and CDMO (57%). The company announced Rs. 311.5 Cr capex (internal accruals-funded), declared 325% interim dividend (Rs. 6.50/share), and raised ~Rs. 750 Cr via QIP. Balance sheet remains robust (net debt/equity ~0.4x post-QIP), with positive operating cash flows (Rs. 281 Cr H1). ESG rating: 61/100 (Strong).
