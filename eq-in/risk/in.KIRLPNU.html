Analysis of Kirloskar Pneumatic Company Ltd (KIRLPNU)

Company Overview: KPCL is a key player in air/gas compression systems (~90% revenue from Compression segment), refrigeration, vapour absorption chillers, gearboxes, and castings. Serves Oil & Gas, Petrochem, CNG, power, steel, pharma, and emerging areas like hydrogen/CBG. H1 FY26 (Apr-Sep 2025) showed revenue decline amid execution slowdowns, but order book grew.

Headwinds

  • Revenue & Profit Pressure: H1 FY26 revenue from operations down 8% YoY to ₹650 Cr (vs ₹706 Cr); Total Income ₹665 Cr (vs ₹717 Cr). EBITDA margin compressed to 16.4% (₹109 Cr) from ~20%; PAT ₹71 Cr (10.7%) vs ₹94 Cr. Q2 FY26 revenue fell 12% YoY to ₹378 Cr due to project delays/clearances.
  • Execution Slowdown: Order finalization and project execution impacted by sector headwinds in Oil & Gas/Petrochem (core markets).
  • Regulatory Demand: Customs order for ₹1.19 Cr differential duty + equal penalty + interest (received Nov 13, 2025); appeal planned, but potential cash outflow if lost (company sees no material impact).
  • Margin Squeeze: Employee costs up (₹97 Cr H1), other expenses elevated amid lower volumes.

Tailwinds

  • Robust Order Book: Grew 3% to ₹1,667 Cr (Oct 1, 2025) from ₹1,624 Cr (Apr 1), signaling backlog visibility (~2.5x H1 revenue).
  • Product Momentum: Centrifugal compressor (Tezcatlipoca) hit 115 installations; first hydrogen compressor order received; new G800 variant launching soon. Tyche semi-hermetic compressor commercialized.
  • Recognition & Efficiency: CII National Award for Energy Efficient Unit; sustained EBITDA at 16%+ despite revenue dip.
  • Low Leverage: Near-zero debt (finance costs negligible); strong cash flows (H1 ops cash ₹143 Cr standalone).
  • Governance: Independent Director re-appointment approved (83.7% votes); active analyst engagement.

Growth Prospects

  • Core Expansion: Compression dominance (91% H1 revenue) with tailwinds in CNG/CBG, upstream/midstream/downstream Oil & Gas. Hydrogen blending pilots (2-20%) open new avenues.
  • Diversification: PLI application for Commercial AC (Zephyros C system; ₹5,000 Cr market); vapour absorption chillers, gearboxes, castings scaling.
  • IP Commercialization: 100+ centrifugal installs; hydrogen breakthrough positions for green energy transition.
  • Capacity & Network: 3 plants (Pune/Nashik); PAN-India dealers (150+), Dubai presence. Order book supports 20-25% revenue growth if execution normalizes.
  • FY26 Outlook: H1 dip transitional; Q3+ benefits from order execution, new products. EPS H1 at ₹10.98 (diluted); FY25 full-year PAT ₹2,110 Cr standalone base.

Overall Summary: Short-term headwinds from execution delays pressured H1 results (revenue -8% YoY), but strong order book (+3%), product milestones (hydrogen/centrifugal), and diversification (AC/PLI) signal recovery in H2 FY26. Growth prospects in energy transition/CNG outweigh risks if Oil & Gas capex rebounds. Investment Thesis: Positive on backlog-driven rebound; monitor Q3 execution.
