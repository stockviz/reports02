GRASIM Industries Limited (NSE: GRASIM) - Q2FY26 Analysis

Grasim, a flagship Aditya Birla Group company, reported robust Q2FY26 results with consolidated revenue at ₹39,900 Cr (+17% YoY) and EBITDA at ₹5,217 Cr (+29% YoY), driven by Building Materials (Cement, Paints, B2B) and Chemicals. TTM revenue hit ₹1,59,663 Cr (+8% YoY). Standalone revenue reached a record ₹9,610 Cr (+26% YoY). Diversified portfolio (Fibres 10%, Chemicals 6%, Building Materials 56%, Financial Services 27%, Others 1%) positions it as a proxy for India’s growth in manufacturing, infra, and financialization.
