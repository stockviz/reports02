Craftsman Automation Limited (CRAFTSMAN) - Analysis Summary (H1 FY26)

Overall Performance Snapshot (Consolidated, Unaudited):
- Revenue: ₹3,786 Cr (+60% YoY; Powertrain ₹2,034 Cr / 54%, Aluminum ₹2,275 Cr / 60%, Industrial ₹476 Cr / 13%).
- EBITDA: ₹582 Cr (15.4% margin; Aluminum led at 15%).
- PAT: ₹16.8 Cr (EPS ₹67.3 basic, not annualized).
- Net Debt/EBITDA: 0.94x (LTM); 2.46x annualized. ROCE annualized: 15%.
- Balance Sheet: Total Assets ₹8,266 Cr (+16% QoQ); Net Debt ~₹2,800 Cr (up due to CAPEX). CWIP ₹453 Cr signals aggressive expansion.
Strong H1 beat driven by Aluminum ramp-up & acquisitions (DR Axion/Sunbeam full ownership, German subs), but not fully comparable YoY due to M&A timing.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)
  • Robust Segment Momentum: Aluminum (60% of H1 revenue) margins sustainable at ~15% (vs. prior startup drag); Bhiwandi/Hosur alloy wheel plants ramping (5.8M+ capacity, orders >6M). Powertrain stable (utilization improving via exports/Europe).
  • Acquisition Synergies: Germany (Fronberg Guss) H1 revenue ₹158 Cr (PAT ₹8 Cr); data center engine blocks full order book. Sunbeam integrating (shared resources with DR Axion).
  • Strategic Expansions: Kothavadi Phase-I operational; Phase-II for $100M data center revenue (~₹800 Cr) by FY29 (50% orders firm). Chennai auto hub + OEM capex (e.g., cylinder blocks).
  • Industry/Macro Boost: China+1 shift; India OEM expansions (50% capacity growth in 3 yrs); outsourcing trend in 2W/PV; tractor/off-highway traction offsets CV slowdown.
  • Financial Discipline: EBITDA margins 15%, EBIT 10%; land sale (Gurgaon) to cut ₹350 Cr debt by mid-FY27.
Headwinds (Challenges)
  • High Leverage & CAPEX Intensity: FY26 CAPEX ~₹1,280 Cr (Craftsman ₹1,000 Cr + DR Axion ₹280 Cr); debt up (short-term borrowings +₹710 Cr QoQ). Cash burn in ops (₹126 Cr outflow) due to WC (inventories +₹284 Cr).
  • Integration/Turnaround Costs: Sunbeam Q2 EBITDA margin 6% (target 10% FY27); startup losses at new plants (Hosur just started). Exceptional items ₹8.7 Cr (relocation).
  • Cyclical Exposure: Powertrain CV share down to 46% (from 49% QoQ); festive slowdown delayed cost rationalization.
  • Margin Pressure: Unallocated expenses ₹37 Cr; forex/ raw material volatility (aluminum implied).
