HCLTech (HCL Technologies Ltd.) Q2 FY26 Analysis

Overall Summary: HCLTech delivered a strong Q2 FY26 with broad-based revenue growth (Services +2.5% QoQ / +5.5% YoY CC), exceptional bookings ($2.6B, first >$2.5B without mega deal), margin recovery (EBIT 17.5%, +116 bps QoQ), and AI milestone ($100M+ Advanced AI revenue, ~3% of total). FY26 guidance raised for Services (4-5% CC). Robust cash generation (LTM FCF/NI 125%) supports dividends (₹12/share) and investments. Employee count +3,489 to 226,640; attrition 12.6% LTM. INR revenue ₹31,942 Cr (+10.7% YoY). Stock-relevant positives: AI traction, bookings pipeline; concerns: Software weakness, wage hikes.

Headwinds (Challenges Pressuring Performance)

  • Software Segment Softness: Revenue -3.7% YoY CC due to lower perpetual licenses; focus on subscriptions (+9% YoY) but overall drag on company growth.
  • Vertical-Specific Weakness: Auto slowdown in Manufacturing (-1.8% YoY); broader macro caution delaying decisions.
  • Margin Pressures:
    • Restructuring costs (55 bps hit in Q2; FY26 total ~40+ bps).
    • Upcoming wage hikes (Q3: 70-80 bps; Q4: +40-50 bps impact; baked into 17-18% guidance).
  • External Factors: H1B visa fee revisions (low exposure: <few hundred visas/year); unpredictable macro (discretionary spend muted).
  • AI Productivity Risk: Potential 10-50% efficiency gains in IT/BPO/SDLC could deflate renewals (seen in 5/10 top renewals).

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum Drivers)

  • Bookings Strength: $2.6B TCV (+41.8% QoQ / +15.8% YoY); well-balanced across verticals/geos; correlates to revenue growth (1-2Q lag).
  • Margin Recovery: EBIT 17.5% via utilization (+50 bps from Project Ascend), forex gains (+56 bps), no Q1 one-offs (+30 bps); Software profitability up.
  • AI Monetization: $100M+ Advanced AI (Agentic, Physical AI, Engineering); AI Force in 47 accounts; embedded in most deals.
  • Services Resilience: +5.5% YoY CC; Digital 42% mix (+15% YoY); ERS +13.4% YoY; client expansions (e.g., +2 $50M clients YoY).
  • Financial Health: Net cash $3.29B; DSO 78 days (-4 QoQ); ROIC 38.6% (+290 bps YoY); high dividend payout (95.7% of NI).
  • Geos: Europe/ROW strong (+7.6%/+17.9% YoY); USA stable (+2.4%).

Growth Prospects (High-Conviction Opportunities)

  • AI Strategy Execution: 4-pillar focus (service transformation, IP like AI Force/UnO, new services e.g., AI Factory/Advisory, partnerships e.g., Nvidia/Dell/Pearson/MIT). Goal: 100 top clients on AI Force; pipeline “record high” with AI central to deals. Expect IP % to rise (currently small).
  • Services Upside: Raised FY26 to 4-5% CC; modernization demand (e.g., Ericsson, retailer deals); Retail/CPG/Lifesciences gaining.
  • Software Recurring Shift: ARR $1.06B (+0.6% YoY CC); Gartner Leader in AST/SOAP; focus on subscriptions/professional services.
  • Client Diversification: +23 high-value clients YoY ($10M+); top-10 concentration low (~20%).
  • Non-Linear Growth: Revenue/employee +1.8% YoY; AI productivity to enable “more with less” headcount.
  • Pipeline/Market: Robust, AI-embedded; hyperscaler/tech demand for AI Factory.
