Cash deployment for capex/other opportunities.

Key Risks: - Climatic/Seasonal: High monsoon dependency (70% Kharif consumption); Rabi rains could disrupt. - Regulatory/Policy: Biostimulant/Glyphosate delays; animal nutrition discontinuation (low-margin, non-material). - Execution: Capex delays (Japan approvals, Dahej 2028); export tariffs/channel pressures. - Competition/Geopolitical: Chinese pricing in generics/exports; LatAm/Africa volatility. - Macro: Input costs, forex; Barrix subsidiary drag (steady topline, profitability hit). - Subsidiary Exposure: Unreviewed financials immaterial but add consolidation risks.

Overall Summary: Despite Q2 weather headwinds, H1 resilience underscores strong fundamentals (cash-rich, margin discipline). FY26 guidance intact: maintain/improve margins, responsible growth.
