Refex Industries Limited (REFEX) Analysis

Overview: Refex Industries (NSE: REFEX, BSE: 532884) is a Chennai-based sustainability-focused company with a dominant Ash & Coal Handling business (96%+ of continuing revenue), supplemented by emerging verticals like Wind (Venwind) and Green Mobility (to be demerged). Q2 FY26 (standalone) showed sequential recovery: Revenue ₹431 Cr (+15% QoQ), EBITDA ₹74 Cr (17% margin, nearly doubled QoQ), PAT ₹52 Cr. Core ash handling drove resilience despite monsoons, with ₹1,200 Cr order book. FY25 consolidated revenue was ₹2,483 Cr. Discontinued ops (Power Trading, Refrigeration) and mobility losses are being streamlined.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Robust Core Business Momentum: Ash/Coal handling (70k MT/day capacity) benefits from India’s coal reliance (50% energy mix by 2033, 544 Mn MT fly ash/year). Largest organized player (40+ plants, 14 states, 2,000+ fleet). Regulations (MoEF 2021) mandate 100% utilization with ₹1,000/MT penalties, creating tailwinds. Q2 margins 10-12% (sustainable 8-11%), order book ₹1,200 Cr (+ recent ₹100 Cr order).
  • Margin Expansion & Efficiency: Product mix + cost discipline lifted EBITDA margins to 17%. PAN-India presence, tech (GPS/fuel sensors), NTPC/PSU paymasters (no delays).
  • Cash-Rich Balance Sheet: ₹278 Cr cash, low debt (sufficiently capitalized, no fundraising plans). ROE 15%, ROCE 15%.
  • Strategic Restructuring: Power trading exit (low-margin), Refrigeration liquidation. Mobility demerger (1:1 swap, listing in 6-7 months) unlocks value; Wind revenue ramps Q3/Q4 (₹1,225 Cr orders: 153MW + 100MW).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Seasonal Disruptions: Extended monsoons hit Q1/Q2 volumes (wet ash harder to handle, site access issues). Some states still rainy.
  • Loss-Making Verticals: Mobility (₹1.8 Cr loss H1) and Wind (setup phase) drag consolidated PAT. Power trading discontinued but minor Q2 hit.
  • Working Capital Strain: High contract assets (₹237 Cr, certifications delay billing), trade receivables ₹584 Cr (up YoY).
  • Fragmented Revenue Mix: 96% from ash/coal exposes to thermal power cycles.

Growth Prospects

  • Ash/Coal (High Conviction): Target 90k MT/day YE25, 105-110k in 3 years (+60-65%). Integrated O&M model (e.g., APGENCO tender). Pipeline: 40-50 tenders (₹10-250 Cr each), NE expansion.
  • Wind (Venwind): 5.3MW turbines (tech edge), factory operational (Silvassa), 5GW target in 5 years. Q3/Q4 revenue from ₹1,225 Cr orders; competitive pricing/margins TBD.
  • Mobility (Post-Demerger): 1,400+ EVs, B2B clients (TCS, BNP). Independent entity for fleet expansion/utilization.
  • Overall: FY26 guidance implicit (momentum in H2), diversified green portfolio. Market: Ash pond 2 Tn MT, wind imports high.
