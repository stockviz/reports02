ICICI Bank (ICICIBANK) Analysis: Q2-2026 (Sep 30, 2025)

ICICI Bank’s Q2-2026 results reflect resilient performance amid moderating growth and policy tailwinds. Core metrics improved (PBT ex-treasury +9.1% YoY, PAT +5.2% YoY), driven by deposit mobilization and asset quality gains, though NIM pressure and slower retail lending tempered upside. Standalone RoE at 16.0%, CET1 at 16.35% (incl. H1 profits). Consolidated PAT +3.2% YoY.

Tailwinds (Key Positives)

  • Deposit Franchise Strength: Avg deposits +9.1% YoY (₹15.57L Cr); CASA ratio stable at 39.2%. Period-end deposits +7.7% YoY. Granular retail focus and digital platforms (e.g., InstaBIZ) aiding share gains.
  • Asset Quality Improvement: Net NPA ↓ to 0.39% (from 0.41% qoq); Gross NPA additions ↓22% qoq. PCR at 75.0%; contingency provisions ₹13,100 Cr (0.9% of advances). Low slippages in retail (post-2022 tightening).
  • Diversified Revenue: Fee income +10.1% YoY (78% from retail/rural/SME); Non-interest excl. treasury +13.2% YoY. Subsidiaries strong (ICICI Life VNB margin 24.5%; ICICI General PAT +18% YoY).
  • Capital Buffer: CET1 16.35%, CAR 17.00% (post-H1 profits); supports growth/dividends.
  • Operational Efficiency: Branch network to 7,246 (+263 in H1); tech spend ~11% of opex.

Headwinds (Key Challenges)

  • Margin Pressure: NIM stable at 4.30% but qoq dip (4.34%); range-bound outlook due to rate cuts, deposit re-pricing, competition. Yield on advances ↓44 bps YoY; CoD ↓24 bps.
  • Muted Loan Growth: Domestic advances +10.6% YoY but retail slowdown (6.6% vs. system); corporate tepid (3.5% YoY, cash-rich corporates prefer bonds/ECBs).
  • Cost Inflation: Opex +12.4% YoY (festive spends, branch expansion); Cost/income 40.6%.
  • Treasury Volatility: Income ↓68% YoY (₹220 Cr) on rising yields.
  • Provisions Normalization: ↓50% qoq (KCC seasonality); expected ↑ in H2 to ~0.4% annualized credit cost.

Growth Prospects

  • Loan Acceleration: Sequential pickup (3.3% qoq domestic); H2 boost from GST cuts, fiscal/monetary easing. Business banking +24.8% YoY; mortgages +9.9%; cards +6.4%. Target mid-teens FY26 growth via ecosystems/micromarkets.
  • Deposit Momentum: Positive high-frequency indicators; transaction banking, 3-in-1 accounts, salaried/IT focus to sustain CASA.
  • Fee/Subsidiary Upside: Retail fees, cross-sell; ICICI Life APE stable, ICICI AMC PAT +20% YoY.
  • Capex Leverage: Strong RoA (2.33%), capital for 12-15% advances growth; ECL/RW changes neutral/positive.
  • Economy Tailwind: Resilient India growth; policy enablers (CRR cuts, acquisition finance easing).
