Analysis of Centrum Capital Limited (CENTRUM): Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Centrum Capital Limited, a diversified financial services group with exposure to banking (Unity Bank), wealth management, investment banking, broking, and alternatives (AIF via Modulus), reported H1 FY26 (ended Sep 30, 2025) consolidated results showing revenue growth but persistent losses amid high finance costs and impairments. Strategic capital raises and divestment plans aim to deleverage the balance sheet (total assets ~₹23,514 Cr, debt-heavy with deposits at ~₹11,953 Cr). Postal ballot approvals (99%+ in favor) enable key moves like selling Centrum Housing Finance (CHF). Below is a structured summary based on the provided filings.
