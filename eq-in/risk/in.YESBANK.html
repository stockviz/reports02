YES Bank (YESBANK) Analysis: Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

YES Bank’s Q2FY26 (ended Sep 30, 2025) results show steady progress post-reconstruction, with PAT up 18% YoY (INR 654 Cr), RoA at 0.6%, stable NIM (2.5%), deposit growth (7% QoQ), and improving asset quality (NNPA 0.3%, PCR 81%). Strategic tailwinds from SMBC’s 24% stake and rating upgrades to AA- bolster stability. However, legacy drags and execution challenges persist. Below is a structured summary based on the filings.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • Liability Franchise Strength: Deposits at INR 2.96 lakh Cr (+7% QoQ, +7% YoY); CASA ratio 33.7% (+170bps YoY). Retail/Branch-led deposits up 14% YoY, outperforming industry (CASA CAGR 20% vs. industry 7%).
  • Asset Quality Turnaround: GNPA stable at 1.6%; NNPA down to 0.3% (-20bps YoY); PCR up to 81% (+11% YoY). Slippages down QoQ to 2% of advances; recoveries/upgrades at INR 854 Cr.
  • Fee & Non-Interest Income Growth: Up 17% YoY (INR 1,644 Cr); core fees +12% YoY (Retail share 56%). Strong traction in cards (+26% YoY), FX, and third-party products.
  • Strategic Partnerships & Ratings: SMBC’s 24% stake (largest shareholder); SBI at 10.8%. All domestic ratings upgraded to AA-/A1+ (stable outlook), reflecting improved governance/capital.
  • Digital Leadership: #1 UPI Payee PSP (54% share); 98% cards issued digitally; apps like IRIS Biz powering 1.1 Cr transactions.
  • Capital & Liquidity: CET-1 at 13.9%; LCR 125%; RIDF deposits down to 7.8% of assets.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Profitability Pressures: RoA 0.6% (below 1% FY27 target); Retail segmental loss (INR -536 Cr in H1FY26); C/I ratio 67% (elevated).
  • NIM Squeeze: Flat QoQ at 2.5%; asset yields down (9.5%) offset by deposit cost cuts (5.7%, -40bps YoY), but repricing lags persist.
  • Provision Volatility: Up 48% QoQ (INR 419 Cr); credit costs 0.4% annualized.
  • Legacy Burdens: AT-1 bonds litigation (Supreme Court hearing Oct 29, 2025); PSL RIDF deposits drag margins (target <5% in 2 years).
  • Opex Discipline Needed: Up 1% YoY despite volumes; staff costs flat but business-linked costs up.
  • Retail Slippages: Elevated at 4% annualized (though improving QoQ); unsecured loans (PL/BL/cards) key watch area.

Growth Prospects (High Potential)

  • Balanced Loan Book Expansion: Advances INR 2.5 lakh Cr (+6% YoY); Retail disbursements +20% QoQ; Commercial +17% YoY. MSME 30% of book; PSL organic ramp-up.
  • Wholesale Acceleration: Fresh sanctions doubled QoQ; focus on granular mid-corps, govt. banking, FI (e.g., NBFCs).
  • Digital/Transaction Banking: UPI CAGR 66%; CMS throughput 3x since FY22; API stack (1,500+); BaaS/BaaP models via fintech partnerships.
  • Branch/Distribution: 43 new branches YTD (target 80); Retail CASA 40%.
  • SMBC Synergies: Cross-border expertise; nominee directors enhance governance. Target RoA 1% by FY27 via NIM expansion (to 3%+), fee growth (20%+ share).
  • ESG/Fin Inclusion: Green finance INR 7,357 Cr; rural/microfinance scaling.
