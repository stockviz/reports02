Analysis of Genus Power Infrastructures Ltd. (GENUSPOWER)

Company Overview: Genus Power is a leading Indian player in smart metering solutions, focusing on Advanced Metering Infrastructure Service Provider (AMISP) under the TOTEX model (CAPEX + OPEX). It offers end-to-end solutions (smart meters, HES, MDMS, communication) with in-house manufacturing capacity of ~20Mn meters annually. Key clients: State DISCOMs, EESL. Order book: ₹28,758 Cr (net of taxes) as of Sep 30, 2025, providing 8-10 year visibility. Strong Q2/H1 FY26 results reflect execution momentum.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust Execution & Financial Momentum: Q2FY26 standalone revenue +136% YoY (₹1,149 Cr), EBITDA +200% (₹244 Cr, 21.3% margin, +456 bps), PAT +162% (₹148 Cr). H1FY26: Revenue +132% (₹2,091 Cr), EBITDA +207% (21.2% margin). Shift to high-margin AMISP/O&M supports 18%+ EBITDA target.
  • Policy & Market Support: RDSS scheme drives India’s smart meter rollout (target: 250Mn+). Genus has ~15-20% market share in key bids. Favorable tailwinds from electrification, digitization, and non-technical loss reduction (global: $80-100Bn opportunity).
  • Strategic Partnerships & Capacity: GIC (Singapore) JV ($2Bn platform, Genus as exclusive OEM). DFC USA investment ($49.5Mn). In-house integration (meters to software) reduces costs. Manufacturing ramp-up (Jaipur, Haridwar, Guwahati) + NABL labs ensure quality.
  • Diversification: Expanding to gas/water meters (15-41% untapped demand), exports (Africa/APAC/ME), software (HES/MDMS for 3rd parties), smart grid/AI analytics.
  • Talent & Governance: 15,500+ workforce (30% women), ESOP trust formed. All AGM resolutions passed overwhelmingly (99%+ favor except minor ones).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Working Capital Intensity: Inventories +69% (₹1,438 Cr), receivables +15% (₹1,570 Cr), contract assets up sharply. H1FY26 cash used in ops: ₹158 Cr (vs. ₹443 Cr prior). Cycle strain from EPC-heavy phase.
  • Debt Burden: Borrowings up 37% to ₹1,744 Cr (current: ₹1,274 Cr). Finance costs +56% YoY (₹77 Cr H1). Interest coverage solid but rising debt could pressure margins if rates stay high.
  • Regulatory Scrutiny: ED search (Dec 2024) under PMLA at corporate office/chairman’s residence. No further notice, management confident of compliance – but overhang on sentiment.
  • Demerger Impact: Strategic Investment Division demerged (discontinued ops), simplifying focus but one-time adjustments.

Growth Prospects (High Potential)

  • Order Book Execution: ₹28,758 Cr (~10x FY25 revenue) with EPC-to-O&M transition creating annuity (stable 8-10yr cash flows). FY26 rollout target on track; H1 already strong.
  • Market Expansion: India (RDSS: 2nd largest global opportunity post-USA/EU). Exports (urbanization in EMs). Gas/water metering (15-41% adoption). Software/analytics (USD 1.5Bn global by 2025).
  • Margin Uplift: AMISP scale + backward integration targets 18-21% EBITDA sustained. 2nd-gen deployments (EU/USA replacement cycle) + AI/IoT add-ons.
  • Projections: Visible revenue CAGR 50-70% FY26-28 from book; ROE potential 25%+ on equity base ~₹1,874 Cr. Analyst meets (Nov 25) signal confidence.

Key Risks (Moderate-High)

Risk Category Description Mitigation
Execution Delays in meter rollout/installations (e.g., DISCOM coordination). Proven track record (100Mn+ meters installed); ramped capacity.
Policy/Regulatory RDSS slowdown/changes; bid disqualifications. ED probe escalation. Strong policy continuity; diversified bids/partners.
Liquidity Negative FCF from WC/debt; forex volatility. Improving O&M mix; GIC/DFC funding.

Macro Commodity inflation (metals/chips); interest rates. Hedging; 41% emission cuts signal sustainability.
Dilution/Other ESOP issuances (~1.8L shares Q2); promoter voting dilution (institutional against some resolutions). Aligned incentives; 99% promoter voting support.

Overall Summary: Strong Buy Bias (High Growth, Manageable Risks). Tailwinds from order book/policy dominate, driving explosive FY26 growth (revenue >₹4,000 Cr possible). Headwinds (WC/debt) transitional as EPC peaks. Prospects immense in India’s $20Bn+ smart meter TAM. Monitor ED probe (low probability overhang) & Q3 cash flows. Target: 20-30% upside from current levels (~₹350-400/share est., based on 1x sales or 20x FY27 EPS). Position for multi-year compounding.
