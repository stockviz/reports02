Analysis of Authum Investment & Infrastructure Ltd. (AIIL)

Company Overview: AIIL is a systemically important non-deposit taking NBFC (rating: CRISIL A/Stable, upgraded Oct’25) with a market cap of ~Rs. 47,500 Cr (Nov’25). Evolved from a pure-play equity investment platform (Authum 1.0/2.0) to a diversified credit/alternative assets platform (Authum 3.0). As of Sep’25: Total assets ~Rs. 20,076 Cr (investments 78%, loans/credit substitutes 12%); net worth Rs. 16,272 Cr; promoter holding 68.79% (diluted from 74.95%, to be reinfused as pref shares). H1FY26 consolidated PAT Rs. 1,710 Cr (EPS Rs. 100.7), driven by investment income (Rs. 821 Cr) and recoveries, but volatile due to fair value changes.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Robust Financial Track Record: Net worth CAGR ~100%+ since FY20 (Rs. 3,416 Cr to Rs. 16,272 Cr). Investment exits delivered 2x+ MOIC (FY25 profit Rs. 1,797 Cr); unrealized MOIC 3.2x on top holdings.
  • Business Diversification: Credit book scaled to Rs. 2,312 Cr (72% of total book via fresh/alt assets); H1FY26 fresh disbursements >Rs. 1,150 Cr. ARC (ISARC, 88% stake) deployed Rs. 110 Cr (AUM Rs. 150 Cr), Rs. 235 Cr cash ready.
  • Low Leverage & Liquidity: Net debt <0.1x net worth; cash Rs. 1,127 Cr. Minimal reliance on external debt.
  • Strategic Acquisitions: ISARC (Jun’25), OEDL/RHFL assets provide 25-branch network, collections infra, and stressed asset expertise.
  • Promoter Backing: Rs. 1,619 Cr ICD from Mentor Capital (to convert to pref shares); postal ballot approved Rs. 2,450 Cr NCRPS issuance to promoter.
  • Governance: Majority independent board; ESOP scheme (5L options) for talent retention.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Revenue Volatility: 80%+ revenue from investments; H1FY26 OCI loss Rs. 152 Cr due to equity fair value swings (e.g., Q2 loss Rs. 1,191 Cr).
  • Legacy Acquired Assets Drag: Subsidiaries (OEDL/RCFL) have accumulated losses (Rs. 891 Cr), SEBI penalty (Rs. 25 Cr, stayed), ED inquiries, and MCA probes under review.
  • Promoter Concentration: High holding (68%+); key funding via RPTs/promoter ICDs raises liquidity dependence.
  • Slow Credit Ramp-Up: Acquired portfolios declining (Rs. 522 Cr, 3% of book); fresh business still nascent amid integration.
  • Margin Pressure: Finance costs up (H1 Rs. 64 Cr vs. FY25 Rs. 52 Cr); credit yields targeted higher but execution pending.

Growth Prospects (High Potential, FY26+ Outlook)

  • Credit/Alt Assets Scale: Target integrated platform (NBFC + ARC + AAMC + servicing); ARC free cash Rs. 235 Cr for FY26 deployment; alt fund launch (RE/turnarounds, skin-in-game).
  • Investment Engine: Continue flow/strategic bets (e.g., Prataap Snacks 42%, NITCO 45%); H1 exits Rs. 120 Cr profit.
  • AUM Expansion: Total book Rs. 18k Cr+; fresh credit + ARC could drive 50%+ YoY growth. Servicing AUM Rs. 1,900 Cr (Q4FY25 start).
  • Capital Infusion: Rs. 2,450 Cr NCRPS (0.01% dividend, 6.5% IRR redemption) funds growth without dilution/equity pressure.
  • Projections: Consensus implies 30-40% AUM CAGR; ROE >50% sustainable via 70%+ credit mix (stable yields 12-15%).

Key Risks (Moderate-High, Monitor Closely)

  1. Regulatory/Litigation (High): Legacy from RHFL/RCFL (SEBI ban/penalty till 2029 stayed; ED/MCA probes). OEDL going concern doubts (losses > net worth).
  2. Market/Volatility (Medium): 78% assets in equities (flow/strategic); sensitive to Nifty corrections (e.g., Q2 OCI hit).
  3. Credit/Execution (Medium): NPA Rs. 199 Cr (net Rs. 16 Cr, 85-90% mortgage-backed); scaling fresh book amid collections from legacy (Rs. 8,810 Cr written-off).
  4. Liquidity/Governance (Medium): Promoter funding reliance (Rs. 2,450 Cr NCRPS approval); RPT scrutiny.
  5. Concentration (Medium): Top investments ~10%; promoter ecosystem ties.
  6. Macro (Low-Medium): NBFC sector headwinds (e.g., RBI norms) but low leverage mitigates.

Overall Outlook: Positive (Buy/Hold). Tailwinds from diversification and capital dominate; growth runway strong (3.0x net worth in 6 years). Risks legacy-tied but contained (stays/comfort letters). Target 20-30% upside in 12M on execution; watch Q3FY26 credit growth/ARC deployments. Promoter alignment via pref shares boosts confidence.
