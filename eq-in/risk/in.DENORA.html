De Nora India Limited (DENORA) Analysis

  • Robust Revenue/Profit Recovery: H1 revenue doubled YoY, driven by electrode demand (likely chlor-alkali, water treatment, green H2 electrolysis). PAT growth despite high provisions signals operational leverage.
  • Strong Liquidity & Balance Sheet: ₹948 cash (+18% YoY); ₹7.3bn liquid investments; Zero debt. Ops. cash flow covers capex/investments, enabling growth.
  • Parent Group Support: Industrie De Nora (Italy) backing; New director (Picari) brings global finance/treasury expertise for strategy/risk mgmt.
  • Cost Discipline: Employee expenses stable; Depreciation modest (₹98 H1). Other income ₹406 (investments/interest).

Headwinds (Challenges)
