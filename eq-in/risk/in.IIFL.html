IIFL Finance Limited (IIFL) Analysis: Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

IIFL Finance, a retail-focused NBFC (listed on BSE/NSE), reported robust Q2 FY26 results with AUM at ₹90,122 Cr (+35% YoY), PAT (pre-NCI) at ₹418 Cr (+52% QoQ), driven by gold loans normalization post-RBI embargo. Focus on secured lending (gold + home = 74% AUM) amid portfolio reset (discontinued micro-LAP/unsecured digital). Strong balance sheet: Liquidity ₹8,170 Cr, net gearing 3.6x, GNPA 2.1%, PCR 93%. Fitch outlook upgraded to Positive. Approved ₹2,000 Cr NCD shelf issuance.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Gold Loan Surge: Record AUM ₹34,577 Cr (+220% YoY, +27% QoQ, 16% tonnage growth); >98% retention, rising yields (18.56%), low NPAs (0.12%). Momentum expected to continue.
  • Secured Portfolio Shift: Core products (home/gold/MSME/micro) = 98% AUM (+37% YoY). Home loans steady at ₹32,034 Cr; new MD Girish Kousgi to drive 15-18% H2 growth.
  • Financial Strength: ROA 1.9%, ROE 9.8%; off-book 34% (assigned ₹18,607 Cr, co-lending ₹11,848 Cr); high CRAR (standalone 18.6%, consolidated computed 28.2%).
  • Bank Partnerships & Ratings: Strong traction in DA/co-lending; positive Fitch outlook supports funding.
  • Phygital Model: 4,800 branches + AI underwriting for MSME scaling; ESG focus enhances appeal.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Legacy Asset Quality Stress: Elevated NPAs in discontinued segments (micro-LAP 20.45%, unsecured 7.51%); MFI GNPA 5.03% due to RBI guardrails (e.g., Bihar arrears 6%, collections 99%). Credit costs skewed H1 (3.5%), full-year guidance 2.8-3%.
  • Opex Pressure: Gold competition led to higher incentives; cost-to-income 45.2%.
  • Slow Segments: Home flat QoQ (leadership transition); MSME AUM flat YoY (-3% QoQ) amid recalibration; MFI down 26% YoY.
  • Co-lending Dip: Temporary slowdown due to new RBI guidelines (effective Jan 2026).

Growth Prospects

  • Medium-Term Target: 15-20% AUM growth; H2 acceleration in home/LAP/gold; MSME secured via AI/co-lending.
  • AUM Mix Evolution: Secured retail to dominate; off-book target 40-45%; ROA guidance 2.5-2.8% FY26.
  • Fundraising: ₹2,000 Cr NCD shelf + NCD call option exercise supports expansion.
  • Market Tailwinds: RBI liquidity, govt MSME/housing push; gold price upside; PSL demand via partnerships.
  • Upside Potential: Gold momentum, rating upgrade could lower COF (down 7 bps QoQ); H1 PAT ₹692 Cr sets strong FY26 base.

Key Risks

  • Credit Costs/Asset Quality: Legacy unwind could prolong elevated provisions (8% in MFI/discontinued); Bihar-like regional stress.
  • Regulatory: Co-lending clarifications, SBR compliance for upper-layer NBFC; past embargo recurrence.
  • Funding/Competition: Rising COF (up 23% YoY), gold poaching; capital raise needed if growth >20% (standalone CRAR 18.6%, target ~20%).
  • Macro: Inflation/geopolitics impacting MFI/MSME cash flows; gold price volatility.
  • Execution: New home finance leadership integration; discontinued segments’ collections.

Overall Outlook: Strong recovery post-challenges; tailwinds from secured pivot outweigh headwinds. FY26 growth 15-20% feasible with improving quality, but monitor credit costs. Positive for stock if H2 delivers on guidance. (Ratings: Buy/Hold bias; target upside 20-30% from current levels based on 1.5-2x FY26 BVPS).
