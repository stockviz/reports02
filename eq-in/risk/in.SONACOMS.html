Key Risks (Potential Downside Factors): - Demand/Execution: Auto slowdowns (esp. EV model risks); ramp-up delays in 29 new EV programs; railway execution post-acquisition. - Concentration/Dependency: Still 72% from top-10 customers; one EV customer drop from 23% (FY22) to 6% revenue. - Geopolitical/Supply: Tariffs, rare-earth bans, chip fires (Nexperia/Novelis minor impact but second-order effects possible). - Financial: High capex (₹198 Cr H1) + acquisitions (₹1,700 Cr H1 outflows); returns dipped (ROE 12.8%, ROCE 15.8%) post-equity raise; dilution risk from PSP 2025 (1.5 Mn shares max). - Competition/Tech: New tech adoption (rare-earth-free motors heavier/less power-dense); robotics nascent (J-curve, 5-10 yr horizon). - Regulatory: PSP 2025 approval pending; SEBI compliance for ESOP/PSP.

Overall Outlook: Strong Q2 resilience amid “stacked odds” (mgmt quote); antifragile strategy (gains from disorder) evident in diversification/tech wins. H1 softness transitional; FY26 growth likely via EV/rail ramps, but monitor BEV recovery/customer risks. Long-term bullish on EV/mobility tech positioning.
