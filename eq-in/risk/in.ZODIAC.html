Analysis of Zodiac Energy Limited (ZODIAC: NSE/BSE)

Zodiac Energy Limited is a solar energy player focused on trading solar photovoltaic (PV) modules and executing EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) contracts (primary segment contributing ~97% of H1 FY26 revenue). It is expanding into electricity generation via owned solar plants (emerging segment). The company operates through subsidiaries/LLPs (e.g., Radhavallabh, Priyapratam, Dharmik Solar Projects LLPs) and an associate (Dhwarkesh Solar Projects LLP). Key data from Q2/H1 FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025), plant commissioning, investor meet, and postal ballot notice.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Explosive Revenue Momentum: H1 FY26 revenue +47% YoY; Q2 at Rs. 9,678L (+83% YoY). EPC order book likely strong amid India’s solar push (500 GW RE target by 2030).
  • Backward Integration into Power Gen: New recurring revenue stream (CODs achieved); 11+ MWp capacity added. Potential for merchant sales/PPAs; subsidies/tariffs supportive.
  • Strong Ops Cash Flow: Rs. 6.8K L generated funds working capital/capex internally.
  • Policy Support: PLI scheme for modules, rooftop solar incentives, Gujarat’s solar hub status. Investor meet (Sep ’25) signals visibility.
  • Funding Flexibility: Postal ballot seeks Rs. 500 Cr approval for loans/guarantees to subs/associates (under Sec 185) → Enables JV/subsidiary expansion without fresh equity.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Debt Overhang: Gross debt ~Rs. 16K L (53% of assets); finance costs Rs. 883L (123% of PBT) → Eroding profitability. Leverage up (Borrowings +38% YoY).
  • Loss-Making Power Gen Segment: Rs. 251L H1 loss (high dep’n Rs. 460L); ramp-up phase with utilization/merchant price risks.
  • Lumpy EPC Nature: Revenue cyclical; Q1 FY26 dip (Rs. 9,805L) vs Q2 surge. Inventory steady at Rs. 5.4K L but receivables Rs. 6.2K L (37 days DSO).
  • Subsidiary Drag: 3 subs +1 assoc. posted Rs. 17L net loss (immaterial but signals early-stage risks).
  • Margin Pressure: Net margins ~2.8% (flat YoY); high dep’n (Rs. 460L) and other expenses rising.

Growth Prospects

  • High Visibility Expansion: Heavy capex (Rs. 5.5K L H1 outflow) signals 50-100 MWp+ pipeline via subs/LLPs. Target FY26 revenue Rs. 80-100K L plausible (double FY25).
  • Dual Model Synergies: EPC feeds own plants (internal modules); power gen to contribute 10-20% revenue by FY27 (stable EBITDA).
  • Market Tailwinds: India solar capex ~Rs. 2-3L Cr annually; Zodiac’s Gujarat focus (local incentives). Postal ballot unlocks Rs. 500 Cr funding for projects.
  • Projections: 40-60% CAGR revenue next 2-3 yrs; PAT margins to 5-7% post-debt optimization/power gen breakeven. MCAP ~Rs. 1,500-2,000 Cr potential (at 15-20x FY26 EPS).
  • Catalysts: Q3 results, more CODs, order wins, debt refinancing.
