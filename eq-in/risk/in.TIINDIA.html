Analysis of Tube Investments of India Limited (TIINDIA)

Overview: TIINDIA (Murugappa Group) reported robust H1 FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025): Consolidated revenue up 14% YoY to ₹10,832 Cr, PAT attributable to owners ₹386 Cr (down slightly YoY due to EV losses), standalone PBT up 9% to ₹473 Cr. Key drivers include CG Power’s power/industrial growth and emerging semis/EV bets. ROIC remains strong at ~44% annualized (standalone). Balance sheet healthy (Net Worth ₹11,352 Cr consolidated, D/E 0.03). High capex (₹1,067 Cr H1) signals expansion.

Tailwinds (Supportive Factors)

  • Revenue Momentum: Consolidated Q2 revenue +12% YoY (₹5,523 Cr), led by Power Systems (+48% YoY to ₹1,254 Cr via CG Power) and Industrial Systems (+22% YoY). Standalone +3% YoY.
  • Profitability Resilience: Standalone PBT margins stable ~12%; consolidated operating margins ~10%. Free cash flow strong (standalone Q2: ₹183 Cr; consolidated H1 operating cash ₹655 Cr).
  • Subsidiary Strength: CG Power (56% stake) revenue +21% YoY (₹2,923 Cr Q2), PBT +32%; benefits from QIP (₹3,000 Cr raised Sep’25 for expansion).
  • Govt Incentives: CG Semi eligible for ₹4,901 Cr semiconductor grants (₹3,501 Cr central + ₹1,400 Cr state) under ISM scheme; ₹475 Cr recognized Q2.
  • Low Leverage & Liquidity: D/E negligible (0.03); cash ₹264 Cr; repaid ₹100 Cr CPs.
  • Group Synergies: Diversified portfolio (engineering, gears, mobility, semis) with Murugappa’s 125-yr legacy.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • EV Segment Drag: TI Clean Mobility (TICMPL) posted ₹158 Cr segment loss Q2 (fair value loss on CCPS ₹11 Cr); capital employed negative (₹-1,718 Cr). High capex/inventory strain.
  • Segment Weakness: Shanthi Gears revenue -15% YoY (₹132 Cr); Mobility PBT thin (₹5 Cr); inventory turnover slowed (consolidated 2.91x H1 vs. 6.37x FY25).
  • Working Capital Pressure: Trade receivables +19% QoQ, inventories +32% H1 (₹2,642 Cr); cash conversion impacted despite strong ops cash.
  • Margin Compression: Consolidated NPM 6% (stable but EV/semis losses offset gains); finance costs up slightly.
  • Capex Intensity: H1 investing cash outflow ₹3,887 Cr (PPE ₹1,067 Cr, acquisitions ₹284 Cr RF Components).

Growth Prospects

  • High-Growth Segments: | Segment | H1 Revenue (₹ Cr) | YoY Growth | PBT Margin | |———|——————-|————|————| | Power Systems | 2,325 | +46% | ~21% | | Industrial Systems | 3,225 | +6% | ~9% | | Semiconductors (New) | 236 | N/A | Loss-making (investing phase) |
  • Semis/Electronics Boom: RF Components acquisition (Apr’25), OSAT facility ramp-up with grants; segment assets ₹1,413 Cr.
  • EV/Mobility Scale: TICMPL investments (₹50 Cr H1); long-term tailwind from India’s EV push despite near-term losses.
  • Acquisitions & Funding: Kcaltech (auto HVAC), 3xper (medtech); CG QIP enables semis/power capex.
  • Outlook: 15-20% revenue CAGR potential FY26+ via semis (OSAT FY24-29), power demand (grids/transformers), industrial recovery. Analyst meet (Nov 20) signals confidence.

Key Risks

  • Execution/Investment Risks: Heavy reliance on subs (CG Power ~50% revenue); semis/EV ramp-up delays (e.g., TICMPL negative capital ₹-1,718 Cr); provisional accounting for recent acquisitions.
  • Fair Value Volatility: CCPS in TICMPL (FVTPL) caused ₹11 Cr Q2 loss (vs. ₹569 Cr gain FY25 standalone); sensitive to valuation/liquidity events.
  • Macro/Operational: Inventory/receivables buildup signals demand softening or supply chain issues; FX exposure (foreign subs like Sedis, CG Drives).
  • Regulatory/Litigation: CGPISL tax disputes (management confident); promoter re-class (Yanmar minor, NOC pending).
  • Competition/Cyclicality: Power/semis cyclical; EV funding dilution risk.
  • Valuation: High capex may pressure FCF if growth misses; current ratios stable (1.79x) but monitor WC.

Summary Verdict: Positive Outlook with Managed Risks. Tailwinds from semis/power grants/QIP outweigh EV headwinds; growth prospects strong (semis/EV inflection FY26+). Risks mitigated by low debt/diversification. Buy/Hold for long-term investors; monitor EV execution & WC. (Data as of Nov 5-11, 2025 filings.)
