Analysis of Inventurus Knowledge Solutions Limited (IKS Health, NSE: IKS, BSE: 544309)

IKS Health is a healthcare enablement platform provider focused on US healthcare providers, offering AI-native solutions for revenue cycle management (RCM), clinical documentation, coding, patient engagement, and value-based care (VBC). It leverages agentic AI workflows, human expertise, and integrations (e.g., Google Cloud, Epic). FY26 Q2 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) show robust performance: Revenue INR 7,811 mn (+21.5% YoY, +5.5% QoQ), EBITDA margin 34.8% (+5.3% pts YoY), PAT INR 1,807 mn (+59.9% YoY, 23.1% margin), EPS INR 10.81. 10-year CAGR: Revenue 28%, PAT 32%. Employee base: 12,940 (clinically trained: 2,228). US$5tn TAM (RCM/VBC/patient services).
