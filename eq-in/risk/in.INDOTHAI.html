Analysis of Indo Thai Securities Ltd (INDOTHAI)

Company Overview: Indo Thai Securities Ltd (BSE: 533676, NSE: INDOTHAI) is a Madhya Pradesh-based financial services firm engaged in stockbroking (NSE, BSE, MCX, NCDEX, MSEI), depository services (CDSL DP), and related activities. Recent activities include a Rs. 118.20 Cr preferential issue (equity + warrants, Jan 2025), stock split (10:1, Jul 2025), and ongoing warrant conversions. Proceeds primarily for margin augmentation, client funding, and pro trading. Strong governance shown via compliant fund utilization and unanimous AGM approvals (Sep 2025).
