Kiri Industries Limited (KIRIINDUS) Analysis

Overview: Kiri Industries is a dyes, intermediates, and chemicals manufacturer with a 37.57% stake in DyStar (key value unlock pending sale) and a JV (Lonsen Kiri). Q2/H1 FY26 showed revenue growth (standalone +34% YoY Q2, +20% H1; consolidated +23% YoY Q2) but EBITDA/PAT losses due to high legal costs (DyStar litigation) and finance expenses (copper project debt). Core ops resilient amid industry recovery, but DyStar sale (~USD 700 Mn expected) and copper diversification are pivotal catalysts. Market cap ~Rs. 3,000 Cr (Sep 2025); share price ~Rs. 554.

Headwinds
  • DyStar Litigation Delays: Primary drag—long-stop date extended to Dec 1, 2025 (Chinese approvals pending for Longsheng). Legal costs elevated (~Rs. 10 Cr EBITDA hit Q2 standalone); uncertainty risks lower sale value or liquidation post-Dec 31 court deadline.
  • High Costs: Finance costs Rs. 61 Cr (Q2 cons.); input volatility (naphthalene/petrochem for intermediates like Vinyl Sulphone/H-Acid). EBITDA margins negative (-6% cons. Q2).
  • Forex/OCI Losses: Rs. 47 Cr OCI hit Q2 cons. from FX translation.
  • Quarterly Volatility: Seq. EBITDA swing (profit Q1 to loss Q2) due to non-recurring items.
Tailwinds
  • Revenue Momentum: Strong YoY growth from dyes intermediates (51% H1 mix) and basics (14%); reactive dyes demand recovery, normalized freight.
  • Associate/JV Profits: Rs. 161 Cr H1 share (DyStar Rs. 82 Cr Q2, Lonsen steady); buffers core losses.
  • Capital Infusion: Warrants converted (equity up to Rs. 60 Cr); financial closure ~95% for copper (~8.5-9% rupee interest).
  • Deleveraging Potential: DyStar proceeds to repay debt, fund copper.
Growth Prospects
  • DyStar Unlock (High Impact): ~USD 696 Mn base (valuing DyStar at USD 1.9 Bn); 85-90% chance of closure by Dec 31 via next bidder (strong PE). Enables debt reduction, dividends, copper acceleration. Kiri positioned as fallback bidder (funds arranged).
  • Copper/Fertilizer Mega-Project: INR 10,661 Cr integrated 500k TPA smelter (cathodes/rods/tubes + by-products like gold/silver) + 1.05 Mn TPA fertilizers. 25% IRR; trial runs Dec 2028. Aligns with India’s copper demand (3 Mn MT by FY29) and fertilizer self-reliance (imports 7-9 Mn TPA DAP/NPK). ESG (ZLD); 70% progress (env clearance, basic eng. done).
  • Core Business: Dyes recovery; export focus; capacity utilization up. Lonsen JV strong.
  • Strategic: Diversification reduces dyes cyclicality; potential mutual fund inflows post-DyStar.
