Cohance Lifesciences Limited (COHANCE) Analysis

Company Overview: Cohance Lifesciences (formerly Suven Pharmaceuticals) is an India-based global CDMO specializing in small molecules, niche modalities (ADC payloads/linkers, oligonucleotides), API+, and specialty chemicals (AgChem, performance chemicals). FY25 revenue: ~INR 26 Bn (USD 310 Mn), 58%+ CDMO. Post-merger platform with US presence (NJ Bio). Strong innovator ties (20/20 top global pharmas). Q2/H1 FY26 results reflect integration phase with flattish topline but margin gains.

Headwinds (Near-Term Pressures)

  • Regulatory Setback: USFDA OAI classification at Nacharam FDF Unit-I (6 observations post-Form 483). Led to ~1-month shutdown; phased resumption ongoing. <2% FY25 US revenue impact, but delays shipments/normalization expected in H2FY26.
  • Demand Volatility: Pharma destocking in 2 key commercial molecules; Phase 2-3 reload delays. Biotech funding slowdown delayed NJ Bio shipments by 2-3 quarters (extended CMC timelines).
  • Operational Disruptions: Lumpy CDMO nature amplified Q2 revenue dip (-8% YoY; adjusted +14%). API+ declined 4-22% YoY due to Nacharam issues.
  • Integration Costs: Upfront talent/capex (INR 1.05 Bn H1) and one-time expenses pressured EBITDA margins (23.8% H1FY26 vs. 32% H1FY25).

Tailwinds (Supportive Factors)

  • Margin Resilience: Gross margins expanded to 73.8-74.6% (vs. 70-71% YoY) on favorable mix (niche tech >17% revenue; targeting low-20s FY26), efficiencies, acquisitions (NJ Bio/Sapala).
  • Pipeline Momentum: 9 Phase 3 programs (4 to commercial in 12-18 months; 2 USFDA-approved). ADC: New payload orders, OEB6 block ready. Oligos: cGMP lab operational, audits scheduled. Small molecules: Phase II win evolving to multi-program.
  • Business Development: Strong CPHI Frankfurt traction (70+ CDMO/API+ meetings). 17 new NJ Bio biotechs YTD; innovator audits scheduled. AgChem/Perf Chem +84% YoY on macro recovery.
  • Financial Strength: Net cash INR 1.7 Bn (cash INR 3.9 Bn); free cash flow INR 1.7 Bn H1. ROCE 21.7%; low debt (0.22x EBITDA).
  • Strategic Positioning: China+1 de-risking; niche tech share rising (ADC/Oligo). 22 USFDA audits passed; EcoVadis Gold rating.
