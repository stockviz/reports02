Analysis of Jubilant FoodWorks Limited (JUBLFOOD) - Q2FY26 Overview

Context: Jubilant FoodWorks (JFL) operates a multi-brand QSR portfolio (Domino’s, Popeyes, Dunkin’, Hong’s Kitchen in India; Domino’s, COFFY in Turkey/SL/BD). Q2FY26 showed robust performance with consolidated revenue +19.7% YoY to ₹23.4 bn, EBITDA +19.5% to ₹4.8 bn (20.3% margin), and PAT (continued ops) +53.7% to ₹1.1 bn (4.7% margin). India (standalone) grew 15.8% YoY; network at 3,480 stores (+93 QoQ). Management confident of H2 momentum.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Strong Execution & Market Share Gains: Domino’s India LFL +9.1% (delivery +16.5%), 7th straight positive quarter; mature store ADS ₹83k (+ve YoY). Claims 65-70% pizza market share vs. peers (e.g., La Pino’z). Popeyes double-digit SSG; Turkey LFL +5.6% (inflation-adj), PAT margin 10.4% (accretive).
  • Network Expansion: ~1 store/day; India +88 (Domino’s +81, now 500 cities), Turkey +5 COFFY. YTD Domino’s India +149 stores.
  • Product & Channel Innovation: Hits like Big Big Pizza, Sourdough (Tier 2/3 traction), Flavor Burst. Delivery mix ~73% (+20%+ growth via free delivery). App MAU +28% to 15.6 mn; loyalty 40 mn; new ad monetization (Apple, Flipkart, etc.) targeting 50bps revenue.
  • Margin Resilience: Pre-IndAS EBITDA margin +37bps YoY (India 12.1%); gross margins seq. up. GST input benefits (~50bps) passed selectively. OpEx leverage via automation, G&A control.
  • Macro Support: Festive demand strong (Oct beat plans); govt. measures (GST cuts, rate cuts) boosting consumption. QSR tailwinds in young demographics.

Headwinds (Challenges Faced)

  • Channel Shifts: Dine-in flat YoY, takeaway -19% (cannibalized by free delivery). Delivery bill size dip ~2% (new customer acquisition at lower tickets).
  • Cost Pressures: Cheese/milk inflation post-Maharashtra elections; earlier aggressive pricing (e.g., Big Big Pizza) offset by premium mix shift.
  • Base Effects: High prior LFL (double-digit); Diwali/Chhath in Nov base. Navratri shift impacted Q2.
  • Emerging Brands Drag: Popeyes/Dunkin’/Hong’s still ramping (EBITDA drag halving expected).
  • Macro Pockets: IT-heavy areas (B’lore, Hyd) softer; offset by energy/auto/services.

Growth Prospects (Forward Outlook)

  • India Core: Domino’s target 15% rev growth (5-7% LFL: 3-4% vol +1-2% pricing/mix; +7-10% stores). Total stores ~900 adds over 3 yrs. Popeyes to 100 stores (ADS nearing industry avg).
  • International: Turkey robust (post-IAS29 steady); SL/BD high-teens growth. Total network to scale with 1k+ Domino’s adds.
  • Margin Expansion: +200bps EBITDA (pre-IndAS) over 3 yrs via gross margin +100bps (mix/procurement), leverage (5-7% LFL), OpEx (automation/manpower/delivery). FY26 H1 sets up strong H2.
  • Tech/Monetization: App ad platform scaling; AI (e.g., Cheezilla campaign). Loyalty/digital driving repeats.
  • Medium-Term: QSR penetration low (India CAGR 4-5% to 2028); premiumization (sourdough/gourmet). Oct sales beat plans; festive tailwinds.

Key Risks

Risk Category Description Mitigation
Competition Unorganized/premium rivals (La Pino’z); peers’ strong QSR growth. 65-70% share; superior ADS/stores; innovations.
Input Costs/Macro Cheese volatility; slowdown in IT/export (offsettable). Hedging, insourcing (marination); broad geo-mix.
Execution Store ramp-up (Q1/Q2 focus for ops maturity); dine-in revival (Rs99 lunch). Proven track record; ROIC improving.
Debt/Finance ₹16bn net debt; Turkey hyperinflation (IAS29 volatility). Interest -23% YoY (refinancing); FCF gen.
Regulatory GST shifts; ESOP/postal approvals passed but dissent (10-20% on ESOPs). Compliant; trust secondary buyback approved.
External Festive shifts; Russia discontinued (gain ₹847 mn in Q2). Diversified (India 70% rev); intl. steady.

Overall Summary: JFL demonstrates resilient growth (19%+ rev, 54% PAT) amid QSR tailwinds, led by Domino’s scale/innovation and Turkey accretion. Tailwinds from expansion/digital outweigh headwinds like channel shifts/costs. Prospects strong (15% India growth, +200bps margins), but base effects/competition warrant watch. Risks manageable via execution focus; stock poised for re-rating on H2 momentum. Valuation context: Trades at ~50x FY26 EPS est. (post-results beat). Buy/Hold for growth investors.
