Analysis of Mangal Credit & Fincorp Limited (MANCREDIT)

Mangal Credit & Fincorp Limited is a Base Layer NBFC (NBFC-BL) focused on lending activities (loans ~75% of assets). FY26 H1 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) show robust AUM growth amid expansion, but moderated profitability due to rising costs. Key metrics: Total Assets ₹450 Cr (up 29% YoY), Loans ₹339 Cr (up 16%), PAT ₹6 Cr (down 14% YoY), GNPA 1.27% (stable), Debt/Equity 1.73x. Recent actions include branch openings, NCD issuances (₹40 Cr outstanding), warrant conversion (₹12.8 Cr equity infusion), and AGM approvals for promoter loans/remuneration.
