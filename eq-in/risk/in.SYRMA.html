SYRMA SGS Technology Limited (SYRMA) Analysis

Based on Q2/H1 FY26 Earnings (ended Sep 30, 2025), Financials, Earnings Call, Announcements (ESOP, AGM, PCB Press Release), and Related Disclosures.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Robust Financial Momentum: Consolidated H1 revenue ~INR 2,109 Cr (+~10% YoY implied, Q2 +37% YoY); record EBITDA (INR 211 Cr, 10.1% margin, +64% YoY); PAT INR 116 Cr (5.5% margin). Gross margins expanded 500 bps YoY to 24.3% due to better mix (Industrial ↑ to 26%, Auto 24%, Consumer ↓ to 32%).
  • Strategic Diversification:
    • 60% acquisition of Elcome (defense, INR 200 Cr rev, 25% EBITDA; consolidation Q4 FY26; growth to INR 300-350 Cr).
    • JVs: Elemaster (Italy, railways; India focus, future global integration); Shinhyup (Korea, PCBs; groundbreaking Dec 2025).
    • KSolare (solar inverters, INR 300 Cr rev; manufacturing at Pune).
  • Backward Integration & Govt Support: PCB plant (INR 850 Cr capex Phase 1; ECMS/PLI approved; trial prod Dec 2026; steady-state rev potential INR 2,500 Cr). PLI incentives ~INR 20-25 Cr FY26.
  • Demand Visibility: Order book INR 5,800 Cr (Auto/Industrial/Consumer ~35% each); 8 new customers ($100M FY27 potential); long-term frameworks ($250M over 2-3 yrs).
  • Export/ODM Strength: Exports H1 INR 502 Cr (+35% YoY); ODM 38% of rev.
  • Balance Sheet: Net cash INR 477 Cr (treasury INR 758 Cr > gross debt INR 282 Cr); QIP proceeds (14.3 Cr shares, INR 10 Bn) funding growth.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Margin Volatility: Q2 gross margin dip to 23.8% (vs Q1 24.8%) due to higher IT mix (low-margin, job-work basis inflating receivables/payables).
  • Working Capital Strain: Net WC days 73 (+4 YoY); inventories +INR 100 Cr (rare earth/supply chain/US tariff pushouts); receivables +80% YoY (vs 40% rev growth); H1 op cash flow -INR 115 Cr. Target: <65 days by FY26-end.
  • Macro Uncertainties: US tariffs delaying decisions (US ~5-6% rev); supply chain constraints.
  • Customer Concentration: Top-10: 56-57%; Top-20: 72% of rev.
  • Industrial Q-o-Q Slowdown: ~9-10% in Q2 (though H1 +30% YoY).

Growth Prospects

  • FY26 Guidance: Organic revenue +30% (±2%); EBITDA margins >9% (vs prior 8.5-9%; H1 already 10.1%).
  • Medium-Term (FY27+):
    • PCB/Defense/Solar/Railways ramp-up (margin-accretive; PCB exports post-2028).
    • Auto/EV/Industrial (power mgmt, data centers, metering ~INR 300 Cr FY26); IT (laptops, Dynabook onboarding).
    • Exports target INR 1,000 Cr FY26 (on track); long-term >25% of rev.
    • Total: Superior growth vs FY26 (excl. PCB/Defense initially); ROCE 15% (adj. for goodwill/IPOs).
  • Capex: H1 INR 45 Cr; FY26 INR 105-145 Cr (incl. PCB); scalable based on demand.
  • Talent/Jobs: 10,000+ employees; PCB to add 1,000+ jobs.
