📣 Final Conclusion

Shekhawati Industries is undergoing a material strategic pivot — from a declining textile player to a real estate and investment-focused entity. The recent profit surge is not from operations but from exceptional items, likely tied to debt restructuring, asset sales, or lease derecognition.

✅ Opportunity: The company holds significant real estate assets (₹1,495L) and may be positioning for large-scale monetization. Shareholders approved RPTs, indicating confidence in the Ruia family-led management.

❌ Risk: Investors relying on sustainable earnings may be disappointed. The stock is currently a story play on real estate development, not a cash flow-generating textile business.
