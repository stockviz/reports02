Analysis of Transindia Real Estate Limited (TREL)

TREL, part of the Allcargo Group, is primarily engaged in logistics parks, commercial properties, investment properties, and equipment hiring (non-crane). It reports under Ind AS with a focus on real estate investments via subsidiaries. FY26 H1 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) show resilient profitability despite revenue softness, driven by other income and asset-light model. Standalone PAT at ₹16.35 Cr (down 39% YoY) on revenue of ₹23.64 Cr; consolidated PAT at ₹16.25 Cr (up 44% YoY) on revenue of ₹41.10 Cr. Balance sheet remains equity-dominant (low debt), with assets at ~₹1,359 Cr standalone / ₹1,349 Cr consolidated. Key events: Land sale by subsidiary (₹14 Cr), ongoing tax search, proposed subsidiary merger, and smooth AGM (all resolutions passed >99% favor).
