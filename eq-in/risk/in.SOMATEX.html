Analysis of Soma Textiles & Industries Ltd. (SOMATEX)

Company Overview: SOMATEX, a legacy textiles firm (est. 1940), has ceased manufacturing operations, sold plant & machinery, and pivoted to trading (cotton/yarn) and investments. Recent promoter change (Jul 2025): Old Somany family sold to new promoters (Roadway Solutions India Infra Ltd., Ameet Harjinder Gadhoke, Teja Ranade Gadhoke). H1 FY26 (ended Sep 2025) showed net profit of ₹300 lakhs (standalone/consolidated), driven by exceptional items (FX gains on associate loan, asset sales). Cash reserves strong at ₹148 Cr; equity ~₹1,608 Cr. Seeking shareholder approvals via postal ballot for aggressive infra diversification.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)
  • Promoter Infusion & Expertise: New infra-focused promoters (Gadhokes) bring 20+ years in roads/EPC. Appointments of MD (Ameet Gadhoke), Chairperson (Teja Gadhoke), and 3 Independent Directors signal governance upgrade.
  • Strong Liquidity: Cash & equivalents surged to ₹148 Cr (from ₹18 Cr YoY) via asset sales/ICDs. Investments in MFs yielded gains.
  • Strategic Pivot: Trading + investments profitable (EPS ₹0.91 H1). Postal ballot approvals enable:
    • Massive infra exposure (₹5,300 Cr EPC/HAM road projects with Roadway Solutions India Infra Ltd.).
    • ICDs (₹800 Cr aggregate from group entities like Vadodara-Mumbai Expressway cos, Mumbai Falcons Racing).
  • Borrowing Flexibility: Proposed hike to ₹800 Cr (from ₹300 Cr) supports expansion.
  • Diversification: Precast concrete acquisition (₹100 Cr) for vertical integration; tyre mfg exploration (₹100 Cr) into high-growth sector.
Headwinds (Challenges)
  • Core Ops Weakness: Revenue negligible (₹205 lakhs H1 vs. ₹503 lakhs YoY); no mfg, reliant on trading/investments. Employee costs high (₹239 lakhs H1).
  • Profit Volatility: H1 profit from exceptionals (₹312 lakhs: FX gain ₹123 lakhs, asset sales ₹189 lakhs). Q2 loss pre-exceptionals (₹248 lakhs).
  • Going Concern Flag: Auditors highlight “material uncertainty” – stopped ops, asset sales; mgmt claims sufficient assets (₹1,642 Cr total).
  • Associate Impairment: Soma Textiles FZC (40%) fully impaired; no further loss recognition.
  • Recent Promoter Change: Integration risks post-Somany exit; execution dependency on unproven infra pivot.
Growth Prospects
  • High-Upside Infra Play: ₹5,300 Cr road projects (EPC/HAM) + precast acq could transform into infra major (vs. current ₹93 Cr FY25 revenue). Group synergies via RPTs.
  • Funding Access: ₹800 Cr ICDs/borrowings fuel capex/working capital.
  • Diversification: Tyre mfg (in-house/licensing/acquisition) taps ₹1 lakh Cr+ India market (15% CAGR). Precast adds infra materials revenue.
  • Balance Sheet Strength: Net worth ₹1,608 Cr; low debt (₹26 Cr). Potential 10x revenue if infra scales.
  • Timeline: Postal ballot ends Dec 2025; execution FY26-28.

Projected Trajectory: Base case: Trading sustains ₹50-100 Cr revenue; bull case: Infra ramps to ₹1,000+ Cr by FY28 (EPS 10x). Near-term catalysts: Ballot results, Q3 results.

But core weakness + RPT dependency warrant caution. Monitor Dec 2025 ballot, Q3 FY26 results. Recommendation: Speculative buy for risk-tolerant investors post-ballot approval.
