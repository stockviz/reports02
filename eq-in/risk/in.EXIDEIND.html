Exide Industries Limited (EXIDEIND) Analysis

Current Context (H1 FY26): Modest revenue growth of 1.3% YoY to ₹8,688 Cr (standalone), with EBITDA margin at 10.9% (impacted by input costs and GST destocking). Debt-free balance sheet, strong cash flow (+₹500 Cr from inventory optimization), and lithium-ion gigafactory nearing commissioning. GST cuts (batteries: 28%→18%; solar: 12%→5%) passed fully to consumers, creating short-term headwinds but long-term tailwinds.

Headwinds (Near-Term Pressures)

  • GST 2.0 Destocking: Announcement (Aug 15, 2025) led to channel de-stocking, production cuts, and Q2 degrowth (-2.1%). Aspirational segments (solar -5% Q2, home UPS) hit hardest; extended monsoon compounded issues.
  • Input Cost Inflation: High lead prices (20-30% above historical norms), forex (₹90/USD), and partial pass-through delayed due to GST. Pressures EBITDA (Q2: 9.4% vs. historical 13.5-14.5%).
  • Weak Segments: Exports (tariffs/geopolitics), telecom, e-rickshaw (last-mile) declined; ~12% of revenue impacted.
  • EPR Compliance: New accruals for battery waste management (Q2 “other expenses” elevated); ongoing cost.

Tailwinds (Supportive Factors)

  • GST Benefits: Full pass-through aligns with govt. goals; expected to boost auto OEM (SIAM Oct data: PV +11%, 2W retail +52%), solar (PM Surya Ghar), and consumer demand.
  • Operational Resilience: Inventory down to 93 days (from 110); +₹500 Cr cash in H1. Tech upgrades (Punched Grid for 100% 2W batteries, automation) yielding cost savings/quality gains.
  • Strong Core: 88% business grew 7% YoY; aftermarket double-digit (4W/2W ~10-12%). Debt-free (standalone), ROCE 16.3%, ICRA AAA rating.
  • Solar Momentum: Q1 +35%; GST 5% + subsidies to drive Q3 rebound.

Investment Summary: Exide resilient amid GST transition; H2 inflection likely with demand rebound and lithium commercialization. Balanced portfolio (lead-acid stability + lithium growth) supports re-rating, but monitor input costs and gigafactory ramp. Target multiples: 30-35x FY27 EPS on 12-15% CAGR prospects.
