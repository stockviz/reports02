HFCL Limited (NSE: HFCL, BSE: 500183) Analysis

Overview: HFCL is a diversified telecom and defence player focused on optical fiber cables (OFC), networking products (5G/Wi-Fi), turnkey projects, and defence electronics (radars, fuzes, sights). Q2FY26 showed sharp recovery (EBITDA +374% QoQ), but H1FY26 revenue/PAT declined YoY amid working capital pressures. Order book at ₹10,480 Cr (up 5% QoQ) signals execution visibility. FY26 guidance implies product-led growth, exports, and defence ramp-up.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Margin Expansion & Recovery: Q2 EBITDA margin at 19.5% (up 1,456 bps QoQ) driven by product mix shift (products ~42% of H1 revenue vs. 57% projects in FY22). Cost efficiencies from backward integration (OFC capacity to 33.9 mn fkm p.a.).
  • Export Momentum: Exports at 26% of H1 revenue (up from 12% FY25); new USD 32M (~₹281 Cr) OFC order via subsidiary. Presence in 60+ countries, repeat global clients.
  • Defence Traction: New orders (thermal sights, BMP-2 upgrades); DRDO tie-ups; 1,000-acre AP facility for artillery shells/grenades. High-margin indigenous products (fuzes, radars).
  • Domestic Telecom: BharatNet router orders (West Bengal +2 circles); FTTH/5G leadership (#1 OFC supplier in India).
  • Order Book & Diversification: ₹10.5K Cr OB (govt: 82%, products: 28%); private revenue share rising (81% H1FY26 vs. 32% FY22).
  • ESG & Credentials: 70.7 score; 7 manufacturing facilities; 368 R&D staff (3 centers).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • YoY Weakness: H1 revenue -15%, PAT -77% due to delayed project ramps, high inventories (₹1,151 Cr, +28% YoY), receivables (₹2,280 Cr).
  • Debt Burden: Borrowings ₹1,552 Cr (+20% YoY); finance costs ₹117 Cr H1 (up 33%). Net debt rise amid capex.
  • Project Delays: Turnkey segment volatile (H1 loss-making); reliance on govt (BharatNet delays).
  • Macro Pressures: Global export headwinds in prior years; forex volatility; competition from China in OFC/telecom gear.

Growth Prospects

  • High (20-30% CAGR Potential FY26-28):
    • Defence: 1,000-acre facility + indigenisation (Atmanirbhar); exports via global showcases.
    • 5G/Data Centers: New products (Wi-Fi 7, routers, FWA CPE); ₹1.1 Tn DC capex opportunity by FY29.
    • Exports/Private Shift: Target 30%+ export mix; private to 65%+ revenue.
    • Capacity: OFC to 42 mn fkm; 7 facilities scaling.
    • Pipeline: OB execution + new wins (e.g., USD 32M); product revenue to 50%+.
  • Triggers: Q3 analyst meet (Nov 3); defence tenders; 5G rollouts.

Summary Verdict: Positive Outlook (Buy/Hold). Tailwinds from defence/export pivot outweigh H1 headwinds. Q2 turnaround + OB growth signal FY26 inflection (revenue ~₹8K-9K Cr est.). Risks tied to execution/debt, but margin leverage (15-20%) and diversification de-risk. Monitor Q3 results/OB inflows. Market cap ~₹10.5K Cr offers value at 1x sales.
