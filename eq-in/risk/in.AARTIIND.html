Analysis of Aarti Industries Ltd (AARTIIND) - Q2 FY26 Overview

Financial Snapshot (Consolidated): - Q2 FY26: Revenue ₹2,250 Cr (+21% QoQ), EBITDA ₹292 Cr (+36% QoQ, 13% margin), PAT ₹106 Cr (+150% QoQ). - H1 FY26: Revenue ₹4,118 Cr (+8% YoY), EBITDA ₹508 Cr (+1% YoY), PAT ₹149 Cr (-21% YoY). - Driven by MMA volume surge (98% utilization); offset by US tariffs and forex losses (₹34 Cr). - Balance sheet: Net D/E 0.66x; Capex ₹267 Cr (FY26 guide: ~₹1,000 Cr).

Headwinds (Near-term Pressures)

  • US Tariffs (Aug 2025): Eroding competitiveness vs. China/Europe; impacted MMA, polymers, dyes (US share down QoQ, volumes diverted but at lower margins). No major revenue loss yet, but sustained pain risks further downside.
  • Margin Compression: Aggressive Chinese pricing in agro/fluoro/pharma; muted dyes/polymer demand; raw material/product price volatility.
  • Forex & Finance Costs: ₹34 Cr M2M loss on ECB; elevated at ₹100 Cr (+61% QoQ).
  • Macro/Geo: Volatile oil spreads, geopolitical tensions delaying recovery.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • MMA Strength: Record Q2 volumes (+65% QoQ, +210% YoY); debottlenecking to 300 KT by Q4 FY26; gasoline-naphtha spreads supportive.
  • Cost Optimization: ₹150-200 Cr initiatives (renewables, yield improvements) ~50% realized; more benefits in FY27.
  • Diversification: Export rebalancing to Europe/ME/Africa; domestic pharma steady.
  • ESG/Partnerships: Zero Waste to Landfill (multi-site); DCM Shriram chlorine deal (350 TPD total).
  • Sequential Leverage: Higher utilization driving EBITDA margins up despite adverse mix.

Growth Prospects (Medium-term: FY26-28)

  • EBITDA Target: ₹1,800-2,200 Cr by FY28 (from FY25 ₹1,016 Cr); via ₹300-450 Cr from capex ramp-up (Zone 4 MPP/CaCl2/PEDA by Q4 FY26), ₹350-550 Cr volume/margin gains, cost savings.
  • Capex Discipline: FY26 ~₹1,000 Cr (peaking); FY27 lower; focus on quick ROI projects (medium-ticket, infra-leveraged).
  • Portfolio Evolution: Energy 30-40%; agro/polymer/pharma growth via Zone 4 (5 blocks FY27), CDMO/R&D (3-4 projects), JV (Augene/Re Aarti commissioning H1 CY26).
  • Innovation/Integration: MPP for fast NPD; PEDA forward integration; chlorotoluene/agro/pharma expansion.
  • Visibility: Healthy long-term volumes; India-US trade deal catalyst.
