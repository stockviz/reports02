Analysis of International Conveyors Limited (INTLCONV)

Company Overview: International Conveyors Limited (CIN: L21300WB1973PLC028854) is engaged in manufacturing conveyor belts and related products, with operations in India (Falta SEZ, WB; Aurangabad) and international subsidiaries (Australia, USA, Singapore holding). Listed on NSE (INTLCONV) and BSE (509709). Strong promoter holding (~70%). FY25 showed robust PAT (Standalone: ₹76 cr; Consolidated: ₹92 cr), but H1 FY26 reflects Q2 weakness. Dividend of ₹0.75/share (75% payout) declared/approved at 52nd AGM (Sep 24, 2025). Balance sheet liquidity-rich (investments/loans ~₹50 cr+).

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Robust Liquidity & Treasury Management: ₹27,324 lakhs current investments + ₹13,934 lakhs loans + ₹5,686 lakhs non-current investments. Generates steady non-operating income (dividends, interest, gains). Strong balance sheet (net worth up 8% QoQ) supports acquisitions/dividends.
  • Dividend Discipline: ₹0.75/share approved (yield ~1-2% at current prices); payout post strong FY25 cash flows. Signals promoter confidence.
  • Revenue Momentum: H1 ops revenue +13.5% YoY (standalone +42%); inventory buildup (+₹804 lakhs) hints at order book strength.
  • Governance: Timely disclosures, clean AGM voting (99%+ approval), SEBI-compliant. International presence diversifies revenue.
  • Macro Tailwinds: Infra/mining boom (conveyor demand); India FY26 capex cycle favors industrials.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Operational Volatility: Q2 FY26 PAT loss (₹17 cr) vs Q1 profit (₹52 cr); core revenue flat QoQ, expenses up (materials +19%, finance +44%). Total income -87% QoQ due to “other income” swing.
  • Margin Pressure: EBITDA margin compressed (H1 implied ~10-12% vs FY25 ~25%+); high finance costs (₹451 lakhs H1) on ₹118 cr borrowings.
  • Profit Dependency on Non-Core: ~30% income from investments; core ops contribute <70% PAT, vulnerable to market corrections.
  • Cash Burn: Negative ops cash flow (₹-623 lakhs H1); dividend outflow (₹475 lakhs) + investments offset by borrowings (+₹3,202 lakhs).

Growth Prospects

  • Short-Term (FY26): H1 revenue growth sustainable if Q2 was seasonal/one-off (e.g., monsoon impact on industrials). Target 15-20% topline growth to ₹17-18k cr; normalize other income for PAT ~₹80-100 cr. Dividend yield attractive for income investors.
  • Medium-Term (2-3 Yrs): Leverage ₹50 cr+ liquidity for capex/M&A in conveyor/infra space. Subsidiaries (Intl Belting, Conveyors Holdings) add forex revenue (minor ~1-2% assets/revenue). Infra push (₹11 lakh cr budget) boosts demand; export potential via Aus/US arms.
  • Long-Term: EPS CAGR 15-20% feasible with ops scaling (FY25 revenue ₹151 cr → ₹200 cr+); treasury yields 8-10%. RoE ~20-25% (FY25 ~25%).
  • Catalysts: Q3 results (Dec 2025), buyback potential (escrow mentioned), rating upgrades on liquidity.
