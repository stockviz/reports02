Indiqube Spaces Limited (INDIQUBE) Analysis

Company Overview: Indiqube is a leading Indian managed workspace provider offering flexible, tech-enabled office solutions (IndiQube Grow, Bespoke, One), facility management (Cornerstone), and tech stack (MiQube). It operates 125 centers across 16 cities with 9.14 Mn sq ft AUM, 87% occupancy, and 96% recurring revenue. Post-IPO (Jul 2025), it focuses on expansion. FY26 H1 (ended Sep 30, 2025) showed robust IGAAP-equivalent growth (they emphasize this over Ind AS due to lease accounting impacts).

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)
  • Strong Demand & Execution: 87% occupancy (up from 81% YoY), 24% YoY occupied area growth. Key wins: 1.4L sq ft lease to world’s largest asset manager; 68K sq ft D&B for top automaker.
  • Expansion Momentum: +22 centers, +3 cities (Indore, Kolkata, Mohali), +17% AUM/seats. Tier-1 heavy (Bangalore 69 centers) but Tier-2 push.
  • Recurring & Profitable Model: 96% recurring revenue, VAS at 13% of ops revenue (up from 11%). Revenue-to-rent ratio 2.13x. MiQube app (87K downloads) drives efficiency (24% cashless/paperless).
  • Financial Health: CRISIL A+ rating, improving RoE (13%), net debt reduction post-IPO. IPO proceeds (₹6K Cr) funding capex (new centers) & debt repayment.
  • Macro Support: India office supply opportunity (Grade-B/ageing assets 52%; 79% occupiers prioritize transit access). IndiQube’s full-building (60%) & green-certified (3.29 Mn sq ft) focus aligns.
Headwinds (Challenges)
  • Accounting Distortions: Ind AS losses mask underlying profitability; investor education needed on IGAAP reconciliation.
  • High Capex Intensity: ₹179 Cr H1 investing outflow (PPE/capex); scaling to 125+ centers strains balance sheet (total assets ₹6K Cr, lease liabilities ₹4.5K Cr).
  • Margin Pressure Potential: Other expenses (67% of total) high; brokerage 2.2% of revenue.
  • Market Saturation: Tier-1 cities (e.g., Bangalore) competitive; slower Tier-2 ramp-up.
Growth Prospects
  • Core Workspace (High Confidence): Flexible office market tailwinds from GCCs (60% clients), startups/MNCs. Target: Deeper Tier-2 penetration, full-building leases (landlord lock-ins 10-20 yrs). VAS/digital (MiQube) to boost ARPU.
  • Diversification: MOA amendment for solar EPC/services (rooftop PV, turnkey solutions) – aligns with green push (56% green-certified offices) & renewables boom (govt subsidies/tenders).
  • Projections: H1 momentum + IPO capital → FY26 revenue >₹2K Cr possible (guided strong H2). AUM/seat growth 15-20% YoY; occupancy stabilization at 88-90%.
  • Medium-Term: PAN-India leader in managed spaces; renewables as 10-20% revenue add-on.

Overall Outlook: Positive. Tailwinds dominate with proven scalability, profitability inflection, and diversification. Growth prospects strong (20-30% CAGR near-term), but monitor execution/debt. Risks manageable via cash generation. Stock likely benefits from results/analyst meets (Nov 19). Buy/Hold for growth investors; watch occupancy/debt metrics.
