Overview:
DCM Limited is a diversified company with operations in IT Services (dominant revenue contributor), Real Estate, Grey Iron Casting (engineering), and others. The documents cover: (1) Sep 2025 shareholder letter on KYC/AGM notice (135th AGM outcomes positive, all resolutions passed >99% approval); (2) Nov 2025 Q2/H1 FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) - consolidated revenue stable at ₹3,523 lakh (flat YoY), PAT up sharply to ₹469 lakh (vs ₹42 lakh YoY) driven by JV profits and IT. Standalone remains loss-making (PAT -₹52 lakh). Key issues: Ongoing engineering lockout (since 2019), stalled real estate JDA (termination notice issued Nov 1, 2025), and liquidity strain (current liabilities exceed assets). Auditors flag emphasis of matter (lockout wages) and material uncertainty related to going concern. Management prepared on going concern basis, citing restructuring and realty revival.

Headwinds (Challenges Pressuring Performance)

H1 loss ₹228 lakh; cumulative unprovisioned wages ₹7,719 lakh (₹135 lakh in Q2). Minimal revenue (₹19 lakh H1). Management evaluating restructuring, but ongoing legal/operational halt erodes value.
