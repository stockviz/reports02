Analysis of BLUESTONE (Bluestone Jewellery and Lifestyle Limited)

Bluestone is India’s #2 digital-first omnichannel jewellery retailer (by FY24 market share among peers), focusing on lightweight, design-led daily/occasion wear (higher margins: 25-40%) vs. traditional heavy wedding jewellery. Key metrics from Q2/H1 FY26 (Standalone, ending Sep 30, 2025): Revenue +37% YoY to ₹5.1 Bn (H1: +39% to ₹10 Bn); Adj. EBITDA +1312% YoY to ₹714 Mn (margin 13.9%, +1255 bps); Positive Cash PAT ₹61 Mn (vs. loss last year); 311 stores (+78 YoY); Repeat revenue 51.4%; SSSG 11%. Post-IPO (listed Aug 2025), company is scaling aggressively with healthy unit economics (e.g., FY19/20 store cohort Adj. EBITDA 23.8%).

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

Factor Details
Robust Growth Momentum Revenue CAGR FY23-25: 51%; Q2 SSSG 11%; festive demand pickup; omnichannel sales boosted by offline stores (online-influenced offline share up).
Profitability Turnaround Adj. EBITDA margin expansion to 13.9-15.3%; Pre-IND AS EBITDA positive (₹320 Mn excl. inventory gain); Cash PAT positive; Gross margins 39.5% (+242 bps YoY).
Omnichannel Scale 311 stores (117 cities, 12.6K PINs); Digital sessions 299 Mn (FY25); High repeats (top 3 peers); In-house tech (3D rendering, inventory mgmt.) drives efficiency.
Vertical Integration 75%+ in-house mfg. (#1 peers); Fastest design-to-shelf turnaround; 7.4K+ designs fuel repeats without heavy marketing (top 4 low-spend peers).
Capital Infusion IPO raised ₹8.2 Bn fresh capital (utilized ₹1.9 Bn working capital; ₹6.3 Bn unutilized); Promoter commitment (PC shortfall infusion).
Market Tailwinds Daily/non-wedding jewellery TAM: ₹3 Tn (USD 35 Bn), 15-21% CAGR to 2029 (vs. wedding 6-9%); Youth focus (25-45 yrs) via social/digital.

Growth Prospects

  • High Conviction Expansion: Target 28-32% omnichannel market share; Pan-India Tier 1-3 rollout (healthy ROCE 0%+ for mature stores); Omni sales catalyst (offline boosts online trust).
  • Category Shift: Daily/occasion wear leadership; Data-driven designs/merchandising; Tech edge (in-house stack, virtual trials) for 50%+ repeat revenue.
  • Efficiency Levers: Adj. EBITDA trajectory (H1 15.3%); Inventory gains ₹388 Mn (H1); Low marketing reliance; Scalable mfg. (Mumbai/Jaipur/Surat facilities).
  • Outlook: FY26 guidance implicit via roadshows/earnings: Sustained 30-40% revenue growth; EBITDA margins 15%+; AOV up 33% YoY (₹59K); Customer base 8.6 Mn LTD.
  • Catalysts: Festive/Q3 ramp-up; Roadshows (Singapore/HK Dec 2025); IPO funds fuel 50+ stores/year.

Overall Summary: Bluestone is at an inflection—strong tailwinds from omnichannel scale, profitability inflection, and category growth outweigh headwinds (losses, debt). Growth prospects robust (30-40% rev, 15%+ margins) with low base/risks manageable via execution. Buy/Hold thesis intact for long-term compounding in organized jewellery (5% market penetration). Monitor Q3 festive, gold prices, debt reduction. (Sources: Q2/H1 FY26 results, investor deck, AGM, press release.)
