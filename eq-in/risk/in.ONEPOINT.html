Executive Summary

One Point One Solutions Limited (OPOS) is a full-stack digital transformation solutions provider with operations across BPO, KPO, IT services, and analytics. The company, listed on the National Stock Exchange (NSE), has demonstrated consistent revenue growth, improved profitability, and a strong expansion in its digital and AI capabilities. Recent strategic initiatives, including international investments, new client wins (e.g., Akasa Air), and M&A activity, signal robust growth momentum and diversification. However, macroeconomic and integration-related risks exist, along with high exposure to working capital cycles.

Despite this, the company appears to be on a structural growth trajectory, benefiting from tailwinds in digital transformation, AI adoption, and contact center automation, with improving financial discipline. The upcoming year is expected to be transformational, with earnings growth outpacing revenue.
