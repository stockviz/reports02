Analysis of Compucom Software Limited (COMPUSOFT / 532339)

Compucom Software Limited is an IT services company focused on software development, manpower supply, operation & maintenance (O&M) of government projects (e.g., Rajasthan Labour Dept.), with exposure to wind power generation. Key inputs from disclosures: Q2/H1 FY26 unaudited results (ended Sep 30, 2025), a ~Rs. 58 lakh government work order, and 31st AGM outcomes (Sep 27, 2025). Financials show stable operations (quarterly revenue ~Rs. 72 Cr, PAT ~Rs. 6-7 Cr), 10% dividend (Rs. 0.20/share), and strong shareholder approval (99.99%+ yes votes). Market cap ~Rs. 100-150 Cr (small-cap). Below is a structured summary:

  • Stable Financial Performance: Q2 FY26 standalone revenue Rs. 72 Cr (+2% QoQ), total income Rs. 83 Cr, PBT Rs. 13 Cr, PAT Rs. 6.4 Cr. H1 PAT Rs. 18 Cr. Balance sheet healthy (equity ~Rs. 145 Cr, total assets ~Rs. 200 Cr). Cash from ops positive (Rs. 19 Cr H1).
  • Dividend Payout: 10% final dividend approved, signaling confidence.
  • Government Contracts: New Rs. 58 lakh (3.5 months) manpower order from RajCOMP (Rajasthan govt.), normal business. Builds on LDMS project O&M.
  • Strong Governance: Overwhelming AGM approvals (all resolutions >99.99% in favor). Auditor limited reviews clean; no related-party issues.

  • Moderate (Diversification): Wind power + investments (Rs. 20 Cr+ non-current) could contribute via rentals/interest (Rs. 11-14 Cr/quarter other income). Subsidiary (CSL Infomedia) consolidated.
  • Outlook: FY26 revenue could hit Rs. 300 Cr+ if orders sustain (EPS ~Rs. 2.3 Q2). Trading window opens Nov 15, 2025—positive momentum post-results. Small-cap IT/govt. services tailwind from digital India initiatives.
