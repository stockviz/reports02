Hindustan Zinc Limited (HINDZINC) Analysis

Summary based on Q2 FY26 earnings, investor presentation, disclosures, and recent announcements (as of Nov 2025). HZL is India’s largest integrated zinc-lead-silver producer (~77% domestic zinc market share), world’s #1 zinc producer, and top-5 silver producer globally. Strong Q2 performance amid resilient commodity prices, with record EBITDA/PAT and cost leadership.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Commodity Price Strength: Zinc resilient (~$3,000/t LME), lead steady (~$1,957/t), silver at ATH >$50/oz (34% YoY rise; ~40% of profits). Tailwinds from tight inventories, industrial demand (galvanizing, batteries, solar/EV), and India steel growth (to 300 Mtpa by 2030).
  • Operational Excellence: Record Q2 mined metal (258 Kt, +1% YoY), 5-yr low zinc COP ($994/t, -7% YoY). H1 EBITDA ₹8,328 Cr (+3% YoY), PAT ₹4,883 Cr (+5% YoY), 52% margins. Renewable energy at 19% (target 70% by FY28), fatality-free ops.
  • Sustainability Leadership: #1 global metals/mining in S&P CSA (90/100 score, 3rd year); first Indian ICMM member. Water-positive (3.32x), EcoZen green zinc. Boosts investor appeal (Nifty 100/Next 50 inclusion; TSR +7% vs. Nifty -3%).
  • Financial Strength: Cash ₹8,155 Cr, ROCE 65% (TTM), net cash/flat debt expected FY26. Contributed ₹8,400 Cr to exchequer (H1).
  • Domestic Demand: India GDP 6.5-6.8% FY26; supportive infra/capex.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Volume Pressures: Refined metal down 5% YoY (H1: 496 Kt) due to lower plant availability, silver grades/inputs (H1 silver 293 t, guidance revised to 680±10 t). Lead production -19% YoY.
  • Macro/External: Global GDP slowdown (2.5-3%), trade tensions, volatile commodities. Input softening helped COP but forex (INR/USD) volatility noted.
  • Regulatory/Minor Penalty: GST order (₹1.08 Cr penalty + tax/interest on 2011 Cenvat; appeal planned, “no material impact”).
  • Capex Intensity: Growth capex $350-400 Mn FY26 (total ~$750-800 Mn incl. maintenance); large projects strain cash if delayed.

Growth Prospects

  • Capacity Expansion: 250 Ktpa integrated smelter + mine/concentrator (₹12k Cr, by Q2 FY29; total refined ~1.38 Mtpa). 10 Mtpa tailings reprocessing (₹3.8k Cr, by Q4 FY28; +100 Kt zinc). Debottlenecking adds ~25 Kt zinc. FY27 refined metal >1.1 Mtpa target.
  • Silver Ramp-Up: From 700 tpa to 1,500 tpa (2x growth via fumers, leaching). FY27 ~750-800 tpa possible.
  • Diversification: 510 Ktpa fertilizer (Q1 FY27), VAPs to 50% sales, critical minerals (potash, tungsten, REEs). Mines life 25+ yrs; R&R to 40 Mt.
  • Sustainability Monetization: Green zinc, 70% renewables, net-zero 2050. CSR impacts 2.3 Mn lives.
  • Outlook: FY26 guidance: Mined 1,125±10 Ktpa, refined 1,075±10 Ktpa, COP ~$1,000/t. Multi-year EBITDA upside from volumes/prices (sensitivities: +$100/t zinc = +₹660-690 Cr EBITDA).
