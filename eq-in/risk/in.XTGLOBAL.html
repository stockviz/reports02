Summary Overview

XTGlobal Infotech Limited (XTGLOBAL) is a publicly listed IT/ITES company headquartered in Hyderabad, India. With over 25 years of experience, it operates primarily in Enterprise Applications, Cloud, Automation, RPA, and Finance & Accounting outsourcing. The company serves global clients, particularly in the U.S., across BFSI, Healthcare, Construction, Retail, and Manufacturing sectors.

Its flagship product Circulus, an AI-enabled Accounts Payable (AP) Automation platform, is driving differentiation and growth, especially in high-value niche verticals like construction and infrastructure.

The company recently reported Q2 FY26 financial results (quarter ended Sept 30, 2025), showcasing strong profitability, growth in revenues (especially on consolidated basis), aggressive international expansion, and disciplined capital allocation, including an interim dividend.
