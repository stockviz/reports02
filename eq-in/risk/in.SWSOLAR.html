Analysis of Sterling & Wilson Renewable Energy Ltd (SWSOLAR)

Sterling & Wilson Renewable Energy Ltd (SWREL) is a leading global pure-play solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) and O&M (Operations & Maintenance) provider, with a portfolio of ~24.4 GWp EPC (commissioned + under construction) and 9.1 GWp O&M. FY26 (Apr-Sep) financials show revenue growth but massive losses from exceptional items. Order inflows remain robust. Below is a structured analysis of headwinds, tailwinds, growth prospects, and key risks, based on the provided documents (Q2/H1 FY26 results, investor presentation, disclosures, press release).
