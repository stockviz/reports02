SAATVIKGL (Saatvik Green Energy Limited) Analysis

Overview: Saatvik Green Energy is an integrated solar player focused on PV modules (4.8 GW capacity at Ambala as of Sep 2025), EPC, solar pumps, and new entrants like inverters (UDAY series). H1 FY26 delivered explosive growth (revenue +133% YoY to ₹16,838 Mn; PAT +146% to ₹2,021 Mn), backed by capacity ramps, a 4.68 GW order book, and supportive policies. Post-quarter orders added ~₹477 Cr visibility. Odisha greenfield (4 GW modules + 4.8 GW cells) on track for FY26/27 commissioning.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)
  • Explosive Financial Momentum: H1 revenue/EBITDA/PAT surged 133%/135%/146% YoY; Q2 +62%/39%/36% YoY. Margins resilient (EBITDA 18% H1). D/E improved to 0.44; ROCE 21.85%. Cash-rich post-IPO (₹7 Bn fresh issue).
  • Capacity & Backward Integration: Ambala at 4.8 GW (83% utilization); Odisha adds 8.8 GW total modules + cells (revenue from cells H2 FY27; 3-4% EBITDA uplift via internal consumption).
  • Order Book & Demand: 4.68 GW booked (9-12 month visibility, 97% of capacity); repeat orders from IPPs/EPCs (₹477 Cr new post-Q2). Strong Q3 rebound expected post-monsoon/GST cut.
  • Policy Boost: GST on modules/cells/inverters cut to 5% (Sep 2025); schemes like PM Surya Ghar (10-12 GW rooftops), PM KUSUM (30 GW agri-solar), CPSU Phase-II favor domestic ALMM-listed players.
  • Diversification: Inverters (UDAY: 1.1-50 kW), pumps (₹9 Cr H1), EPC/BESS entry. High-efficiency TOPCon focus (4.2/4.8 GW).
  • Industry Seculars: India solar #3 globally (108 GW generation); 500 GW non-fossil target by 2030; energy demand CAGR 5.5-6%.
Headwinds (Challenges)
  • Quarterly Volatility: Q2 revenue -16% Q-o-Q (₹7,680 Mn vs Q1 ₹9,157 Mn) due to monsoons (4-5 months rain delay), GST cut deferrals (18-day billing halt), logistics shortages. EBITDA margin dipped to 16% (vs Q1 20%).
  • Margin Pressures: Per-watt realization/EBITDA declined Q-o-Q amid scale-up costs, forex/overheads (₹22 Cr H1 impact), monsoon fixed costs.
  • Competition Intensity: Capacity glut risk (peers doubling); new entrants; shift to N-TOPCon obsoletes older PERC (Saatvik 88% TOPCon ready).
  • Execution Hiccups: Odisha ramp (3-4 months stabilization); cell imports until FY27.
Growth Prospects
  • High Visibility (FY26/FY27): H2 FY26 ~2.4 GW execution (₹2,400-2,600 Cr revenue at ₹12-13/W); Odisha Phase-1 modules Q4 FY26, cells H2 FY27 → total 8.8 GW modules/4.8 GW cells. Guidance: 80-90% YoY revenue growth, EBITDA ~16-17%.
  • Margin Expansion: Backward integration + premium products/inverters/pumps/BESS → EBITDA 20%+ sustainable; cell delta 3-4%.
  • Market Capture: Rooftops (PM Surya Ghar), agri (KUSUM), CPSU/utility; exports (USA). Inverters/pumps scale to ₹50 Cr FY27.
  • Long-Term: 190-200 GW RE installs FY25-30 (solar 70-75%); integrated model + bankability (CC+ rating) positions for 20-25% CAGR.

Overall Summary: Strong Buy Thesis. Tailwinds dominate (policy, demand, execution) with 133% H1 growth validating scalability. Growth prospects robust (FY27: doubled capacity, integration-led margins). Headwinds tactical (Q2 dips temporary). Risks manageable via balance sheet/strategy. Target sustained 80%+ YoY FY26 growth; monitor Odisha milestones. Valuation attractive post-IPO at scale-up phase. (Ratings: Tailwinds 9/10; Prospects 8/10; Risks 6/10).
