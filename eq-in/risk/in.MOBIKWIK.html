MOBIKWIK (One MobiKwik Systems Ltd.) Analysis

Stock Overview: Listed on NSE (MOBIKWIK) / BSE (544305). Digital payments (wallet, UPI, bill payments), financial services (credit distribution, wealth), and gateway (Zaakpay). Q2FY26 results show operational momentum but persistent losses. Post-IPO (Dec 2024), focus on scaling via ₹5.3 Bn proceeds.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust User/Merchant Scale: 183.5 Mn users (+10% YoY), 4.71 Mn merchants (+7% YoY). #1 Wallet by GTV; top-3 fastest-growing UPI app (38% QoQ txn growth, 28% GMV growth).
  • Payments Momentum: GMV ₹432 Bn (+53% YoY, 13% QoQ record high). Gross margins at 29.4% (up YoY); Net Payments Margin stable ~14 bps.
  • Financial Services Recovery: Digital Credit GMV ₹8.1 Bn (+16% QoQ); Gross margins 41.8% (231% QoQ gross profit jump). Lending expenses down 29% QoQ.
  • Cost Discipline: EBITDA swing +₹248 Mn QoQ to -₹64 Mn; Contribution Margin 34.4% (+690 bps QoQ). Path to breakeven evident.
  • Strategic Wins: SEBI nod for stock broking; RBI-partnered Digital Rupee/FX Retail launches; AI tools (Lens.ai, voice collections/payments); Zaakpay scaling for enterprises (IRCTC, Uber).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Persistent Losses: Q2FY26 Net Loss ₹286 Mn (QoQ better from ₹419 Mn, but YoY wider). Half-year loss ₹705 Mn. Negative EBITDA (-2.3% margin).
  • Fraud Hit: ₹118 Mn exceptional loss from merchant bug exploit (Nuh/Mewat); ₹219 Mn recovered, ₹66 Mn secured, balance pursued (ongoing FIR).
  • High Costs: Payment processing (₹1.34 Bn), employee expenses (₹353 Mn), lending ops (₹128 Mn). Cash burn: Ops cash flow -₹1.8 Bn half-year.
  • Revenue Pressure: Total Income flat QoQ (₹2.79 Bn); FS take-rate dipped to 7.6%. Waiver with lending partner (per RBI guidelines) impacted prior periods.
  • Macro/Competition: UPI dominance by giants; slowing user adds (3.3 Mn new Q2FY26 vs. 3.8 Mn prior).

Growth Prospects

  • High (Medium-Term: FY26-FY28): | Segment | Key Metrics (Q2FY26) | Outlook | |———|———————-|———| | Payments | GMV +53% YoY | UPI scale, Zaakpay B2B push (100+ modes). | | FS/Credit | GMV +16% QoQ | ZIP EMI expansion; Wealth AUA ₹140 Bn (+10% QoQ). | | New Verticals | FX/D-Rupee live; Broking approved | Annuity from investments; AI-driven personalization. | | Overall | EBITDA path to positive | IPO funds: ₹2.55 Bn unspent for FS/payments/R&D. Target: Breakeven FY26; 20-30% revenue CAGR via inclusion (voice UPI, Bharat focus).
  • Catalysts: GFF partnerships (RBI, banks); 71K new merchants Q2; Multilingual AI for underserved Bharat.

Key Risks (High Impact)

Risk Description Mitigation
Regulatory RBI/SEBI changes (UPI caps, lending norms); Forex/D-Rupee compliance. Strong ties (RBI pilot partner); Waiver precedents handled.
Fraud/Cyber Bug exploits (₹403 Mn incident); Rising digital threats. Independent audit; Recoveries ongoing; Tech fixes.
Competition PhonePe/Google Pay dominance; Margin pressure. Niche (wallet #1, fastest UPI); AI differentiation.

Financial Losses/cash burn (-₹1.4 Bn cash half-year); Debt ₹3.2 Bn. Improving EBITDA; IPO liquidity (₹2.8 Bn cash).

Overall Outlook: Positive with Improving Trajectory. Tailwinds from scale/new products outweigh headwinds; profitability inflection FY26 likely. Risks tilted regulatory/fraud, but management proactive. Buy/Hold for growth investors; monitor Q3 results/FX traction. Valuation: Early post-IPO; peers at 4-6x FY26E sales (MOBIKWIK ~3x implied).
