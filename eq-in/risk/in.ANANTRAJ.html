Anant Raj Limited (ANANTRAJ) Analysis

Based on provided filings (QIP closure Oct 2025, Q2/H1 FY26 results Nov 2025, analyst meet disclosure Dec 2025, share transfer report Sep 2025). Company operates in real estate development (core), data centers/cloud services (growth area). FY26 H1 shows robust growth: Standalone revenue +28% YoY to ₹723 Cr, PAT +50% to ₹144 Cr; Consolidated revenue +24% to ₹1,223 Cr, PAT +34% to ₹264 Cr. Balance sheet strengthened post-QIP (₹1,100 Cr raised at ₹662/share, +4.86% discount to floor). Net cash positive; prepaid ₹125 Cr debt.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • Fundraising Success: QIP oversubscribed by FPIs/DIIs, providing ₹1,100 Cr war chest for expansion without diluting much (equity base up ~5% to 36 Cr shares).
  • Financial Strength: PAT margins expanding (standalone ~20%, consolidated ~22%); low finance costs (₹2.7 Cr H1 standalone); debt reduction (long-term borrowings down 22% to ₹274 Cr standalone).
  • Real Estate Momentum: Approvals/advances for high-value launches (e.g., “The Estate One” 1.09 Mn sq ft luxury in Gurugram; Phase-IV township +5L sq ft); near-completion of Ashok Estate (1.34 Mn sq ft); JV (Avarna Projects) Phase-2 deliveries from Dec 2025.
  • Data Center/Cloud Tailwinds: Capacity showcased via “BHARAT BUILT” event; expansions at Manesar/Panchkula/Rai (initial 20 MW at Rai, total 200 MW planned); Anant Raj Cloud targeting 117 MW IT load by FY28 with funding secured. Acts as DR sites, enhancing reliability.
  • Operational Efficiency: Inventory drawdown (consolidated -24% to ₹873 Cr) signals strong sales bookings; cash from ops ₹156 Cr (standalone).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Working Capital Strain: Current borrowings up sharply (+132% standalone to ₹159 Cr, +124% consolidated to ₹173 Cr); other financial assets ballooned (investments/receivables), tying up liquidity. Cash equivalents down 11-12% (₹223-292 Cr).
  • Margin Pressure Potential: Employee costs +32% YoY (data center hiring?); depreciation up 40% (capex). Other expenses volatile.
  • Transfer/Re-lodgement Issues: SEBI special window saw 1 request rejected (Aug 2025), signaling minor legacy physical share hurdles.
  • Macro Sensitivity: Real estate cyclical; H1 inventory sales may slow if Gurugram demand softens (e.g., oversupply in luxury segment).

Growth Prospects (High Potential)

  • Real Estate (Core Driver): Pipeline adds ~10-15 Mn sq ft (Group Housing-2/3, township expansions); launches in Q4 FY26. Single reportable segment but geographically focused (Haryana/Gurugram prime corridor).
  • Data Centers/Cloud (High-Growth Diversifier): 117 MW by FY28 (from current ~low double-digits); Rai greenfield (200 MW); “Ashok Cloud” IaaS rollout. Positions as hyperscale player amid India DC boom (govt push for digital infra).
  • Funded Expansion: QIP funds + net cash positivity enable capex without leverage spike. FY26 revenue could hit ₹2,500-3,000 Cr (+20-45% YoY) if launches execute.
  • Analyst Engagement: Upcoming ‘Emerging Ideas Conference’ (Dec 2, Mumbai) to showcase no UPSI, but builds visibility.
  • Projections: EPS H1 ₹4.19 (standalone)/₹7.69 (consol.); full FY26 could see 20-30% PAT CAGR if DC utilization ramps.
