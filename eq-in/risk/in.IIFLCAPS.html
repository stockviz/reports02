Key Risks: - Regulatory/Policy: F&O curbs, brokerage caps, true-to-label norms; potential 20-30% broking revenue hit. - Tax/Compliance Uncertainty: IT search outcome could lead to liabilities (block assessment ongoing). - Market/Volume Volatility: 21% YoY ADT decline; prop trading exclusion masks retail weakness. - Execution/Competition: Wealth ramp-up gestation (losses ₹20-25 Cr in H1); RM attrition; institutional reliance (FII outflows). - Financial: MTM volatility; rising finance costs (up 32% QoQ).

Overall Summary: IIFLCAPS faces near-term headwinds from regulations eroding broking revenues (50%+ of mix), but tailwinds in institutional IB and wealth distribution provide diversification. Growth hinges on wealth scale (high ARR potential) and IB execution, with strong balance sheet support. Risks center on regs/tax; long-term prospects positive in India’s capital market expansion (stable PAT margins ~14%, RoE potential 25%+ if wealth delivers). Monitor Q3 volumes and tax updates.
