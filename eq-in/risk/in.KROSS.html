I. Executive Summary

Kross Limited is a specialized manufacturer of trailer axles, suspension assemblies, and critical automotive components for the M&HCV (Medium & Heavy Commercial Vehicles) and agriculture segments. Listed on BSE/NSE in Sept 2024 post-IPO, the company is leveraging backward integration, capacity expansion, and export diversification to drive long-term growth. The year-on-year decline in H1 FY26 revenues reflects a temporary headwind from GST-related demand deferral, but underlying fundamentals remain strong, with a positive outlook for H2 FY26 and beyond.
