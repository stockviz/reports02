Summary Analysis for GSS Infotech Limited (NSE/BSE: GSS)

FY26 H1 (ended Sep 30, 2025) results show standalone profitability but consolidated losses, driven by segment weakness and high costs. Revenue is modest (~₹42 Cr consolidated H1), with a large balance sheet (~₹522 Cr assets) dominated by goodwill (₹405 Cr), investments (₹365 Cr standalone), and borrowings (₹144 Cr consolidated). All AGM resolutions (Sep 25, 2025) passed with >99% approval, including fundraising authority.
