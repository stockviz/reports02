Alembic Limited (ALEMBICLTD) Analysis

Overview: Alembic Ltd, a diversified player in Real Estate (dominant revenue ~80%) and API Pharma, reported solid H1 FY26 (Sep 2025) results. Consolidated revenue grew modestly to ₹10,561L (+1.8% YoY), but profits surged to ₹19,013L (+7.5% YoY) driven by associate share (₹9,627L, likely Alembic Pharma) and other income (₹6,851L, incl. dividends/FV gains). EPS at ₹7.40 (+7.4%). Balance sheet strong (assets ₹2,72,689L, equity ₹2,53,580L). Recent postal ballot approved Udit Amin as MD for leadership stability. Auditors flagged ongoing electricity duty dispute (provision ₹2,052L; ₹35Cr deposited with SC).

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Associate Leverage: ~50% of profits from associates (e.g., Alembic Pharma’s strong Q2 performance); investments grew to ₹2,03,249L (+15%).
  • Real Estate Resilience: H1 revenue ₹8,235L (+/- flat YoY), segment profit ₹3,635L (+ve margins ~44%); investment property up to ₹29,172L.
  • API Growth: Revenue doubled YoY to ₹2,326L; segment profit ₹468L (20% margins).
  • Balance Sheet Strength: Equity up 7.5% to ₹2,53,580L; low debt (borrowings <1% of assets); consistent dividends (₹6,128L payout).
  • Cash Generation: Op cash ₹5,019L; investing inflow ₹1,281L (sales/redemptions).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Modest Core Revenue Growth: Operations up just 1.8% YoY; total expenses flat but cost pressures in construction/employees.
  • Other Income Volatility: 39% of H1 income (₹6,851L) from dividends/FV gains – market-dependent.
  • Declining Liquidity: Cash equivalents down to ₹141L; net cash outflow ₹130L H1.

Growth Prospects

  • Catalysts: New MD for execution; capex in PPE/investment property (₹2,321L H1).
