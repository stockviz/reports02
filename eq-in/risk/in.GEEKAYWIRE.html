Summary Analysis for Geekay Wires Limited (GEEKAYWIRE)

Stock sub-division (1:1 FV split from ₹2 to ₹1/share) approved at 36th AGM (Sep 2025), record date revised to Oct 30, 2025, enhancing liquidity.

  • Profitability Resilience: Gross margins stable (~28-30%); Q2 FY26 revenue/PAT near YoY levels despite one-off other income drop.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Revenue & Profit Dip: H1 revenue/other income ↓8% YoY (other income halved due to likely forex/one-offs); PAT margins compressed to 7.2% (↓ from 8.7%).
  • Rising Costs: Employee expenses ↑23%, depreciation ↑37% (capex-led); finance costs steady but leverage up (debt/equity ~0.97x from 0.99x).

Growth Prospects

  • Margin Expansion Potential: Post-capex, depreciation stabilizes; segment results show underlying strength (total segment profit pre-i/d ↑ YoY).
  • Market Positioning: ISO-certified; diversified products (wires/nails/bolts); FY25 revenue ₹458 Cr base + split liquidity could drive multiple expansion (EPS ₹2.97 H1, annualized ~₹6).
