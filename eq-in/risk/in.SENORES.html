🔍 SUMMARY OVERVIEW: SENORES PHARMACEUTICALS LIMITED

Senores is a global, research-driven specialty pharmaceutical company with focus areas in Regulated Markets (US, Canada), Emerging Markets (Southeast Asia, MEA, Latin America), and the Indian Branded Generics space. It went public via an IPO in December 2024, raising ₹582.11 crores (including OFS), and is actively investing in expansion, R&D, and inorganic growth.

The latest results are for Q2 and H1 FY26 (Sept 30, 2025), showing strong YoY growth across revenues, profits, and cash generation, backed by new product launches, improved operational efficiencies, and market diversification.

1. Outstanding Financial Performance (H1 FY26)

    • Consolidated Total Income: ₹308.51 Cr (H1 FY26) → +69% YoY vs ₹183.36 Cr (H1 FY25)
    • Q2 Revenue: ₹161.76 Cr → +60% YoY

5. Forward-Loading of Growth Expectations
