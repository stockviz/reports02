Oberoi Realty Limited (OBEROIRLTY) Analysis

Summary Overview: Oberoi Realty reported strong Q2/H1FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) with bookings up 17% YoY to ₹2,938 Cr (H1), revenue up 2% YoY to ₹2,767 Cr (H1), and PAT marginally up to ₹1,181 Cr (41% margins). Balance sheet remains pristine (D/E 0.18x, current ratio 4.3x), with ₹2/share interim dividend declared. Low debt, high liquidity, and festive momentum support resilience, but real estate cyclicality and execution risks persist. Stock benefits from premium Mumbai positioning and diversification.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust Demand & Bookings: H1FY26 bookings ₹2,938 Cr (+17% YoY), Q2 ₹1,299 Cr; sustenance sales strong across residential, office, retail.
  • Top-line & Margin Strength: H1 revenue ₹2,767 Cr (+2% YoY), Q2 ₹1,844 Cr (+86% QoQ); operating margins 55-57%, net margins ~40-41% (industry-leading).
  • Financial Health: Net worth up to ₹16,740 Cr (+7% YoY); cash ₹14 Cr, bank balances ₹145 Cr; redeemed ₹60 Cr NCDs; healthy ratios (interest coverage 10.6x H1, DSCR 5.9x).
  • Diversification & Momentum: Leasing ramp-up (Sky City Mall, Commerz III); hospitality stable; awards (e.g., Most Admired Shopping Centre); NCR entry via Gurugram office.
  • Shareholder Returns: 2nd interim dividend ₹2/share (20% payout); EPS ₹32.5 (H1, basic/diluted).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Slow Inventory Turnover: 1,519-1,641 days (H1/Q2); high inventories ₹961 Cr signal project gestation delays.
  • Margin Pressure QoQ: Q2 EBITDA margin 53% vs. Q1 57%; land/construction costs up 43% YoY (₹1,222 Cr H1).
  • Capex Intensity: CWIP ₹170 Cr (+6% YoY); investing cash outflow ₹469 Cr H1 (PPE, properties).
  • Macro Sensitivity: Elevated interest rates could curb buyer affordability; Mumbai-centric (92% segment revenue real estate).
  • Working Capital Strain: Trade receivables up to ₹24 Cr (+117% YoY); cash from ops ₹1,279 Cr offset by taxes ₹381 Cr.

Growth Prospects

  • Pipeline Visibility: Strong pre-sales to fuel FY26 revenue recognition; festive quarter tailwinds expected.
  • Leasing & Expansion: Retail/office leasing growth (Oberoi/Sky City Malls); hospitality EBITDA positive; NCR push unlocks new markets.
  • EBITDA/PAT Trajectory: H1 EBITDA ₹1,693 Cr (+10% YoY implied); FY26 PAT potential >FY25 ₹2,225 Cr with 15-20% bookings growth.
  • Investor Engagement: Nov 2025 meets (UBS, CLSA, Morgan Stanley) signal confidence; investor presentation highlights Q2 update.
  • Long-term: Premium branding, integrated developments (50+ projects delivered); debt capacity for acquisitions.
