Analysis of Samvardhana Motherson International Limited (MOTHERSON) - Q2 FY26 Overview

Key Financial Snapshot (Consolidated, Q2 FY26): - Revenue: Rs. 30,173 Cr (+8.5% YoY, organic ~6-8% ex-FX/acquisitions). - EBITDA: Rs. 2,719 Cr (double-digit YoY growth; margins stable amid seasonal weakness). - Normalized PAT: Rs. 856 Cr (+15% YoY). - Net Debt/EBITDA: 1.1x (stable; target 0.9x FY26-end). - ROCE: 14.2% (impacted by Q1 transitions; expected recovery in H2). - Booked Business: $87.2 Bn (5-6 year visibility; EV ~22%).

MOTHERSON outperformed global LV production (+3% YoY) via content wins, volumes, and Atsumitec integration. H1 CAPEX Rs. 2,600 Cr (full-year ~Rs. 6,600 Cr upper end).

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Strong Order Pipeline & Diversification: $87.2 Bn book (up from prior); non-auto (Aerospace +37% YoY H1, Consumer Electronics +36% QoQ) at $3 Bn booked. Entry into 2W ARAS (Rider Dome), thermal/rotor-stator (Yutaka), elastomers (Rubbertec).
  • Operational Turnaround: Europe polymers margins +100 bps QoQ (6.4%→7.4%); 75-80% transformative actions complete. H2 acceleration via new programs, efficiencies, AI/GBS/automation.
  • Strategic Expansion: 12 greenfields (2 live, 10 ramping FY27); 3 new acquisitions; Honda partnerships (Yachiyo/Atsumitec/Yutaka). Hiring 5,000 engineers for DEMA (Design-Engg-Mfg-Assembly).
  • Balanced Portfolio: Engine-agnostic (ICE/hybrid/EV mix); India/China tailwinds offsetting NA CV weakness (-25%).
  • Financial Discipline: Leverage stable despite CAPEX; FCF improvement expected.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Cost Headwinds: $10 Mn tariff P&L hit (under negotiation); exceptional provisions Rs. 172 Cr H1 (restructuring).
  • Working Capital Strain: Inflation from ramps/greenfields; impacted Q1 profitability/ROCE.
  • EV Moderation: Europe EV taper-off (share 24%→22%); prolonged ICE programs.

Growth Prospects

  • Near-Term (H2 FY26): Stronger revenues/margins from efficiencies, new launches, festive recovery; premium OEM platforms ramping.
  • Medium-Term (FY27+): Global production ~90 Mn vehicles; non-auto ramp (Consumer Elec. profitable FY27; Aerospace Tier-1 Airbus wins). 7th 5-year plan targets 4x growth via DEMA, tech hires, AI.
  • Long-Term: $3 Bn non-auto book to swell; diversification (Aerospace, 3C electronics, 2W); 425+ facilities in 44 countries.
  • Outlook: Double-digit EBITDA/PAT growth sustained; ROCE normalization to 15%+.
