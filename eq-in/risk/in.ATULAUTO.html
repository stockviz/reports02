Atul Auto Limited (ATULAUTO) Analysis: Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Atul Auto Limited, a Gujarat-based manufacturer of three-wheelers (auto-rickshaws, cargo vehicles) with IC engine and EV models, reported solid standalone H1 FY26 (Apr-Sep 2025) performance amid EV softness. Key metrics: Standalone H1 revenue ₹317.8 Cr (↑9% YoY), PAT ₹16.8 Cr (↑19% YoY), volumes ~15.4k units (↑2% YoY). Consolidated H1 revenue ₹353 Cr (↑11% YoY), PAT ₹10.3 Cr (↑94% YoY but lower than standalone due to subs). Oct’25 sales: 4k units (Domestic+Export, ↑5% YoY). Debt-free balance sheet (equity ₹452 Cr standalone), healthy liquidity. Litigation with ExxonMobil settled amicably (₹10L payout, no material impact).

Strong Financials (Standalone) H1 Revenue ↑9%, PAT ↑19%; Margins stable (~7% PBT); No debt, cash ₹3.8 Cr, investments ₹13.7 Cr. Op. cash flow +₹9 Cr H1. Supports capex (₹4.8 Cr H1), dividends; ROE ~15-20%.
