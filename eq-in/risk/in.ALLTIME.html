Analysis of All Time Plastics Limited (ALLTIME)

Company Overview: All Time Plastics is India’s 2nd largest exporter of plastic consumer ware (houseware, furniture, etc.), with 50+ years of operations, 4 facilities (3 plastic, 1 bamboo pilot), 2,200+ workforce, and exports to 29 countries. FY26 H1 standalone revenue: ₹305 Cr (+17% YoY); PAT: ₹17 Cr (-33% YoY due to margins). Post-IPO (Aug 2025), strong balance sheet with ₹136 Cr cash, reduced debt (₹127 Cr from ₹219 Cr).

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust Revenue Momentum: Q2 FY26 revenue +13% YoY (₹147 Cr), H1 +17% (₹305 Cr), driven by 6-15% volume growth and stable pricing. Export mix rising (Q2: 18% vs 16% YoY; H1: 17% vs 17%).
  • High Utilization & Efficiency: 83-95% capacity utilization; automated, energy-neutral plants; polymer processing volumes up 7-15% YoY.
  • Strong Balance Sheet Post-IPO: ₹329 Cr fresh proceeds used for 60% debt repayment (net debt/equity: 0.2x from higher), ₹214 Cr cash/FDs; ROCE/ROE dip temporary due to dilution/capex.
  • Customer Validation: Awards from Target (COPD excellence) & Plexconcil (1st in houseware/plastic furniture exports FY24-25); long-term ties.
  • Cash Generation: H1 operating cash flow ₹53 Cr (+26% YoY).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Margin Erosion: EBITDA margin -870 bps Q2 YoY (11% vs 20%), -470 bps H1 (15% vs 19%); PAT margins halved (Q2: 3% vs 10%). Driven by +29% employee costs, +35% other expenses, higher dep/finance (capex/IPO related).
  • Cost Pressures: Raw material costs up (60% of revenue), inventory build-up; fixed asset turnover down to 1.7x (capex drag).
  • Profitability Dip: PAT -69% Q2 YoY, -33% H1 despite revenue growth; EBITDA -37% Q2 YoY.

Overall Summary: Positive Outlook with Near-Term Margin Recovery. Tailwinds from expansion, exports, and IPO firepower outweigh headwinds (margin squeeze from capex ramp-up). Growth prospects strong (15-20% revenue CAGR), but Q3FY26 watch for EBITDA rebound to 18%+. Risks manageable with robust liquidity; buy/hold for 2-3 yr horizon if plastics demand sustains. Target upside: 20-30% from current levels (inferred from growth).
