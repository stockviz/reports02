TVS Srichakra Limited (TVSSRICHAK) Analysis

Based on Q2/H1 FY26 Unaudited Results (Sep 30, 2025), Balance Sheet, Cash Flows, AGM Minutes, and Press Release.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Revenue & Profit Momentum: Consolidated revenue up 7% YoY to ₹1,746 Cr (H1 FY26); standalone up 7% to ₹1,627 Cr. Q2 revenue grew 18% QoQ (standalone) and 10% YoY (consolidated), driven by tyre sales in a single segment (2W/3W automotive tyres, tubes, flaps).
  • Margin Resilience & Cost Control: PBT up 28% YoY (consolidated ₹38 Cr). Employee expenses and finance costs moderated; debt reduced ~10% YoY (total borrowings down to ₹788 Cr consolidated).
  • Strong Liquidity: Operating cash flow robust at ₹206 Cr (consolidated H1), up from ₹122 Cr YoY. Inventories slashed 24% YoY (₹610 Cr), aiding working capital efficiency.
  • Dividend & Shareholder Returns: ₹16.89/share (169%) declared and approved at AGM (99.99% approval).
  • Global Expansion: Showcased premium tyres (e.g., Protorq Extreme, Trailhound) at Expo Moto Mexico and Colombo Motor Show, targeting Latin America/South Asia. Exports to 85 countries; production capacity 3M tyres/month.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • OCI Drag: Total Comprehensive Income negative (-₹17 Cr consolidated H1) due to forex hedges/items reclassified to P&L (-₹54 Cr pre-tax), offsetting PAT gains.
  • Exceptional Volatility: Net exceptional loss ₹15 Cr (H1 standalone) from VRS (₹3.8 Cr) offset by grant income (₹18.8 Cr from Tamil Nadu SIPCOT subsidy).
  • Margin Pressure: Gross margins squeezed by material costs (up YoY); other expenses high at ₹376 Cr (consolidated H1).
  • Equity Erosion: Other Equity down ~3% YoY (₹1,147 Cr consolidated), reflecting OCI losses and dividends.
  • Moderate Volume Growth: Revenue growth modest amid auto sector slowdown; inventories drawdown signals potential destocking.

Growth Prospects

  • Export-Led Expansion: Key markets (Mexico, Sri Lanka) via premium radials/off-road tyres. R&D in Milan/India, multi-terrain testing positions for global OEM/replacement gains.
  • Domestic Strength: Significant OEM/replacement share in India 2W/3W; TVS Mobility group synergies.
  • Capacity & Tech Edge: 3M tyres/month output; new grant (12-year SIPCOT subsidy) supports capex (₹71 Cr H1 investing outflow).
  • Outlook: H1 PAT +41% YoY (consolidated); full FY26 potential from auto recovery, exports (20-25% of sales implied), and premiumization. EPS trajectory supports 10-15% earnings growth if forex stabilizes.

Key Risks

  • Forex & Commodity Volatility: Heavy OCI forex losses; raw material exposure (rubber/natural resources).
  • Auto Cycle Dependence: Tied to volatile 2W/3W sector (e.g., weak rural demand, EV shift).
  • Execution Risks: VRS signals labor costs; capex continuity amid high debt (D/E ~0.7x).
  • Geopolitical/Regulatory: Export risks (85 countries); Ind AS 20 grant conditions.
  • Competition: Intense from MRF, Apollo, CEAT in domestic/export markets.

Overall Summary: TVSSRICHAK shows resilient H1 growth (revenue +7%, PAT +41% consolidated) amid tyre sector headwinds, bolstered by debt reduction, cash flows, and global outreach. Tailwinds from exports and efficiency outweigh OCI noise; prospects strong in premium tyres/exports (target 15-20% growth). Risks center on forex/auto cycles—watch Q3 for sustained momentum. Neutral-positive stance; attractive at reasonable valuations post-dividend.
