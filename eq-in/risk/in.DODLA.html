Executive Summary

Dodla Dairy Limited reported a strong performance in Q2 FY26 with revenue at INR 1,019 crores and EBITDA margins improving to 9.1%, supported by integration of the recently acquired OSAM Dairy, robust volume growth in milk and value-added products (VAP), and better product mix. Despite seasonal supply-side challenges, the company maintained healthy profitability and provided an optimistic outlook for H2 FY26. Additionally, a favorable ITAT ruling has made the company eligible for a tax refund of INR 22.24 crores, providing a positive non-operating boost.

While growth is solidifying across regions and segments, key risks remain around weather volatility, cost pressures, and integration timelines. However, management remains confident about margin sustainability and long-term expansion strategy.
