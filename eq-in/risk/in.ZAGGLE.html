Zaggle Prepaid Ocean Services Limited (Zaggle) - Analysis Summary

Company Overview: Zaggle (NSE: ZAGGLE, BSE: 543985) is a SaaS fintech player focused on spend management solutions (e.g., prepaid cards, platforms like Propel/Zoyer/Dave, program/SaaS fees). FY26 H1 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) show robust growth, with diversified revenue streams (program fees: ~40%, Propel/gift cards: ~57%, SaaS/service fees: ~3%) and >3.5 Mn users. All revenue domestic (India). Recent filings highlight contracts, awards, product launches, and capital raises.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Explosive Financial Momentum: Highest-ever Q2/H1 FY26 performance. Consolidated revenue ₹7,642 Mn (+37% YoY), Adjusted EBITDA ₹765 Mn (10% margin, +39% YoY), PAT ₹611 Mn (+68% YoY). Q2 revenue ₹4,322 Mn (+42% YoY), PAT ₹350 Mn (+79% YoY). Cash PAT ₹751 Mn (+64% YoY). Strong across segments: Program fees (+38% YoY), Propel (+47% YoY), SaaS (+38% YoY).
  • Client Wins & Partnerships: New 3-year ₹9.84 Cr contract with GIC (employee expense mgmt.); agreement with BIBA Fashion (Zoyer platform, 36 months). Expanded banking network (Standard Chartered, AU SFB, Suryoday SFB); retail cards entry with AU SFB.
  • Product Innovation & Recognition: Launches: Zaggle GlobalPay Forex Card, Mastercard Prepaid Card. Top 10 DGEMS 2025 (Forbes India). Multi-payment ecosystem (credit/prepaid/retail cards + UPI).
  • Capital Strength: IPO/QIP proceeds largely deployed (tech/products, debt repayment); unutilized funds in safe instruments. EGM approved ₹600 Mn warrants (preferential issue).
  • Acquisitions: Greenedge Enterprises (55.56% stake, subsidiary w.e.f. Sep 30); contributions from subsidiaries/associate (e.g., ₹25 Mn share of associate profit H1).
  • Guidance Upgrade: Revenue growth to 40-45% FY26 (from prior); EBITDA margins 10-11%.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Cash Flow Pressure: Negative operating cash flow (-₹344 Mn H1 FY26) due to working capital buildup (trade receivables +₹543 Mn YoY to ₹2,692 Mn; other assets +₹900 Mn). High growth straining liquidity despite ₹802 Mn cash.
  • High Capex Intensity: ₹409 Mn invested in PPE/intangibles H1; intangibles under dev. up to ₹583 Mn. Depreciation/amort. doubled YoY (₹145 Mn H1).
  • Cost Pressures: Employee expenses, incentives/cashback (₹2,114 Mn H1), other expenses up (travel/marketing). ESOP costs (₹16 Mn H1, adjusted out).
  • Domestic Concentration: 100% revenue India; limited international exposure amid global fintech competition.
  • Macro Sensitivity: Rising sales/marketing in line with growth, but potential slowdown in corporate spending could hit.

Growth Prospects

  • High (40-45% Revenue Growth Guided): User/client additions, cross-sell (new products to existing 3.5 Mn+ users), diversified revenue (SaaS/cards/programs). Retail cards/UPI expansion could drive B2C scale. Subsidiaries/associate adding synergies (e.g., Mobileware, Span Across IT).
  • 3-5 Year Outlook: Tech investments (₹400 Mn IPO allocation) position for market leadership in spend mgmt. (50 Mn+ prepaid cards issued historically). Banking partnerships enable ecosystem lock-in. Potential M&A (recent Greenedge). EBITDA margins sustainable at 10%+ with scale.
  • Catalysts: Analyst meet (Nov 21), warrant conversion (₹600 Mn infusion), further contract wins (e.g., GIC-like deals).

Key Risks

Risk Category Details Mitigation
Execution New products/partnerships (retail cards, forex) unproven at scale; integration risks from acquisitions (Greenedge provisional accounting). Proven track record (IPO/QIP execution); strong mgmt. (MD Avinash Godkhindi).
Financial High receivables (₹2.7 Bn, 35% of assets); negative FCF. Dilution from warrants (10.6 Mn, ~8% equity) + ESOP (18k shares allotted Q2). Cash reserves ₹5 Bn+ (bank balances); debt low (₹79 Mn).
Regulatory/Competition Fintech regs (RBI on prepaid/cards); rivals (e.g., Pine Labs, Fi Money). Compliance filings robust; differentiated B2B2C model.
Market Corporate spend cyclicality; 100% India exposure (e.g., to BFSI/tech clients). Diversified sectors (BFSI, tech, healthcare, FMCG); no promoter/group interest in new deals.
Other Forex (new GlobalPay); credit impairment provisions (₹12 Mn H1). Schedule changes (e.g., investor meets). No material RPTs; audited/limited review clean.

Overall Outlook: Strong Buy/Bullish. Tailwinds dominate with hyper-growth, innovation, and wins outweighing cash/execution headwinds. Risks manageable for a high-conviction fintech play. Monitor Q3 results (Feb 2026) and warrant conversion for sustained momentum. Market cap implied positive reaction to H1 beat/guidance upgrade.
