IXIGO (Le Travenues Technology Limited) Analysis

Company Overview: IXIGO is an AI-powered online travel agency (OTA) focused on flights, buses, trains, hotels, and cabs, targeting India’s next billion users. Key metrics from Q2/H1 FY26 (ended Sep 30, 2025): GTV ₹4,347 Cr (+23% YoY), Revenue ₹283 Cr (+37% YoY), Adj. EBITDA ₹28.5 Cr (+36% YoY), H1 Op. Cash Flow ₹91.5 Cr (+30% YoY). Recent ₹1,295 Cr preferential allotment to MIH Investments One B.V. (Prosus affiliate, now ~14.6% stake) bolsters balance sheet.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • Robust Growth Outpacing Market: Flights (+29% YoY GTV), Buses (+51%), Trains (+12%); market share gains across verticals despite sector headwinds.
  • Strong Financials & Cash Generation: Consistent EBITDA/CFO growth; healthy margins (Contribution Margin +20% YoY).
  • Capital Infusion: ₹1,295 Cr raise at ₹280/share (10.1% dilution) funds AI/tech investments, international expansion (Singapore WOS with SGD 50K initial capex).
  • Product & Partnership Momentum: AI Smart Filters, Train Alternates, Aadhaar IRCTC auth, DMRC/ONDC/Delhi Metro integration, 7 new SRTCs (AbhiBus now covers 17), HDFC tie-up.
  • Scale: 54 Cr FY25 AAU; leadership in trains OTA.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Capacity Constraints: Q2 faced airline/bus capacity headwinds, tempering absolute growth (e.g., Train GTV +12% vs. higher peers).
  • One-Off Costs: ₹27 Cr ESOP expense dragged reported PBT to -₹2.5 Cr (ex-ESOP: +₹24 Cr, +26% YoY).
  • Macro/Seasonal Pressures: Travel sector vulnerability to fuel prices, economic slowdowns, monsoons (Q2 typically softer).

Growth Prospects

  • High: Fresh capital enables new categories (e.g., hotels/cabs scaling), international push via Singapore sub for synergies/markets.
  • Domestic Expansion: Bus network buildout, flight monetization (Revenue +60% YoY), AI-driven personalization/user acquisition.
  • Long-Term: AI-first platform for “smartest travel decisions”; 20-40% YoY growth sustainable if market expands (India travel ~$20-25 Bn GTV potential).
  • Projections: H1 momentum + balance sheet strength → FY26 Revenue/EBITDA could exceed 30-40% YoY; market leadership in buses/trains, flight catch-up.

Headwinds are short-term (capacity), but risks like dilution and competition warrant monitoring. Buy/Hold bias for long-term investors; track Q3 execution and Singapore progress.
