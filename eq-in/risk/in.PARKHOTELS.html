Analysis of Apeejay Surrendra Park Hotels Limited (PARKHOTELS)

Company Overview: ASPHL is a leading Indian hospitality player with a portfolio of upscale/upper mid-scale hotels (The Park, Zone brands) and F&B (Flurys). Q2/H1 FY26 showed record revenue growth amid industry-leading occupancy (93%) and RevPAR (12% YoY). Strong balance sheet (net worth ₹1,305 Cr, ICRA A+/positive) supports expansion.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Operational Excellence: India’s highest occupancy (93%), 13% ARR growth, 12% RevPAR growth in upper upscale segment. Flagship hotels (Kolkata 100%, Chennai 97%) outperform peers by 20-30% premium. F&B contributes 43% revenue (stable earnings buffer).
  • Brand Momentum: Global awards (Michelin Key, Prix Versailles for Ran Baas Patiala; Travel+Leisure for Lotus Palace). Flurys (102 outlets) grew 22% YoY in Q2, high single-digit EBITDA margins.
  • Favorable Industry Cycle: “Super cycle” with demand-supply gap (demand 10.5% CAGR vs. supply 8.4%; only ~90K rooms added over 5 years). Tailwinds from weddings (36 dates Nov-Mar), T20 World Cup (Feb 2026), state visits, rising per capita income (~$3K), air traffic growth (5-26% in key cities).
  • Asset-Light Expansion: 588 keys adding in FY26 (73% management/lease). Target 50 hotels/3,000 keys by FY26 end (from 36/2,436). Pipeline: 500 keys/year long-term.
  • AI/Digital Boost: Nor1 (AI upsell), SAP S/4HANA, web check-ins (₹15 Cr capex) for pricing/forecasting.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • EBITDA Pressure: Consolidated EBITDA flat/decline YoY due to one-time other income drop (insurance claims FY25). Operating EBITDA strong (15% growth, 30% margin) but PAT volatile.
  • Project Delays: Kolkata (Jan 2026 groundbreaking, Jan 2029 open; +9 months), Pune/Vizag (+12-18 months), Zillion Mumbai (Dec 2026; +6 months). Capex heavy (H1 outflow ₹188 Cr).
  • Localized Weakness: Hyderabad RevPAR -8% QoQ (CBD softness offset by 90% occupancy premium). Flurys additions cut to 30 (from 50 guidance) due to infra buildup.
  • High Costs: Employee/other expenses up 15-18% YoY with revenue.
