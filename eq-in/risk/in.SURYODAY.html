Suryoday Small Finance Bank (SURYODAY) Analysis: Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Suryoday Small Finance Bank, focused on inclusive finance (IF) and retail secured lending, reported Q2/H1 FY26 results amid microfinance sector stress. Gross advances grew 18.9% YoY to ₹11,124 Cr, deposits +35.5% to ₹11,991 Cr, but profitability dipped (PAT -33% YoY in Q2 to ₹30 Cr) due to asset quality pressures and NIM compression. Secured assets now ~55% of mix. Below is a structured summary based on earnings call, investor presentation, financial results, and filings.

Headwinds (Challenges Pressuring Performance)

  • Asset Quality Deterioration: GNPA rose to 5.9% (from 2.9% YoY), NNPA 3.8% (from 0.8%). Slippages ₹206 Cr in Q2 (down QoQ but elevated); IF PAR30+ at ~11-12%. Sector-wide MFI stress from headwinds like monsoons/GST delays.
  • Profitability Squeeze: NIM fell to 6.9% (-276 bps YoY) due to yield drop (16.4% from 19.6%), non-performing drag, and faster deposit growth. Cost-to-income at 76.6% (up 1,312 bps YoY). PAT/RoA/RoE down (0.8%/6.2% in Q2).
  • Collection Efficiency Dip: Current bucket CE at 98.6% (stable), but overall (1 EMI adj.) at 86% (down from 93% YoY). Prior book (<12M vintage) CE ~98%, but legacy stress persists.
  • Other Income Volatility: PSLC/investment gains halved; muted Q3 outlook.

Tailwinds (Supportive Factors)

  • Liability Franchise Strength: Deposits +35.5% YoY; retail share 86% (up 581 bps), CASA 20.7% (up 280 bps). Digital deposits ₹1,300 Cr (~10% of book, low CAC).
  • Secured Growth & Diversification: Non-IF (CV/mortgages) at 55%; CV +49% YoY to ₹1,564 Cr, mortgages +39% to ₹2,555 Cr. Vikas loans (individual, 70% of IF) disbursements +111% YoY.
  • Risk Mitigation via CGFMU: ~98% IF coverage; 3rd claim ₹313 Cr received (100%). NNPA ₹411 Cr vs. ₹378 Cr receivable; protects capital (CAR 23.4%, well above 15%).
  • Improving Collections/Green Shoots: Last-12M book CE 99.5%; slippages trending down (Q3 target ₹35 Cr/month). Digital collections (UPI/SI) rising.

Growth Prospects

  • Advances/Deposits: 30-35% FY26 advances growth (H2 acceleration via ₹8,000 Cr pre-approved limits). Deposits 40-45% growth, CASA >22%. Run-rate: IF ₹415-500 Cr/month.
  • Product Mix Shift: Secured to 65% by FY28 (from 55%); scale MSME, used cars/CE, digital (UPI credit: 50k users/₹50 Cr sanctions; secured CC).
  • Profitability Recovery: RoA 1.2% FY26 (H2: paying book to ₹4,700-4,800 Cr at 25-26% yield; lower credit costs). Steady-state yield 17-18%; NIM ~8%.
  • Digital/Franchise: 3.6 Mn customers (+12% YoY); 1 Mn pre-qualified Vikas leads (₹9k Cr opp.); SBOs (smart outlets) for granular retail.
  • Capital Headroom: CAR 23.4%; ₹1,000 Cr equity raise (enabling, not immediate). FY27 growth to ₹15,000 Cr advances without dilution.

Overall Outlook: Steady recovery in H2 FY26 via collections, disbursements, and mix shift; FY27 stronger growth. Stock trades at stressed multiples but offers value if asset quality stabilizes (target GNPA 5%/NNPA 3% FY26-end). Monitor MFI flows and CGFMU claims.
