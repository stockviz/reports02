Borrowing approvals fund ₹10,000+ Cr capex. - Strategic Positioning: Gadchiroli focus (local land/manpower) aligns with India’s mining push; Japan roadshow for equity/debt.

Key Risks (Moderate-High): - Debt & Execution: High gearing (total liabilities ₹11,974 Cr cons.); capex delays/CWIP slippage; negative investing cash (₹2,505 Cr H1). - Regulatory/Legal: NTPC receivable dispute (₹262 Cr in step-down sub, Delhi HC suit); mining approvals, land title issues. - Commodity/Concentration: 60%+ revenue mining/steel; price volatility (e.g., Q2 mining profit down 43% QoQ). - RPT Scrutiny: Material RPTs approved (Thriveni, pellets subs) but public institutions voted against some (43-83% favour); promoter interests. - Other: FX (intl subs), integration (10+ step-downs), auditor emphasis on receivables; low cash (₹2 Cr standalone).

Overall Outlook: Strong tailwinds from expansions/acquisitions position LME for 30-50%+ CAGR over 2-3 yrs, but debt/cash burn demands monitoring. Bullish if steel/MDO delivers; risks tied to execution/commodities. Market cap potential uplift via global visibility.
