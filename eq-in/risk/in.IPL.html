I. Company Overview

India Pesticides Limited (IPL) is a specialized agrochemical manufacturer focused on technical-grade agrochemicals and active ingredients (API), with growing emphasis on formulations and specialty chemicals. Established in 1984 and headquartered in Uttar Pradesh, India, the company operates two manufacturing facilities in Lucknow and Sandila (Hardoi). Through its subsidiary Shalvis Specialities (Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh), it is expanding into higher-value specialty molecules.

The company has strong compliance credentials: ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, ISO 45001:2018, and ISO 10002:2018 ‚Äî reflecting commitment to quality, safety, environment, and customer satisfaction.
