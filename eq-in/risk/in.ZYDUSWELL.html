Zydus Wellness Limited (ZYDUSWELL) Analysis: Q2 & H1 FY26

Overall Performance Snapshot
Zydus Wellness reported resilient topline growth amid headwinds: Consolidated net sales grew 31% YoY to ₹6,429 mn in Q2 FY26 (12.8% in H1 to ₹15,006 mn), driven by new acquisitions (Comfort Click) offsetting seasonal softness. EBITDA rose 17.3% YoY to ₹230 mn in Q2 (2.1% H1 growth), with gross margins expanding +483 bps YoY to 52.4% in Q2 due to favorable input costs. PAT turned negative in Q2 (-₹528 mn) due to one-offs (acquisition costs, amortization), but adjusted PAT was -₹186 mn (H1 positive at ₹1,093 mn). Cash EPS accretive from Comfort Click. Balance sheet strengthened by acquisitions; debt up due to bridge financing.

Headwinds (Short-term Pressures)

  • Weather Impact: Early/prolonged monsoons hit seasonal brands (Glucon-D, Nycil), causing de-growth and deleverage (80-90% sales typically in Q4/Q1).
  • GST 2.0 Disruptions: Transitory slowdown in GT/organized trade; unquantified but stabilized post-Q2.
  • Category/Macro Softness: Nutrition drinks (Complan) de-grew 3.2%; overall categories flat/low single-digit (e.g., Glucon-D/Nycil). Nielsen under-reports e-com (15-20% of sales).
  • Acquisition Overhang: Comfort Click amortization (₹2,400 cr brands over 15 yrs → ~₹40 cr/qtr); bridge loan interest (5% on GBP 240 mn); one-off costs (Naturell liquidation).
  • Commodity Divergence: Edible oils/milk easing, but others firm; thin Q2 margins from seasonal deleverage.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • Non-Core Resilience: Double-digit growth in non-seasonal portfolio (ex-seasonal/new buys).
  • Acquisitions Delivering: Comfort Click (VMS e-com/D2C; 57% 5-yr CAGR, Amazon ratings >4.6) in-line post-acq; RiteBite Max Protein beat estimates (topline/bottomline), integrated into distribution.
  • Channel Shifts: Quick commerce/e-com booming; Tier 2/3 expansion; distribution to 3-3.5 mn outlets.
  • GST Reforms: 85% products at 5% slab; affordability boost from tax cuts/spending.
  • Margin Tailwinds: Input recovery (milk -1.1%, dextrose -10.7% YoY); gross margins at multi-year highs.
  • Brand Strength: Sugar Free #1 (96.2% share, +98 bps); Nycil #1 prickly heat (32.9%); Everyuth #5 facial cleansing (7.9%); Complan up to #4.
  • ESG Edge: S&P score 84 (99th percentile globally).

Growth Prospects (Medium-Term Outlook: 3-5 Years)

  • Double-Digit Revenue Aspiration: Multi-category (VMS, protein, sweeteners), multi-channel (e-com 75-80% for Comfort Click), multi-geo (40+ countries; US/EU entry via RiteBite/Comfort Click).
  • Key Drivers:
    • Comfort Click Synergies: Scale VMS via digital (Amazon/D2C); teens+ growth vs. category; US foray underway.
    • Protein/Nutrition: RiteBite innovations (millet bars, peanut butter); new consumers via healthy snacking.
    • Domestic Expansion: Innovations (Sugar Free Green 18-qtr double-digit); RTD pivot for Glucon-D.
    • EBITDA Target: 17-18% via +gross margins/opex leverage (base business on track).
  • Catalysts: Q3/Q4 seasonal reset; refinancing bridge loan; GST demand stimulus; international ramp-up.

Balanced Outlook: Near-term Q3 muted (small seasonal quarter), but H2 recovery expected with normalcy, innovations, and synergies. Long-term structurally positive (acquisitions unlock global VMS/protein; domestic resilience). Target 10-15% revenue CAGR; margins to 17-18%. Buy/Hold for growth investors; monitor debt refinance/debt metrics. (Valuation: At ~45x FY26E EPS ex-items, premium but justified by growth.)
