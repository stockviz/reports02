Gland Pharma Limited (GLAND) Q2FY26 Analysis

Summary Overview:
Gland Pharma reported solid Q2FY26 results with consolidated revenue up 6% YoY to ₹14,869 Mn and PAT up 12% YoY to ₹1,837 Mn, driven by US growth (10% YoY) and margin expansion (Adj. EBITDA margin +200 bps YoY to 23%). 6M FY26 revenue grew 7% YoY, PAT surged 30%. Base business (Gland) showed resilient 1% YoY revenue growth with superior margins (37% Adj. EBITDA). Cenexi contributed 21% YoY revenue growth but posted EBITDA loss due to planned shutdowns. Strong cash position (₹27 Bn cash, net cash positive) and pipeline momentum support outlook, tempered by regional softness and Cenexi challenges.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • US Market Strength: 53% of 6M revenue; Q2 up 10% YoY to ₹8.3 Bn. 7 new launches (e.g., Daptomycin-RTU, Sumatriptan), 6 ANDA filings/5 approvals (cumulative: 378 filed, 329 approved). RTU bags pipeline ($659 Mn US opportunity); complex injectables (6 launched, 3 pending).
  • Margin Expansion: Consolidated gross margin +400 bps YoY to 63%; base business Adj. EBITDA margin +300 bps to 37%. Efficient cost control, R&D at 5.8% of revenue (up QoQ).
  • Pipeline & Capacity: 15 co-development products (commercialization FY28); GLP-1 capacity ramp-up (40 Mn to 140 Mn units); in-house complex injectables as growth pillar.
  • Financial Health: ₹5.9 Bn operating cash flow (6M); net worth up to ₹95.6 Bn; ROCE/RONW stable ~8-11%; CCC improved to 138 days.
  • Cenexi Momentum: 21% YoY revenue growth; post-shutdown upgrades (GMP renewed to CY26), new lines/vaccines ramping.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Regional Declines: India down 24% YoY; Other Core Markets (Canada/Aus/NZ) -5%; RoW -3%; Europe base business phasing issues (-14% QoQ).
  • Cenexi Losses: EBITDA -€6 Mn (-15% margin) due to planned Fontenay shutdown; ongoing Hérouville recovery.
  • QoQ Pressure: Consolidated revenue -1%, PAT -15%; Cenexi revenue -12% QoQ.
  • Forex/OCI Drag: ₹1 Bn forex loss in Q2 OCI; euro volatility impacts Cenexi reporting.

Growth Prospects

  • High Potential Markets: US (pending 49 ANDAs) and Europe (Cenexi ramp-up) to drive H2 acceleration; target robust growth per management.
  • Product Pipeline: 20 RTU bags filed (14 approved); GLP-1/beyond expansions; 15 partnerships for FY28 launches.
  • CDMO/Expansion: Global capacity builds (lyophilizers, high-speed lines); own products up 19% in RoW H1.
  • Outlook: Management expects “stronger momentum” in H2FY26 via launches/Cenexi recovery; 6M PAT growth (30%) signals full-year upside.
