Analysis of Sonata Software Limited (SONATSOFTW) – Q2 FY’26 (Sep 2025)

Sonata Software, a listed IT services firm (NSE: SONATSOFTW, BSE: 532221), positions itself as an AI-led Modernization Engineering company focusing on Cloud, Data, Dynamics, AI, and partnerships (e.g., Microsoft Inner Circle, AWS). Q2 FY’26 showed resilient profitability (PAT +10% QoQ to ₹120 Cr) amid revenue volatility (consolidated -28.5% QoQ to ₹2,119 Cr, driven by domestic cyclicality), with H1 FY’26 revenue +8.2% YoY. International IT Services (key growth engine) grew steadily in USD/CC terms. The company declared a 2nd interim dividend of ₹1.25/share (record date Nov 21, 2025). Upcoming analyst event: Elara Corporate Day (Dec 8, Bengaluru).

Headwinds (Challenges Pressuring Performance)

  • Revenue Volatility: Sharp QoQ declines (consolidated -28.5%, domestic -38.8%) due to lumpy product sales in domestic business (e.g., GC products steady at ₹69 Cr but services cyclical). International flat QoQ in USD (0.2% CC growth).
  • Client Concentration: Top 10 clients contribute 53-70% of international revenue; Top 20 ~70-77%. Steady but vulnerable to renewals/key account risks.
  • DSO Stretch: International DSO at 68 days (up from 62); domestic improved to 42 days. Could pressure cash flows.
  • Macro/IT Sector Headwinds: “Prevailing industry headwinds” in certain verticals (e.g., TMT resurgence but BFSI/HLS scaling amid caution). Utilization ~84% (stable), but LTM attrition implied stable.
  • Net Debt Position: Gross cash ₹323 Cr but net cash negative ₹280 Cr; borrowings ₹60 Cr (current + non-current). Finance costs ₹14 Cr QoQ.
  • Forex Exposure: Rupee-denominated reporting with USD revenues (43:57 onsite:offshore mix); unrealized Fx gains helped margins but adds volatility.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum Drivers)

  • Profitability Expansion: EBITDA margins (pre-OI/Fx) +280bps QoQ to 8.1% (international +70bps to 17.3%). PAT +10% QoQ (international +10.3%, ROCE 17.8%, RONW 22.6%).
  • International Stability: USD revenue $82M (+0.2% QoQ CC, +3.2% YoY); Rupee +4.3% QoQ. Steady large-client base (40% Top 10 are Fortune 500).
  • Margin Discipline: Cloud/Data revenue mix up to 62% (from 50% FY22); verticals shifting to high-margin HLS/BFSI (33% combined).
  • Dividend Commitment: Quarterly policy (2nd interim ₹1.25/share); signals cash confidence despite net debt.
  • Operational Efficiency: Domestic GC +0.3% QoQ (18% 5-yr CAGR); ROCE/RONW strong (consolidated 22.1%/27.1%).

Growth Prospects (Medium-Term Opportunities)

  • Large Deals Momentum: 1 large win Q2 (Healthcare, Platform Engineering); 24 net-new deals FY22-onwards ($467M TCV). Pipeline: 28 large deals (40% active pipeline, 32% Fortune 500); RMD/TMT/BFSI/HLS focus.
  • AI/Modernization Tailwind: AI win $10.8M (Q2), AI pipeline $293M (+ Fabric $45M). Launches: AgentBridge 4.0 (Agentic AI), Harmoni.AI; partnerships (Wharton AI, IISc). Cloud/Data pipeline 55%.
  • Vertical/Geography Scale: HLS/BFSI from 9% to 33% revenue; US steady (72% geo-mix). GTM aligned with MSFT/AWS/Snowflake (e.g., Fabric, Dynamics).
  • Aspiration: “Top-quartile growth firm” via Sonata.AI evolution (People + Asset model). $1.2B+ revenue, 15.4% 10-yr CAGR; 6,600+ engineers, 15+ nationalities.
  • Domestic Resilience: Steady GC growth; H1 +10.7% YoY.
  • Projections: Steady international (1% CC QoQ trend), AI-led orders ~10% book; net-zero by 2050 ESG push.

Overall Outlook: 10-15% FY’26 revenue growth feasible if large deals convert (4-6Q pursuit) and AI scales; margins 16-18% international EBITDA sustainable.
