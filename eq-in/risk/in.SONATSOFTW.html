Sonata Software Limited (SONATSOFTW) - Q2 FY26 Analysis

Sonata Software reported resilient Q2 FY26 results amid headwinds: Consolidated revenue declined 28.5% QoQ to ₹2,119 Cr (driven by 38.8% drop in Domestic/SITL), but PAT grew 10% QoQ to ₹120 Cr (8.2% YoY). International IT Services (70%+ of revenue) was steady at $82M (flat QoQ USD, +1% CC), with EBITDA margins expanding 70bps to 17.3%. Dividend of ₹1.25/share declared (quarterly policy). Order book at $105M (1.28 book-to-bill). Below is a structured analysis of headwinds, tailwinds, growth prospects, and key risks based on earnings call, financials, investor presentation, and results.

Headwinds (Near-Term Pressure Points)

  • Client-Specific Ramp-Downs: Largest BFSI client (organizational changes/budget cuts) caused major QoQ decline; tail impact into Q3. Largest TMT client saw decision delays post-re-org, offsetting growth elsewhere.
  • Domestic (SITL) Volatility: Revenue down 38.8% QoQ to ₹1,391 Cr due to Microsoft testing direct billing in 10-15 large India accounts (first in APAC); no major renewals at risk in next 6 months, but potential hits post-contract expiry.
  • Vertical/Geography Softness: Retail/Mfg muted (tariffs); TMT “tale of two cities” (big tech weak, mid-tech strong). Non-NA geos softer.
  • Operational: Lower offshore mix (57% vs. peers’ 70-75%); DSO up to 68 days (Intl); wage hikes (90bps margin hit).
  • Impact: Revenue flat YoY; growth lopsided (HLS/BFSI strong at 33% mix, up from 13% in 3 yrs).

Tailwinds (Momentum Drivers)

  • Deal Wins & Pipeline: 1 large Healthcare deal (US provider, multi-year platform modernization); 3 mid-sized AI deals (mortgage, retail playground, CSPs). Pipeline: 55% Cloud/Data, 40% large deals (28 pursuits, 32% Fortune 500).
  • AI Scaling: AI order book at 10% (up from 8% QoQ); AgentBridge deployed internally; pursuits in 100+ clients. Recognitions: CII AI Awards, Everest PEAK Matrix.
  • Margin Levers: Utilization +70bps to 87.3%; planned offshoring; AI productivity gains → 160bps gross EBITDA accretion (net 70bps post-wage). PAT to accrete in H2.
  • Portfolio Resilience: 8 clients >$10M ARR (up from prior); NA at 70%+ revenue; low attrition (14%); 95% workforce AI-trained.
  • Domestic Stability: GC flat QoQ at ₹69 Cr; ROCE/RONW strong (43.8%/42.5%).

Growth Prospects (Medium-Term Outlook)

  • Strategic Focus: AI-led modernization (Platformation.AI); target top-quartile growth via 4 verticals (HLS/BFSI to $250M in 3-5 yrs) and 5 geos (NA/UK/EU/India/Aus).
  • AI/Modernization Tailwinds: Cloud/Data pipeline 55%; MS Fabric partner; Dynamics programmatic growth; AgentBridge 4.0 (cloud-agnostic agentic AI) with IISc/Wharton collab.
  • Partnerships: 30+ yrs MS (Inner Circle, 10 specializations); expanding AWS, Oracle, IBM, Snowflake.
  • SITL Diversification: 3 pillars (MS SMC/SonatonCloud, ISVs, large SI); GC CAGR 18% over 5 yrs.
  • Bookings: 1.28 book-to-bill; TCV $467M net new since FY22 (24 deals). Ex-large client headwinds, broad-based growth.
  • Guidance Signals: H2 PAT accretion; Q3 full upside from Q2 efficiencies; TMT/BFSI stabilization by Q4/Q1FY27.

Overall Outlook: Positive with caution. Q2 resilience (PAT/margin growth despite revenue dip) underscores execution strength; AI/deal momentum positions for H2 inflection (post-headwinds absorption). FY26 growth likely modest (mid-single digit CC), accelerating FY27 via large deals/AI (target: high-teens EBITDA). Risks tied to top clients/MS India, but diversified pipeline/portfolio buffers. Buy/Hold for long-term AI-modernization play; monitor Q3 BFSI/TMT ramps. (Valuation: ~20x FY26 EPS est.; peer avg. 25x).
