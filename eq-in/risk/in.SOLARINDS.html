Risk Type Details

2. Dependence on Government & Regulatory Approvals Much of the defence pipeline depends on Indian MoD clearances, export licenses, and international defence policy — subject to bureaucratic delays.
3. Execution Risk on Large Order Book While ₹17,100cr+ order book is positive, scaling production capacity, talent, and supply chain to meet it on time is challenging.
4. Currency & Commodity Fluctuations International sales exposed to forex volatility; raw material (chemicals, metals) price swings impact margins.
5. Competition in Defence & Explosives Entry of other PSUs and private players (e.g. L&T, Bharat Dynamics, Zen Technologies) could intensify pricing and bidding pressure.
6. Supply Chain & Logistics Global operations require reliable logistics; disruptions (e.g., war zones, port delays) could affect exports.
7. Governance & Director Independence Scrutiny Though new IDs are independent per SEBI norms, investors may monitor board effectiveness, especially given the technical nature of defence projects.
