  • Net loss of ₹535.55 Cr (vs. ₹987 Cr profit YoY) — a swing of over ₹1,500 Cr.

2. Unfavorable Demand or Market Dynamics

  • The document indicates that management now aims to focus on core domestic formulations following the aborted merger with Gujarat Themis Biosyn Ltd (GTBL), suggesting the earlier strategy to consolidate was an attempt to counter softening conditions.

🔚 Conclusion

Themis Medicare Limited is undergoing a challenging transition phase, marked by a sharp revenue decline, losses, and abrupt reversal of a strategic merger. While financial stability is maintained through strong cash reserves and continued market access, the core business appears under pressure.

However, the pivot back to domestic formulations, successful AGM outcomes, and strong affiliate performance suggest that management is attempting course correction.

For investors: This is a speculative, turnaround opportunity. Monitor next quarter closely for: - Signs of revenue stabilization - Cost containment - Resolution of foreign subsidiary write-off - Positive operating cash flow

Until then, exercise caution. The company’s future depends on execution — not strategy.
