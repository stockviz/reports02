Analysis of Rajoo Engineers Limited (RAJOOENG)

Growth Prospects

  • Geographic/Market Penetration: 70+ countries; focus on LatAm/ME/Africa; K-2025 exposure for exports (50%+ revenue).
  • Margin Uplift: High-margin innovations (20%+ EBITDA); automation/energy efficiency; QIP funds for scale (target RoE 25-30%).
  • Sector Tailwinds: India plastics processing @8.5% CAGR; RE capacity to 500 GW by 2030 (sheet demand); flexible packaging growth.
