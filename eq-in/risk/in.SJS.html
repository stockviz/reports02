Overview

S.J.S. Enterprises Limited (BSE: 543387, NSE: SJS, ISIN: INE284S01014) is a leading Indian manufacturer of decorative aesthetics products for the automotive and consumer electronics/appliance industries. The company specializes in self-adhesive labels, 3D badges, overlays, chrome-plated parts, and optical plastics. With manufacturing facilities in Bangalore and Pune, SJS serves over 220 customers across 22+ countries, offering a full-stack in-house production capability.
