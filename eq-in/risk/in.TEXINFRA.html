Analysis of TEXINFRA (Texmaco Infrastructure & Holdings Limited)

It is part of the Adventz Group, listed on NSE (TEXINFRA) and BSE (505400). Financials show investment-heavy balance sheet with profits driven by investment income. Below is a structured analysis of headwinds, tailwinds, growth prospects, and key risks.

  • Shareholder Approval: FY25 AGM (Sep 19, 2025) saw overwhelming promoter support (99-100% in favor); dividend declared/paid; cost/secretarial auditors ratified.
