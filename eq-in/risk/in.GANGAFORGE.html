Summary Analysis for GANGAFORGE (Ganga Forging Limited)

Company Overview: Ganga Forging Ltd. (NSE: GANGAFORGE) is a Gujarat-based manufacturer of closed-die forged products (e.g., auto/engineering components). FY25 full-year revenue was ₹4322 lakhs with modest profit (₹61 lakhs). Promoters hold ~39% stake (52.85M/134.8M shares). Recent Q2/H1 FY26 results (ended Sep 2025) show severe deterioration, with AGM (Sep 27, 2025) resolutions passing unanimously (promoter-driven).
