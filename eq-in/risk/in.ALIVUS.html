Alivus Life Sciences Limited (ALIVUS) Analysis

Overview: Alivus (formerly Glenmark Life Sciences) is an API manufacturer focused on high-value, non-commoditized molecules in chronic therapies (e.g., CVS, CNS, diabetes). Q2FY26 (ended Sep 30, 2025) showed resilient performance amid GPL softness, with strong non-GPL growth, margin expansion, and a debt-free balance sheet (cash ~₹6,526 Mn). Total reactor capacity: 1,424 KL, expanding to ~2,690 KL by FY28E.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust Non-GPL Momentum: +39.7% YoY growth (₹4,443 Mn, 76% of revenue), driven by ROW, LATAM, Japan, Europe, and India ex-GPL. Consistent outperformance since Q3FY25.
  • Margin Resilience: Q2 EBITDA margin 33% (+480 bps YoY), Gross margin 57.7% (+210 bps YoY) despite no PLI benefits; sustained by product mix, new launches, and efficiencies.
  • Strong Balance Sheet & Cash Generation: ₹1,477 Mn free cash flow in Q2; cash equivalents ₹6,526 Mn. ROICE ~29%, debt-free.
  • Diversified Portfolio: Regulated markets 81%, Chronic therapies 69%; 586 DMF/CEP filings; CDMO +15.8% YoY with H2 ramp-up.
  • R&D & Pipeline: 26 HP APIs ($66 Bn TAM), iron complexes ($2.7 Bn TAM); steady filings signal launch readiness.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • GPL Weakness: -23.9% YoY (₹1,437 Mn, 24% of revenue) due to customer inventory rationalization; QoQ de-growth -5.8%.
  • QoQ Revenue Dip: -2.3% (₹5,880 Mn vs. Q1), reflecting GPL softness and inventory dynamics.
  • Operational Incident: Flash fire at Ankleshwar Unit 6 (Sep 30, 2025) led to GPCB penalty (₹50 L interim compensation + ₹10 L BG); no material financial/operational impact claimed, but appeal pending.
  • Capex Intensity: Ongoing expansions (Solapur Phase 1, Dahej/Ankleshwar brownfield) amid rising inventories (₹7,225 Mn, +7% QoQ).

Growth Prospects

  • H2FY26 Outlook: MD confident of stronger performance via non-GPL continuity, GPL recovery, CDMO ramp-up (new projects), and ~30% margins.
  • Capacity & Segments: Total capacity to grow 89% by FY28E (Solapur greenfield key); Generic API +15.3% YoY; CDMO/geos expansion (e.g., regulated markets).
  • Strategic Levers: New launches (NCE-1, patent cliffs), complex platforms (oncology, HP APIs, iron), backward integration, flow chemistry for efficiencies.
  • Medium-Term: H1 revenue +8.6% YoY to ₹11,898 Mn; scalable R&D (3-4% of revenue); global footprint (USFDA/PMDA-approved plants).
  • Valuation Support: PAT +36.5% YoY (₹1,301 Mn); EPS ₹10.60; healthy dividends (₹5/share FY25).

Key Risks

Risk Category Details Mitigation
Regulatory/Compliance Recent GPCB order (appealable); USFDA/PMDA inspections at Ankleshwar/Dahej/Mohol. No material impact claimed; strong track record (recent inspections passed).
Customer Concentration GPL ~24% of revenue; inventory risks from key clients. Diversification (non-GPL 76%); multi-geography exposure.
Operational Fire incident signals plant safety risks; capex execution delays (Solapur Phase 1 ongoing). Expansions calibrated to demand; no ops disruption noted.
Market/External Competition in APIs; forex/raw material volatility; absence of PLI. Product mix focus; efficiencies sustaining margins.
Financial Rising working capital (trade receivables ₹10 Bn, inventories up); H1 capex ₹1,130 Mn. Strong cash buffers; FCF positive.

Summary Verdict: Alivus demonstrates strong fundamentals with tailwinds from non-GPL/diversification outweighing GPL headwinds. Growth prospects are solid (double-digit revenue, 30% margins) via CDMO/capex, but monitor GPL recovery, regulatory appeals, and capex execution. Debt-free status provides buffer; attractive for long-term investors in API/CDMO space. (Data as of Nov 2025 disclosures.)
