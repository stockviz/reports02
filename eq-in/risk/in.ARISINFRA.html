Arisinfra Solutions Limited (ARISINFRA) Analysis

Company Overview: Tech-enabled B2B platform for construction materials supply, contract manufacturing (42% revenue mix), and value-added services (development management, sales/marketing). Serves marquee clients (L&T, KEC, Sobha, etc.). Post-IPO (Jun 2025), strong balance sheet with reduced debt, ₹184 Cr cash, and ₹850 Cr order book (GDV ₹1,400+ Cr). Q2/H1 FY26 showed robust growth amid India’s infra/realty boom.

Tailwinds (Positive Catalysts)

  • Financial Momentum: Q2 revenue ₹242 Cr (+37% YoY, +12% QoQ); H1 ₹458 Cr (+23% YoY). EBITDA ₹23 Cr (9.34% margin, +83 bps YoY); PAT ₹15 Cr (Q2 turnaround from loss). ROCE 17.5%, WC days down to 84 (from 121).
  • Operational Scale: Daily dispatches +30% YoY (792); customers 2.9k (+17% YoY); vendors 2k (+22% YoY); 16 Mn+ MT delivered. Repeat orders 78%.
  • Order Book & Partnerships: ₹850 Cr integrated book (2.5 Mn sq ft). Wins: Merusri Sunscape (₹250 Cr GDV), Arsh Greens (₹200 Cr), AVS (₹40 Cr), Transcon/Amogaya (₹48 Cr revenue + ₹12k Cr GDV unlock).
  • Margin Expansion: Services/contract mfg mix rising (high-margin); asset-light model.
  • Macro Support: Infra/realty formalization; govt push (e.g., housing schemes).

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Cyclical Exposure: Tied to construction/infra cycles; material price volatility or slowdowns (e.g., monsoon delays).
  • Working Capital Intensity: Still 84 days (improving but high vs. peers); trade receivables ₹3.6 Bn.
  • Post-IPO Adjustments: Exceptional IPO costs (₹26 Cr H1); one-time debt prepayments.
  • Fragmented Market: Competition from unorganized players; execution risks in scaling services.
  • Subsidiary Reliance: 6 subs contribute ~40% revenue; consolidation complexities.

Growth Prospects

  • High Visibility: ₹850 Cr book + partnerships (e.g., Transcon/Amogaya: ₹9.6 Cr EBITDA in 5 months; materials ₹30 Cr). Services scaling to 2.5 Mn sq ft GDV ₹1,400 Cr.
  • Integrated Model: Supply (58%) + services (42% mix target); reserved plant capacity expansion.
  • Tech Leverage: Platform enables 790+ daily deliveries; deeper client penetration (top-5 clients 42% revenue).
  • Expansion: Bengaluru/Mumbai focus; CSR/philanthropy (Vishwa Hitay) for brand. FY26 guidance: Continued 20-30% revenue growth via volumes/services.
  • Capital Efficiency: Post-IPO cash for WC/investments; net worth ₹707 Cr.

Key Risks

  • Execution/Market: Project delays (e.g., Ayana95 turnaround success, but scaling risks); realty slowdown.
  • Related Party: Material RPTs with sub Buildmex-Infra (approved); promoter group interests (e.g., foundation).

  • Regulatory/Compliance: SEBI LODR scrutiny (e.g., disclosures, ESOP ratifications passed 99.99% AGM); insider trading windows.
  • Concentration: Top clients 42%; regional focus (South/West India).
  • Other: Forward-looking risks (economy, competition); no UPSI in investor meets.

Overall Outlook: Strong tailwinds from growth/margins outweigh headwinds. High growth potential (20-40% CAGR) via order book/services, but monitor WC/execution. Buy/Hold for infra play; target multiples on 9-10% EBITDA margins. (Ratings neutral; based on docs only.)
