Analysis of Kopran Limited (NSE/BSE: KOPRAN) – Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Kopran Limited is an integrated pharmaceutical company focused on APIs (via subsidiary Kopran Research Laboratories Ltd.) and formulations (oral solids/dry powders). It exports to 50+ countries, with state-of-the-art facilities in Mahad (Maharashtra) and Khopoli (Maharashtra), and a new API/R&D site at Panoli (Gujarat). FY25 full-year revenue was ~Rs. 633 Cr (strong growth from Rs. 360 Cr in FY20), but HY26 (Apr-Sep 2025) showed a slowdown: revenue Rs. 253 Cr (down 14% YoY), EBITDA Rs. 17 Cr (down ~77%), net loss Rs. 2.5 Cr (vs. Rs. 18.5 Cr profit YoY), driven by forex losses (Rs. 58 Cr) and Q2 revenue dip (Rs. 118 Cr). Balance sheet stable (net worth Rs. 501 Cr, debt/equity 0.31x), with strong operating cash flow (Rs. 55 Cr). Recent AGM approved dividend (Rs. 3/share), re-appointments, and auditor.
