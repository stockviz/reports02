Analysis of Sagility Limited (SAGILITY): Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Sagility Limited is a technology-enabled healthcare BPO/services provider primarily serving US payers (88-89% revenue) and providers (11%). FY26 H1 results (ended Sep 30, 2025) show robust performance: H1 revenue ₹31,974M (+25.5% YoY, 21.4% CC; organic +16.9%), Adj. EBITDA ₹8,039M (25.1% margin, +26% YoY), Adj. PAT ₹5,007M (15.7% margin, +62.4% YoY). Q2 standalone highlights: +25.2% YoY revenue growth, healthy new wins ($34M ACV), and AI traction. Balance sheet strong (net debt down, OCF ₹5,585M), with interim dividend (₹0.05/share). Below is a structured summary based on the filings.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Strong Organic Growth & Demand: 16% organic YoY growth in Q2/H1 from existing client expansions (24 clients in Q2) and new logos (9 in H1, incl. 5 in Q2). Payer revenue +24.5% YoY; provider +33.9% (rising mix).
  • Margin Resilience & Efficiency: Adj. EBITDA margins stable at 25-26% despite growth; driven by operational leverage, AI automation (25 use cases across 9 clients), and cost discipline. PAT margins expanded to 18.1% in Q2 (+84% YoY growth).
  • Deal Evolution & Innovation: Shift to outcome-based models (PMPM, gain-share, transform-in-place) with AI/GenAI for 20-40% client savings. Thought leadership (e.g., AHIP webinars, whitepapers) positions as partner for US regulatory changes (e.g., Marketplace Rule, HIRE Act).
  • Cash Generation & Balance Sheet: OCF conversion 67% of EBITDA; debt repayment on track (full by FY27); ROCE improving (14% TTM).
  • Talent & Operations: Headcount +15% YoY to 44k; 34 sites in 5 countries; accolades (Great Place to Work India, Asia CEO Awards).
  • Minimal Macro Impact: Company insulated from US tariffs/H1B (99% US staff citizens/green card holders); offshoring remains cost-effective.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Seasonality & Volatility: Revenue shows quarterly fluctuations (Q4 FY25 strongest at 38% of FY); DSO stable at 78 days but includes unbilled.
  • Talent Pressures: Voluntary attrition 26.3% (annualized, post-90 days); wage inflation in delivery hubs (India/Philippines).
  • Forex & Adjustments: Reported numbers adjusted for forex gains (₹566M H1), earnouts (DCI/BirchAI/BroadPath), and share-based payments; unrealized FX impacts cash conversion (57.6% H1).
  • US Market Pressures: Payer/provider margin compression from tariffs, eligibility rules, and outsourcing taxes (HIRE Act); though Sagility positioned to benefit via tech solutions.
  • Execution in Ramping: New wins steady-state in H2; BroadPath integration (acquired Jan 2025) ongoing.

Growth Prospects

  • High Visibility Pipeline: $34M Q2 ACV (expansions + new SOWs/cross-sells to BroadPath); H2 ramp-up from H1 wins. Client base grew to 82 active groups (+9 gross new); diversification (top-3 share down to 63% from 72% in FY23).
  • Strategic Shifts: Emphasis on AI-led transformation (7 new Q2 use cases); bundled tech-services deals; provider growth (11.5-11.6% mix). Targets: 25%+ revenue/EBITDA growth sustained.
  • Market Tailwinds: US healthcare digitization (eligibility/enrollment automation, cost take-outs); payers/providers outsourcing for efficiency amid profitability squeeze.
  • M&A/Expansion: Recent BroadPath adds mid-market payer exposure; 3 new sites in H1.
  • FY26 Outlook (Implied): Momentum into H2; CC growth 20%+ in Q2 suggests full-year outperformance vs. FY25 (₹55.7B revenue).

Growth prospects bright (H2 ramps, deal evolution), but watch client concentration and attrition. Risks manageable; stock likely supported by beats vs. expectations. Valuation context: Trades at reasonable multiples given growth (check current P/E ~20-25x FY26E implied). Positive for investors focused on healthcare outsourcing.
