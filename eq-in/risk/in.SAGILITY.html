Analysis of Sagility Limited (SAGILITY) – Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Sagility Limited, a technology-enabled healthcare BPO/services provider focused on US payers (88-89% revenue) and providers, reported robust Q2 FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025): Revenue ₹16,585M (+25.2% YoY, 20% CC; organic 16%), Adj. EBITDA ₹4,352M (26.2% margin, +25.6% YoY), Adj. PAT ₹3,010M (18.1% margin, +84% YoY). H1 FY26: Revenue ₹31,974M (+25.5% YoY), Adj. PAT ₹5,007M (+62.4% YoY). Strong cash flows (OCF 67.3% of EBITDA), debt reduction (to be cleared by FY27), interim dividend ₹0.05/share. Client base: 82 active groups (9 new in H1), 34 sites, 44k employees (26% attrition). Top-3 clients: 63% revenue (improving diversification).

Tailwinds

  • Robust Demand & Execution: 16% organic YoY growth from existing client expansions ($34M ACV wins in Q2, incl. 24 expansions/5 new clients/2 BroadPath cross-sells). Evolving deals (PMPM/gain-share models) emphasize AI-led transformation (25 AI use cases deployed), cost take-outs (20-40% savings), and bundled tech/services – aligning with payer/provider margin pressures.
  • Margin Resilience: Adj. EBITDA margins stable at 25-26% despite growth/inflation; operational efficiencies, automation, and forex gains (₹566M in H1) support PAT surge (driven by lower interest/amortization).
  • Financial Strength: High OCF (₹5,585M H1, 67% EBITDA conversion), net debt down (₹6.9B), ROCE improving. Debt fully repayable by FY27, freeing cash for growth/AI investments.
  • Talent & Recognition: Headcount +15% YoY to 44k; attrition stable at 26%; awards (Great Place to Work India, Asia CEO Service Excellence) aid retention.
  • Minimal Macro Impact: US policy changes (tariffs, HIRE Act, H1B) have low exposure (99% US staff citizens/GCs; offshoring cost-effective).

Headwinds

  • Client Concentration: Top-3/5/10 clients = 63%/74%/88% revenue (TTM); heavy reliance on few large payers risks churn/renewal delays.
  • Attrition & Costs: 26% voluntary attrition (stable but elevated); employee costs ~61% of revenue, pressuring margins if wage inflation persists.
  • Seasonality & Collections: Revenue shows Q seasonality (Q2 weaker historically); DSO 78 days (incl. unbilled), with working capital adjustments impacting cash (-₹309M H1).
  • Forex/Non-Op Volatility: Adjustments for earnouts/SARs (₹428M H1) and forex swings distort reported metrics (e.g., unrealized gains boosted PAT).
  • US Healthcare Pressures: Payer/provider margin compression from rules (Marketplace Integrity, tariffs) could slow outsourcing if clients onshore.

Growth Prospects

  • High Visibility Pipeline: H1 new wins/expansions ramp in H2; BroadPath integration/cross-sells adding mid-market payer traction. Target: Sustained 15-20% organic + inorganic via M&A.
  • AI/Tech Transformation: 25+ AI cases live (9 clients); GenAI for engagement/eligibility; thought leadership (e.g., “One Big Beautiful Bill”) positions as partner for regulatory shifts.
  • Diversification: Payer revenue 88% but Provider +34% YoY; client diversification (million-$ clients up: 8 >$20M); geographic expansion (34 sites, 5 countries).
  • Margin/FCF Upside: Post-debt, lower interest/amort (~₹1.4B FY26) boosts PAT; FCF 58% EBITDA H1 supports dividends/buybacks/investments.
  • Market Tailwinds: US$ healthcare outsourcing growth (cost pressures, tech adoption); Sagility’s domain edge (clinicians 3.4k+) in payer/provider ops.

Projected Trajectory: FY26 revenue ~₹65-70B implied (25%+ growth); margins 25%+ Adj. EBITDA; EPS ₹2.5+ adj. Long-term: Debt-free FY27 enables aggressive growth/AI scaling.

Key Risks

  1. Concentration Risk (High): Loss of top clients could erase 60%+ revenue; monitor renewals.
  2. Execution/Integration Risk (Medium): BroadPath (acquired Jan 2025) ramp-up/cross-sells; earnout costs (₹503M FY26).
  3. Talent/Attrition Risk (Medium): 26% rate in competitive BPO space; AI workforce transition needed.
  4. Macro/Policy Risk (Low-Medium): US elections/regulations (e.g., 25% outsourcing tax if HIRE passes); forex (USD/INR volatility).
  5. Competition/Valuation Risk (Medium): Crowded healthcare BPO (e.g., Cognizant, Accenture); stock trades at ~20x FY26 adj. EPS (premium to peers).
  6. Accounting/Non-GAAP Risk (Low): Heavy reliance on “adjusted” metrics (excludes earnouts/SARs/forex); reported PAT lower (₹3,994M H1 vs. adj. ₹5,007M).

Overall Summary

Bull Case (Likely Near-Term): Strong execution + AI tailwinds drive 20%+ CC growth, 25%+ margins, debt-free balance sheet. Target: Top-decile performer in healthcare IT/BPO. Bear Case: Concentration churn or policy shocks cap growth at 10-15%. Rating: Positive (Buy/Hold); growth prospects outweigh risks amid robust FY26 momentum. Investors should track Q3 ramps, client wins, and attrition. Website: www.sagilityhealth.com for updates.
