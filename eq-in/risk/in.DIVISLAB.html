DIVISLAB (Divi’s Laboratories Ltd.) Analysis: Q2/H1 FY26 (Ended Sep 30, 2025)

Divi’s Laboratories reported robust financials with consolidated total income up 17% YoY to ₹2,860 Cr in Q2 (H1: +16% to ₹5,389 Cr), driven by custom synthesis (CS) strength (56% mix) and forex gains (₹63 Cr Q2; ₹102 Cr H1). PAT grew 35% YoY to ₹689 Cr in Q2 (H1: +31% to ₹1,234 Cr). Gross margins stable ~60% amid generics pricing pressure offset by CS and backward integration. Balance sheet strong: ₹3,451 Cr cash, but high inventories (₹3,433 Cr) and receivables (₹2,614 Cr). Capex aggressive (H1: ₹1,550 Cr; FY26 guidance >₹2,000 Cr; CWIP ₹2,030 Cr). US FDA inspection at Unit-I clean (no 483s). Exports ~90% (74% EU+US).

Headwinds

  • Generics Pricing Pressure: Low single-digit decline in Q2; ongoing erosion (e.g., Naproxen, Dextromethorphan) despite stable volumes/customers. Management expects stabilization in “next few quarters” but no firm timeline; contributes ~44-45% mix, dragging overall margins (stable EBITDA ~32-33%).
  • Supply Chain/Macro Risks: US tariffs on China, Russia sanctions impacting raw materials/solvents; monitored via diversified sourcing and inventory buffers.
  • Increasing Competition: More Indian players bidding on CS RFPs; though Divi’s cites edge in track record, EHS, sustainability (SBTi), and capacity.

Tailwinds

  • CS Momentum: Highest-ever Q2 revenue/mix (56%); steady RFPs/site visits from innovators (Phase I-III). 3 major projects (validations underway) backed by long-term contracts; peptides center of excellence scaling (fragments up to octamers/decamers; no generics focus).
  • Backward Integration: Unit-3 (Kakinada) operational for intermediates/starting materials, freeing GMP capacity elsewhere and controlling costs (material consumption ~39.5%).
  • Nutraceuticals Growth: ₹242 Cr Q2 (₹492 Cr H1); leverages carotenoids strengths amid wellness trends.
  • Forex & Exports: ₹102 Cr H1 gain; 90% export-led (stable EU/US demand).
  • Regulatory Cleanliness: Recent US FDA inspection Form 483-free boosts credibility.

Growth Prospects

  • Near-Term (FY26-27): Constant currency revenue growth ~11-12%; CS/peptides ramp-up, nutraceuticals expansion, generics volume stability. New off-patent launches with partners.
  • Medium-Term (1-2 Years): Commercialization of 3 CS projects (post-regulatory approvals/EU-US qualifications); peptides leadership (20+ years expertise in protected amino acids). Flow chemistry/biocatalysis/green tech platforms entering commercial phase.
  • Longer-Term: Capex for capacity/tech upgrades (spread across units); asset turnover improvement as new projects online. Management positive on “exciting” pipeline vs. last decade; industry tailwinds from China+1 diversification (though not quantified for Divi’s).
  • Guidance: FY26 capex up >₹2,000 Cr; product mix drives earnings (no segment-specific outlook due to CDAs).

High-quality balance sheet supports growth, but monitor generics stabilization and capex ROIs. Valuation likely premiums on CDMO purity, but sensitive to project timelines.
