Analysis of Alldigi Tech Limited (ALLDIGI) - Q2 & H1 FY26 Overview

Alldigi Tech Limited (BSE: 532633, NSE: ALLDIGI) reported robust top-line growth in Q2/H1 FY26, driven by its two key segments: Tech & Digital (T&D/HRO/Payroll) (25% of revenue) and Business Process Management (BPM/CXM) (75% of revenue). Revenue grew 12.2% YoY in Q2 (₹147 Cr) and 11.7% YoY in H1 (₹291 Cr), with EBITDA up 17% YoY (margins ~25%). PAT was impacted by prior-year exceptional gains (LLC divestment). International revenue share rose to 64% (BPM: 76%). Strong cash position (₹137 Cr) and OCF (92% EBITDA conversion). Guidance: mid-high teens revenue growth, 100-150 bps EBITDA margin expansion.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Broad-based Growth: T&D +15-17% YoY (Q2/H1), BPM +10-11% YoY; volumes up (47.6L payroll records Q2, +10% YoY; base at 16.2L employees).
  • International Momentum: Share up 2% YoY overall; BPM international +16% YoY (76% mix); ACV strong (T&D H1: ₹18 Cr, 2x YoY; BPM: ₹22 Cr).
  • Operational Excellence: Records/FTE +3-5% QoQ; payroll accuracy/on-time delivery at benchmarks; Everest PEAK Matrix recognition as “Star Performer” (MCP growth/innovation) and “Major Contender” (CXM APAC/US).
  • Platform Investments Yielding: Smart Pay 4 migration complete (India); Smart HR/Buzzily ACV ₹9 Cr (SME focus); efficiencies driving 40% YoY T&D segment EBITDA growth.
  • Financial Strength: OCF +41% YoY Q2 (₹33 Cr); collections +11% YoY H1 (₹304 Cr); DSO stable at 77 days; net cash ₹137 Cr post-dividend.
  • Talent & Culture: Gender diversity +2% YoY (48%); high Glassdoor/AmbitionBox ratings; CSR focus.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Margin Pressure QoQ: EBITDA margins dipped 100 bps QoQ (24.4%) due to sales/leadership hires (professional fees in opex); T&D margins off-peak (40% vs 41-42% guided for Q3).
  • PAT Volatility: H1 PAT -26% YoY (₹32.5 Cr) due to no repeat of LLC divestment gain (₹17 Cr prior H1); depreciation +47% YoY Q2 (₹14.8 Cr) from capex (facilities, infra).
  • Domestic Softness: BPM domestic -1% YoY Q2/-7% H1; flat T&D domestic.
  • Higher Costs: Capex up H1 vs prior (facilities like Chennai/Bangalore); employee costs +10% YoY; inflation partially unrecovered.
  • Segment Imbalance: BPM margins flat YoY (11.6%); reliance on healthcare mining/new logos.

Growth Prospects

  • Guided Trajectory: Mid-high teens FY26 revenue (10 straight quarters of growth); EBITDA margins +1-1.5% YoY via international mix (higher margins), efficiencies (records/FTE +5-6% YoY), cost controls.
  • T&D Expansion: Double payroll volumes in 4-5 years (high-teens CAGR); SME SaaS (Buzzily: ₹2.4 Cr ACV); multi-country payroll leadership.
  • BPM Upside: Healthcare/RCM ramp-up (Philippines, Q3 revenue); AI infusion; funnel expansion post-Everest recognition; international ACV from mining/new logos.
  • Strategic Levers: Platform scale (SP4/SP HR); partnerships (14 sales partners, new UK F&A tie-up); acquisitions (onshore/nearshore for CXM, BFSI/healthcare verticals); MSME measured growth.
  • Market Tailwinds: India payroll leadership; global MCP/CXM demand; Digitide parent synergies.

Key Risks

Execution/Operational Client ramp timings, competition (ADP, Workday, Ramco, Humanica @25-30% growth), MSME pricing pressure/cannibalization. Strong pipeline, direct/partner sales mix, 90%+ utilization, just-in-time capacity.
Financial Rising depreciation/capex eroding PBT 1:1; tax scrutiny (refunds pending 3-4 months); forex volatility. Disciplined ROI on capex; robust cash buffers; hedging initiated.
Macro/External Inflation pass-through lags; US/Philippines policy risks; economic slowdown delaying ACVs. 64% international hedge; high-teens baseline resilient.
Strategic Over-reliance on healthcare (BPM growth driver); acquisition pricing/execution. Diversification (BFSI), measured MSME, clear M&A criteria.

Overall Outlook: Strong fundamentals with 10+ quarters of execution; tailwinds from recognition/investments outweigh headwinds. High-teens growth sustainable if capex stabilizes and international ramps. Stock likely supported by cash generation and guidance. Monitor depreciation/PAT trajectory and Q3 ramps.
