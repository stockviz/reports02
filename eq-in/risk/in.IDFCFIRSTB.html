IDFC FIRST Bank (IDFCFIRSTB) Analysis: Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Robust Liability Franchise: Deposits +23.4% YoY (avg. +24%), CASA +26.8% YoY (50.1% mix, avg. 48.6%). Retail deposits 79% of total (up from 27% at merger), LCR retail deposits 65%. CoF fell 23 bps YoY/19 bps QoQ to 6.23%.

  • Diversification & Scale: Retail/MSME/Rural 80% of loans (5x since merger). Credit cards 4M issued (+spends 36% YoY H1), Wealth AUM ₹55k Cr (+28% YoY). Digital: 26M app registrations, #1 rated app.
  • Capital Strength: CRAR 14.34% (16.82% post-CCPS conversion), CET1 12.27%.
  • Macro Support: RBI repo cuts (100 bps), GST/income tax relief boosting rural/urban consumption.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • MFI Stress: Portfolio -41.6% YoY to ₹7.3k Cr (2.7% of assets), high runoff/slippages (though improving: SMA -88 bps QoQ, collection 99.1%). Utilized ₹75 Cr buffer (₹240 Cr remaining).
  • NIM Pressure: 5.59% (-59 bps YoY/-12 bps QoQ) due to repo pass-through, MFI decline (high-yield), asset mix shift. FD re-pricing lag ~12 months.
  • High Leverage: CD ratio 94% (target mid-80s), borrowings 8% of liabilities (₹25k Cr to repay). SA rates uncut to prioritize LDR.
  • Profitability Dip: PAT -23.8% QoQ (lower trading ₹56 Cr vs ₹495 Cr). H1 PAT ₹815 Cr (-7.5% YoY). C:I 70.9%.
  • Opex Sticky: 59.1% on assets (up due to MFI income drop), though improving.

Growth Prospects

  • Product Ramp-Up: 1M digital loans/month capability (durables, 2W, cards). New: Indigo CC, gold/tractor/KCC loans, rural PSL. Branches to 1,041 (+25 in Q2).
  • MFI Recovery: Stabilize by FY26-end, resume growth (PSL, high-margin). Insurance cover 77%.
  • Fee/Non-Interest Income: +13.2% YoY Q2 (+91% retail). Cards/wealth/digital to drive.
