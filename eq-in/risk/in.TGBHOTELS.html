  • Revenue Momentum: 11.5% YoY H1 growth amid hospitality recovery (weddings, MICE events, tourism boom in Gujarat). Consistent quarterly profits (₹0.21 Cr avg.).

  • Sector Tailwinds: India hospitality demand strong (domestic tourism + weddings). No major disruptions; single-segment focus (hotels/banquets) aligns with event-led recovery.
  • Cost Control: Zero current/deferred tax; stable finance costs.

Headwinds (Negative Factors)

  • Cash Burn: Op. cash flow swung to -₹3.06 Cr (vs. +₹4.07 Cr YoY) due to ₹6 Cr rise in receivables/loans/advances + inventory drawdown. Cash depleted 72% YoY to ₹0.63 Cr.
  • Thin Margins & Cost Pressures: PBT margin ~2%; employee costs +23% YoY (to ₹3.12 Cr), other expenses elevated. High depn (₹26 Cr H1 run-rate) from PPE (₹19 Cr).
  • Leverage: Net debt/equity ~0.15x but leases (₹9.6 Cr) add burden. Negative working capital cycle worsening.
  • Modest Scale: Revenue run-rate ~₹37 Cr FY26E; PAT <₹1 Cr annualizes to low ROE (~0.5%).

Growth Prospects

  • Moderate Upside (10-15% CAGR Near-Term): Leverage H1 momentum for FY26 revenue ~₹40 Cr (+7% YoY). Potential from Ahmedabad’s event hub status (S.G. Road location). Expansion via capex (₹0.12 Cr H1) or RPTs (e.g., related entity synergies).
  • Hospitality Sector: India market to grow 12-15% CAGR to FY30 (ICRA est.); weddings/tourism key drivers. Digital push (e-voting, email updates) aids efficiency.
  • Catalysts: Margin expansion to 4-5% via cost optimization could double PAT. Demat push reduces physical share risks.
  • Valuation: Trades at low multiples (P/E ~20-30x FY25 EPS); growth re-rating possible if cash flow stabilizes.
