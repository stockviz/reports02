Butterfly Gandhimathi Appliances Ltd. (BUTTERFLY) Analysis

Company Overview: Standalone kitchen/small domestic appliances manufacturer (mixer grinders, pressure cookers, gas stoves, wet grinders). Top-3 brand in India, strong South India presence with in-house manufacturing. Q2 FY26 (ended Sep 30, 2025) results show robust growth amid festive demand and premiumization.

Tailwinds (Positive Drivers)

  • Strong Revenue Momentum: Q2 revenue +14% YoY to ₹293 Cr (H1 +9% to ₹480 Cr); driven by double-digit volume growth across core categories, new “Idea First” premium series, and channel gains in e-com/modern trade/regional chains.
  • Margin Expansion: EBITDA +21% YoY to ₹28 Cr (margin 9.5%, +60 bps); gross margins improved via spend optimization. PAT +34% YoY.
  • Macro Support: Festive buoyancy + GST cuts boosted kitchenware (e.g., pressure cookers).
  • Management Strengthening: New SMP hires (manufacturing/demand planning from Jan 2026; marketing from Dec 2025) with proven track records in ops, cost reduction, and brand growth.
  • Healthy Balance Sheet: Equity up 7% to ₹3,488 Cr; low finance costs (₹0.45 Cr in Q2); investments in mutual funds for liquidity.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • Working Capital Strain: Trade receivables +85% to ₹1,513 Cr; inventories +17% to ₹1,287 Cr – led to negative working capital cash flow drag (H1 op. cash ₹116 Cr vs. -₹241 Cr YoY).
  • Cost Pressures: Employee expenses +14% YoY; other expenses elevated amid expansion.
  • Cash Flow Volatility: H1 investing cash outflow (₹83 Cr) from capex (₹56 Cr) and mutual fund churn; overall cash dip to ₹86 Cr.

Growth Prospects

  • High: 15-20%+ revenue CAGR potential via premiumization (e.g., Idea First traction), pan-India expansion from South stronghold, and e-com/modern trade scaling. Backward integration + new mgmt. hires support margins (target 10%+ EBITDA). FY26 guidance implied strong (H1 already 55% of FY25 PAT). Festive tailwinds extend into H2; no subsidiaries limits but focuses ops.
  • Catalysts: Brand refresh reception; further product launches; potential North/West penetration.

Key Risks

Risk Category Details Mitigation/Impact
Working Capital High receivables/inventories risk bad debts (provision +16% YoY); cash conversion cycle extension. Provisions in place; payables up 70%. Medium impact.

Overall Summary: Bullish outlook with strong Q2 execution (revenue/EBITDA beats), margin leverage, and strategic hires offsetting working capital headwinds. Growth prospects robust (double-digit FY26 trajectory); monitor receivables for liquidity. Target upside if festive H2 sustains; key watch: Q3 results for WC trends. No material red flags from filings.
