Aurobindo Pharma Limited (AUROPHARMA) Analysis

Based on Q2FY26 results (ended Sep 30, 2025), investor presentation, financials, FDA update, and other disclosures.

Tailwinds (Positive Momentum)

  • Strong Revenue Growth: Consolidated revenue +6.3% YoY to ₹8,286 Cr; formulations +10.3% YoY (US +3.1%, Europe +17.8%, Growth Markets +8.7%, ARV +68.7%). US formulations (ex-Puerto Rico) stable at $417 Mn (+2% QoQ).
  • Profitability Resilience: EBITDA +7.1% YoY to ₹1,678 Cr (20.3% margin, +16 bps YoY); gross margins expanded to 59.7% (+88 bps YoY) via efficiencies.
  • Robust Pipeline Execution: 13 US ANDA filings, 7 approvals, 6 launches in Q2; cumulative 876 ANDAs filed (711 final approvals). Global filings: 5,782 formulations, 4,369 APIs.
  • Biosimilars Acceleration: EU/UK approvals for trastuzumab, bevacizumab, pegfilgrastim, filgrastim; Phase 3 successes (e.g., BP16 Prolia biosimilar ready for filing); 3+ immunology/oncology filings by FY27.
  • Financial Strength: Net cash $170 Mn (up from prior); free cash flow $57 Mn pre-dividend; R&D spend ₹414 Cr (5% of sales) focused on specialties/biosimilars.
  • Market Expansion: Capacity builds for biosimilars; new subsidiaries (e.g., CuraTeQ Malta, Malaysia entities) for testing/supply chain self-sufficiency.

Headwinds (Challenges)

  • API Segment Weakness: -16.9% YoY to ₹961 Cr (11.6% of revenue); beta-lactam -20.1% due to market conditions.
  • US Base Business Pressure: Growth tempered by lower transient sales (e.g., gRevlimid); ex-gRevlimid +6% QoQ but overall +2% QoQ.
  • Forex/Operational Costs: Rupee depreciation (avg. ₹87.29/$); depreciation +12.3% YoY; overheads +8.2% YoY.
  • Regional Volatility: ARV QoQ dip (-8.3%); domestic formulations minor (₹81 Cr, ~1% revenue).

Growth Prospects

  • Short-Term (FY26): US generics ramp-up (172 pending final approvals); Europe/Growth Markets momentum (target high-teens growth); ARV sustained double-digit gains.
  • Medium-Term (FY27+): Biosimilars commercialization (pegfilgrastim, filgrastim launches; Avastin/Xolair readouts CY26); post-2028 pipeline (immunology/oncology); capacity expansions in filling/bulk.
  • Strategic Levers: 30%+ formulations mix; partnerships for market access; $106 Mn capex for capabilities/new markets. Guidance-aligned for steady growth (mid-single digits revenue, stable margins).
  • Overall Outlook: Biosimilars to drive shift from commoditized generics; global filings support 10-15% formulations CAGR.
