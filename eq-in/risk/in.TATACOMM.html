Analysis of Tata Communications (TATACOMM) - Q2 FY26 (Ended Sep 30, 2025)

Overall Summary: TATACOMM delivered robust revenue growth (6.5% YoY consolidated to ₹6,100 Cr), driven by Data Services (7.3% YoY) and Digital portfolio (14.9% YoY), with sequential Data EBITDA margin expansion (140 bps QoQ to 18.6%). However, PAT declined 27% YoY to ₹183 Cr due to discontinued operations losses and exceptional items. Balance sheet shows rising short-term debt (₹6,205 Cr current borrowings, up from ₹3,883 Cr FY25), but operational cash flow remains positive (₹954 Cr H1). Strategic tailwinds from digital bets (AI, Cloud, IoT) and partnerships (Real Madrid, BSNL, AWS) offset regulatory headwinds (DoT AGR demands). Growth outlook positive on digital transformation, tempered by debt and litigation risks.
