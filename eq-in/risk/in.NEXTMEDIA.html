Analysis of Next Mediaworks Limited (NEXTMEDIA)

Next Mediaworks Limited, a media company (CIN: L22100MH1981PLC024052), appears operationally dormant with zero revenue from operations in recent periods (Q2/H1 FY26 ended Sep 30, 2025, and prior comparatives). It relies on holding company support amid persistent losses driven by finance costs on inter-corporate borrowings. FY25 showed an exceptional gain (₹882 Cr) boosting PAT to profit, but core operations remain unprofitable. Net worth is deeply negative (-₹3,139 Cr as of Sep 30, 2025), with investments declining due to fair value losses. AGM resolutions (Sep 24, 2025) passed overwhelmingly, indicating promoter control (75%+ voting power).

Headwinds (Negative Factors)

  • Zero Revenue & Negative EBITDA: No operational income (₹0 revenue for 6+ periods); EBITDA losses of ₹11 Cr (H1 FY26) vs. ₹52 Cr (FY25), signaling stalled media/radio business (no reportable segments per Ind AS 108).
  • Persistent Losses & Negative Net Worth: PAT loss ₹232 Cr (H1 FY26); total equity -₹3,139 Cr (eroded by borrowings & OCI losses of ₹415 Cr in Q2 from equity investment FV decline). Other equity at -₹9,828 Cr.
  • High Finance Costs: ₹221 Cr (H1 FY26) on ₹3,684 Cr non-current borrowings (due Aug 2027), consuming all cash flows.
  • Cash Burn: Negative operating cash flow (₹33 Cr H1 FY26); cash equivalents dwindled to ₹3 Cr from ₹9 Cr.

Tailwinds (Positive Factors)

  • Holding Company Support: Letter of support for operational expenses; deemed capital contribution (₹7 Cr in H1 FY26). No bank/FI debt.
  • Liquidity Match: Current assets (₹6 Cr) ≈ current liabilities (₹6 Cr); borrowings long-term (no immediate maturities).
  • Promoter Strength: 75% promoter holding; 100% voting in favor at AGM. Unmodified limited review by S.R. Batliboi.
  • Going Concern Basis: Management deems appropriate due to support & liquidity.

Growth Prospects

  • Low Visibility: No revenue pipeline, operations, or revival plans disclosed. Historical exceptional gain (₹882 Cr FY25) suggests past asset monetization, but none indicated now.
  • Potential Upside: Investments (₹396 Cr) could recover (classified FVOCI); holding company infusion or business restart (e.g., radio/media revival) possible but speculative. No consolidated financials required post-Feb 2025.
  • Outlook: Neutral-negative; dormant state limits growth unless strategic pivot (e.g., asset sale/debt restructuring). EPS remains negative/not annualized (-₹0.35 H1 FY26).

Key Risks

  1. Liquidity & Going Concern Risk (High): Heavy reliance on holding company; withdrawal could trigger default/cash crunch.
  2. Debt Repayment Risk (Medium): ₹3,684 Cr due 2027; interest (₹110 Cr/quarter) unsustainable without revenue.
  3. Investment Volatility (High): ₹416 Cr FV decline (H1 FY26); further drops could deepen losses.
  4. Regulatory/Delisting Risk (Medium): Negative net worth may attract SEBI/BSE/NSE scrutiny; low liquidity/public float (low non-promoter voting).
  5. Operational Stagnation (High): No revenue revival path; employee costs persist despite dormancy.
  6. Market/Execution Risk: Thin trading volume; promoter interests aligned but no growth catalysts.

Summary

NEXTMEDIA faces severe headwinds from zero revenue, losses, and negative equity, offset minimally by holding company support (key tailwind). Growth prospects are weak (dormant ops, no catalysts), with high risks around liquidity/debt. Stock suitable only for speculative/promoter-bet plays; avoid for fundamentals. Monitor holding co. actions & debt maturity. (Data as of Nov 3, 2025 filings.)
