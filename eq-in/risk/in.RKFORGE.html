Analysis of Ramkrishna Forgings Limited (RKFORGE) – Headwinds, Tailwinds, Growth Prospects, and Key Risks

Overview: RKFORGE, a leading forging and casting manufacturer (primarily auto, railways, non-auto), reported weak Q2/H1 FY26 results amid global headwinds (revenue down 11% QoQ/4% YoY H1 consolidated; EBITDA margin 13.5%; PAT loss ₹9.5 Cr due to ~₹25 Cr one-offs). Capacity at ~400kt (forging+casting), utilization 60%. Debt elevated at ~₹2,550 Cr post-capex. Management guides double-digit FY26 growth, H2 recovery (18-20% volume), 17-18% margins. Promoter funding via warrants (~₹250 Cr total). Diversification into railways/Europe offsets export weakness.

Headwinds (Challenges Pressuring Performance)

  • Export Weakness (56% revenue mix): US tariffs (25-50%) disrupted demand; reciprocal tariffs raised end-prices, slowing OEM orders. NA shipments cut to avoid inventory buildup; Q2 exports down 32% YoY. Mexico ops loss ₹3 Cr.
  • Margin Pressure: EBITDA down 17.5% QoQ (13.5% vs. 14.6%); forex loss ₹6.8 Cr (imports), tariff hit ₹10.8 Cr. Utilization dipped to 60% (69% Q1). Input costs/currency volatility persist.
  • High Debt & Capex Drag: Debt up ~₹600 Cr H1 (capex ₹485 Cr); interest costs rising. FY26 capex ~₹600 Cr (H2 <₹100 Cr).
  • One-offs & Restatements: Inventory discrepancies (WIP overstatement) led to restated FY25 priors; forex/JV losses masked ops strength.
  • Macro: Geopolitical tensions, auto inventory rationalization globally.

Tailwinds (Supportive Factors)

  • Domestic Resilience (44% mix, up 8% YoY H1): GST cuts revived auto sentiment; railways traction (bogies/castings ₹496 Cr orders). Domestic growth offset exports.
  • Order Inflows: Q2 wins ₹1,116 Cr (4-yr life; 69% auto intl., 27% railways). H1 Europe CV ₹927 Cr, NA PV ₹307 Cr. Bulk railway bogie dispatches started.
  • Promoter Commitment: ₹51 Cr upfront from prior warrants; new ₹200 Cr warrants to Chaitanya Jalan (₹50 Cr upfront expected). Debt reduction guided ₹500-600 Cr by Mar’26.
  • Capex Completion: FY26 capex done; new lines (8kt/3kt/1.6kt presses, cold forging 40% util, aluminum ramping, 45kt casting near-sold). Rail JV (51% stake) trials Jan’26.
  • Diversification: Railways (assemblies, castings, JV wheels); Europe/NA new customers; Mexico machining orders ₹200 Cr (bulk FY27).

Growth Prospects (Medium-Term Outlook)

  • FY26: Double-digit revenue (H2 18-20% volume); EBITDA 15-16%+; “worst behind us.” Q3 traction in US/Europe post-tariff clarity.
  • FY27+: 85% util → ₹6,200-6,500 Cr peak revenue (blended forging/casting). Railways uplift (40k JV wheels ~₹500 Cr RKFORGE share; full 228k → ₹1,600-1,700 Cr by FY28). Order book (₹6-7k Cr recent) → ~₹1,000 Cr annual revenue FY28.
  • Margins: 17-18% blended (forging>casting); aspire 20%+ via value-add (assemblies, EV aluminum).
  • Catalysts: GST auto boom; rail passenger boom; Europe/NA ramps; Mexico FY27 revenue; mergers (Multitech/MAL into RKCSL Q3).
  • Capex: Negligible FY27; focus utilization.
